Peter Kurrild-Klitgaard & Gert T. Svendsen (2003), “Rational Bandits: Plunder, Public Goods, and the Vikings,” Public Choice.库里尔德-克利特高、斯文森（2003）《理性盗匪：掠夺、公共物品与维京人》，Public Choice。

Case illustrationDeductive theory

Dependent variable (DV)

The main dependent variable is the change in Viking behavioral patterns, from roving plunder to settlement, taxation, and provision of public goods.

Specifically, the phenomena the authors focus on include: changes in the frequency and intensity of Viking raiding activities, the establishment of Viking settlements, the shift from plunder to collection of “Danegeld,” and various public goods provided by Viking rulers (such as security protection, legal systems, trade promotion, etc.) (pp. 260-261).

主要因变量: 维京人行为模式的变化，从流动性掠夺向定居、征税和提供公共物品的转变

具体而言，作者关注的现象包括：维京人掠夺活动的频率和强度变化、维京人定居点的建立、由掠夺向收取“丹麦金”（Danegeld /ˈdeɪnˌɡeld/）的转变、以及维京统治者提供的各种公共物品（如安全保护、法律制度、贸易促进等）（第260-261页）。

Independent variable (IV)

The main independent variable is the profit rate of plundering activities, and the over-exploitation problem that plundering faces as a “common-pool resource.”

The authors borrow the analytical framework of fishery economics, treating the wealth available for plunder as an open-access common resource. When more and more Vikings participate in plundering, “over-plundering” occurs, causing per capita returns to decline until profits reach zero (pp. 257-258). Changes in this variable drive the transformation of Viking behavioral patterns.

主要自变量: 掠夺活动的利润率，以及掠夺作为“公共池塘资源”（common-pool resource）所面临的过度开发问题

作者借用渔业经济学的分析框架，将可供掠夺的财富视为一种开放获取的公共资源。当越来越多的维京人参与掠夺时，就会出现“过度掠夺”（over-plundering），导致人均收益下降直至利润归零（第257-258页）。这一变量的变化驱动了维京人行为模式的转变。

Stateness

This article’s conceptualization of the “state” is deeply influenced by Olson’s theory, viewing the state as the institutionalized form of the “stationary bandit.”

According to this perspective, the essential characteristics of the state are monopolization of violence, “stealing” through taxation, and provision of certain public goods to stimulate economic growth (pp. 259-260). The authors cite McGuire and Olson (1996), noting that such governments arise “not because of voluntary social contracts, but because of rational self-interest among those who can impose organization” (p. 259).

In terms of measurement, the authors primarily identify “stationary bandit”-style state building through the following indicators: First, establishment of territorial control (such as the establishment of Viking kingdoms in Dublin, York, etc.); second, the shift from one-time plunder to regular taxation (such as the evolution of Danegeld); third, provision of public goods (such as security protection, legal systems, trade facilities) (pp. 261, 265-268).

本文对“国家”的概念化深受Olson理论的影响，将国家视为“固定性盗匪”的制度化形式。

根据这一视角，国家的本质特征是对暴力的垄断、通过税收进行“窃取”、以及为刺激经济增长而提供某些公共物品（第259-260页）。作者引用McGuire和Olson（1996）的观点指出，这类政府的出现“不是因为自愿的社会契约，而是因为那些能够强加组织的人的理性自利”（第259页）。

在测量方面，作者主要通过以下指标来识别“固定性盗匪”式的国家建设：第一，领土控制的建立（如维京王国在都柏林Dublin /ˈdʌblɪn/、约克York /jɔːrk/等地的建立）；第二，从一次性掠夺向定期税收的转变（如丹麦金的演变）；第三，公共物品的提供（如安全保护、法律制度、贸易设施）（第261页、第265-268页）。

Core argument

The authors’ core argument is that the Viking transition from roving plunder to settlement and state-building was a rational economic response to declining plunder profits.

The logical chain of the argument is as follows: First, in a state of anarchy, individuals receive the full benefits of plundering but bear only a small share of the costs, so plundering is rational at the individual level (p. 256). However, since access to plundering opportunities is open, more and more plunderers will enter, leading to “common-pool resource”-style over-exploitation. Eventually, over-plundering will drive profits toward zero (pp. 257-258).

When profits from roving plunder disappear, individuals with a comparative advantage in violence (i.e., successful Viking chieftains) are motivated to change their strategy: by settling and excluding other plunderers, they can prevent further over-plundering and maximize their own future profits (pp. 258-259). Once settled, these “stationary bandits” find that rather than plundering everything at once, “stealing” regularly through taxation is more profitable, because this preserves the source of future revenues (p. 260).

Furthermore, as the “owner” of all wealth within the territory, the stationary bandit has an incentive to stimulate economic growth by providing public goods, thereby increasing the taxable base (p. 260). The authors use an optimal tax rate of 1/3 as an example: if the tax rate exceeds this point, the loss in social income will be greater than the increase in the bandit’s tax revenue (p. 260).

作者的核心论点是：维京人从流动性掠夺向定居和国家建设的转变，是对掠夺利润下降的理性经济反应。

论点的逻辑链条如下：首先，在无政府状态下，个人从掠夺中获得全部收益但只承担成本的一小部分，因此掠夺在个人层面是理性的（第256页）。然而，由于获取掠夺机会是开放的，越来越多的掠夺者会进入，导致“公共池塘资源”式的过度开发。最终，过度掠夺会使利润趋近于零（第257-258页）。

当流动性掠夺的利润消失时，具有暴力比较优势的个体（即成功的维京首领）有动机转变策略：通过定居并排除其他掠夺者，他们可以阻止进一步的过度掠夺并最大化自己的未来利润（第258-259页）。一旦定居，这些“固定性盗匪”会发现，与其一次性掠夺殆尽，不如通过税收定期“窃取”更为有利，因为这保留了未来收益的来源（第260页）。

更进一步，固定性盗匪作为领土内全部财富的“所有者”，有动机通过提供公共物品来刺激经济增长，从而增加可征税的基础（第260页）。作者以1/3的最优税率为例说明：如果税率超过这一点，社会收入的损失将大于盗匪税收的增加（第260页）。

Mechanisms

The authors propose three interrelated mechanisms:

Mechanism One: The Logic of Common-Pool Resource Over-Exploitation

The authors borrow the fishery economics model to analyze plundering behavior (p. 257). Just as fishermen will overfish until profits are zero, Vikings will also over-plunder. When individual Vikings decide whether to plunder, they assume that the impact of their own behavior on the total amount of plundering is negligible, so they do not consider how their behavior affects the overall sustainability of the resource (p. 258). Formally, individual i’s utility is ui = piB(P), where pi represents whether to participate in plundering, P represents total plundering, and B’<0 means that the more plundering, the lower the return. Individual maximization leads to P* (actual plundering) being greater than P** (optimal plundering) (p. 258).

Mechanism Two: Monopolization of Violence and Exclusion

When plundering profits approach zero, the most efficient plunderers are motivated to transform into “stationary bandits.” By monopolizing violence and excluding other plunderers, they can control the amount of plundering at the optimal level, thereby restoring positive profits (p. 259). Figure 2 shows this logic: the marginal benefit of reducing roving bandits is diminishing, while the marginal cost is increasing; the optimal reduction R* is the point where marginal benefit equals marginal cost. The net benefit from this reduction (area A in the figure) is the incentive for settlement (p. 259).

Mechanism Three: Time Horizon and Public Goods Provision

Once stable rule is established, the stationary bandit’s optimal strategy depends on its time horizon (p. 260). If long-term rule is expected, one-time confiscation of all property is unwise because it would destroy the source of future tax revenue. Instead, setting a moderate tax rate preserves production incentives. Citing the analysis of Frohlich and Oppenheimer /ˈfrəʊlɪk ænd ˈɒpənhaɪmər/ (1974), the authors note that “the method of stealing, taxation or confiscation, depends on the time horizon” (p. 260).

The same logic applies to the provision of public goods. If the stationary bandit’s tax rate is 1/3, then he has an incentive to provide public goods until “the last $1 spent would result in a $3 rise in income” (p. 260). Therefore, the stationary bandit will invest in building bridges, establishing a legal system, reducing crime, and ensuring the health of citizens (p. 260). This explains why Viking rulers often began to promote trade, establish legal systems, and build fortifications after settling.

作者提出了三个相互关联的机制：

机制一：公共池塘资源的过度开发逻辑

作者借用渔业经济学模型来分析掠夺行为（第257页）。正如渔民会过度捕捞直至利润为零，维京人也会过度掠夺。个体维京人在决定是否掠夺时，假设自己的行为对总体掠夺量的影响可以忽略不计，因此不会考虑其行为如何影响整体资源的可持续性（第258页）。形式化地表示，个体i的效用为ui = piB(P)，其中pi表示是否参与掠夺，P表示总掠夺量，B’<0表示掠夺越多收益越低。个体最大化导致P*（实际掠夺量）大于P**（最优掠夺量）（第258页）。

机制二：暴力垄断与排他性

当掠夺利润趋于零时，最有效的掠夺者有动机转变为“固定性盗匪”。通过垄断暴力并排除其他掠夺者，他们可以将掠夺量控制在最优水平，从而恢复正利润（第259页）。图2展示了这一逻辑：减少流动性盗匪的边际收益递减，而边际成本递增；最优减少量R*是边际收益等于边际成本的点。这一减少带来的净收益（图中A区域）就是定居的激励（第259页）。

机制三：时间视野与公共物品提供

一旦建立稳定的统治，固定性盗匪的最优策略取决于其时间视野（第260页）。如果预期长期执政，一次性没收所有财产是不明智的，因为这会摧毁未来税收的来源。相反，设定一个适度的税率可以保留生产激励。引用Frohlich和Oppenheimer（1974）的分析，作者指出“窃取方式，税收还是没收，取决于时间视野”（第260页）。

同样的逻辑适用于公共物品的提供。如果固定性盗匪的税率是1/3，那么他有动机提供公共物品直到“花费的最后1美元能带来3美元的收入增长”（第260页）。因此，固定性盗匪会投资于建设桥梁、建立法律体系、减少犯罪、保障公民健康等（第260页）。这解释了为何维京统治者往往在定居后开始促进贸易、建立法律制度、修建防御工事等。

Data & methods

Data

The authors acknowledge that data meeting “the strictest, modern criteria” is extremely hard to find (p. 262). Therefore, the article primarily relies on secondary sources from historical literature and archaeology to provide “indicia” of empirical support.

Main Data Sources:

Annals of Ulster: The authors use this chronicle’s records of the number of Viking raids on Irish churches between 800-910 (Table 1, p. 263). The data shows that the number of raids peaked in the 830s (25), then declined sharply after the Vikings began to settle.

Danegeld Records: Table 2 summarizes the amounts of Danegeld paid by the Franks and the English in the 9th-11th centuries (p. 264). The Frankish data shows higher payments initially (7,000 pounds of silver in 845), then declining; the English data shows continuously rising payments in the 10th-11th centuries (from 22,000 pounds in 991 to 48,000 pounds in 1012), ultimately ending in conquest.

Historical Case Studies: The authors cite numerous specific historical events and figures, including:

The case of Danish King Godfred (†810) building the Dannevirke border wall and establishing the trading city of Hedeby (p. 268)

The case of Viking chieftain Weland (†863) as a “mercenary” (p. 265)

The case of Rollo (†932) receiving the Normandy territory as a fief (p. 266)

The legal system building by Canute the Great (988-1035) (p. 267)

Geographic Coverage: Primarily covers the Western European Viking activity areas, including England, Ireland, the Frankish realm, Normandy, and the Scandinavian homeland. The article explicitly excludes the activities of Swedish Vikings in Eastern Europe (p. 270, note 7).

Time Period: The “Viking Age” of approximately 780-1080, with a focus on the 9th-11th centuries (p. 261).

作者坦承，符合“最严格的现代标准”的数据极难获取（第262页）。因此，本文主要依赖历史文献和考古学的二手资料来提供“迹象性”（indicia）的经验支持。

主要数据来源:

《厄尔斯特编年史》（Annals of Ulster /ˈʌlstər/）：作者利用该编年史记录的800-910年间维京人袭击爱尔兰教堂的次数（表1，第263页）。数据显示，袭击次数在830年代达到峰值（25次），随后在维京人开始定居后急剧下降。

丹麦金记录：表2汇总了9-11世纪法兰克人（Franks /fræŋks/）和英格兰人（English）支付的丹麦金数额（第264页）。法兰克数据显示初期支付较高（845年7000磅白银），之后下降；英格兰数据则显示10-11世纪的支付额不断攀升（从991年的22000磅到1012年的48000磅），最终以征服告终。

历史案例研究：作者引用了大量具体历史事件和人物，包括：

丹麦国王戈德弗雷德（Godfred /ˈɡɒdfred/，†810）建设丹麦边墙（Dannevirke /ˈdænəvɜːrkə/）和建立海泽比（Hedeby /ˈhedəbiː/）贸易城市的案例（第268页）

维京首领韦兰（Weland /ˈwiːlənd/，†863）作为“雇佣兵”的案例（第265页）

罗洛（Rollo /ˈrɒloʊ/，†932）获得诺曼底（Normandy /ˈnɔːrməndɪ/）领地的案例（第266页）

克努特大帝（Canute the Great /kəˈnjuːt/，988-1035）的法律制度建设（第267页）

地理覆盖范围: 主要涵盖西欧维京活动区域，包括英格兰（England /ˈɪŋɡlənd/）、爱尔兰（Ireland /ˈaɪərlənd/）、法兰克王国（Frankish realm）、诺曼底，以及斯堪的纳维亚本土（Scandinavia /ˌskændɪˈneɪviə/）。文章明确排除了瑞典维京人在东欧的活动（第270页注7）。

时间跨度: 约780-1080年的“维京时代”（Viking Age），重点关注9-11世纪（第261页）。

Methods

The article employs what the authors call a “theoretical history” method (p. 256), i.e., using the logical framework of public choice theory to organize and interpret historical evidence.

Methodological Characteristics: The authors first start from Olson’s “roving bandit-stationary bandit” model, combine it with common-pool resource theory, and deduce three theoretical predictions about Viking behavior (p. 260):

When plundering opportunities are open, Vikings will over-plunder until profits are zero

To maximize profits, roving Vikings are motivated to transform into stationary bandits

Stationary Vikings will maximize tax revenue by providing public goods

Then, the authors use historical cases and anecdotal evidence to illustrate the consistency of these predictions with historical facts (pp. 262-268). This is a “process-tracing” style qualitative method aimed at demonstrating the operation of theoretical mechanisms in history.

Key Assumptions:

Rational behavior assumption: Vikings are assumed to be rational actors pursuing wealth maximization, who adjust their strategies according to changes in constraints. The authors explicitly state that this assumption contrasts with “cultural” or “values” explanations (p. 269).

Complete information assumption: The theory requires actors to be able to accurately assess changes in the benefits and costs of plundering to make optimal decisions.

Homogeneity assumption: The model to some extent assumes that Vikings are homogeneous actors, ignoring differences among them in resources, risk preferences, social status, etc.

本文采用的是作者所称的“理论史学”（theoretical history）方法（第256页），即运用公共选择理论的逻辑框架来组织和解释历史证据。

方法特征: 作者首先从Olson的“流动性盗匪-固定性盗匪”模型出发，结合公共池塘资源理论，演绎出关于维京行为的三个理论预测（第260页）：

当掠夺机会开放时，维京人会过度掠夺直至利润为零

为最大化利润，流动性维京人有动机转变为固定性盗匪

固定性维京人会通过提供公共物品来最大化税收

然后，作者以历史案例和轶事证据来说明这些预测与史实的一致性（第262-268页）。这是一种“过程追踪”（process-tracing）式的定性方法，旨在展示理论机制在历史中的运作。

关键假设:

理性行为假设: 维京人被假定为追求财富最大化的理性行为者，会根据约束条件的变化调整策略。作者明确表示，这一假设与“文化”或“价值观”解释形成对照（第269页）。

完全信息假设: 理论要求行为者能够准确评估掠夺的收益和成本变化，从而做出最优决策。

同质性假设: 模型在某种程度上假定维京人是同质的行为者，忽视了他们之间在资源、风险偏好、社会地位等方面的差异。

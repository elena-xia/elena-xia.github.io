Melissa Dell, Nathan Lane & Pablo Querubín (2018), “The Historical State, Local Collective Action, and Economic Development in Vietnam,” Econometrica 86(6).戴尔、莱恩、克鲁宾（2018）《历史国家、地方集体行动与越南的经济发展》，Econometrica。

Dependent variable (DV)

The main dependent variable in this study is long-run economic development and prosperity. The authors operationalize this concept through multiple measures across different time periods. For contemporary outcomes, they primarily use household consumption expenditure from the Vietnam Household Living Standards Surveys (VHLSS) collected between 2002 and 2012. Specifically, they construct a measure of equivalent household consumption by subtracting transfers received from total consumption, then adjusting for household composition where children aged 0-4 are counted as 0.4 adults and children aged 5-14 as 0.5 adults, following Deaton’s (1997) methodology (p. 2097). The study finds that household consumption on the Dai Viet [daɪ vjɛt] side of the boundary is approximately one-third higher than on the Khmer [kmɛr] side (p. 2084).

Beyond consumption, the authors examine human capital measured as years of schooling for individuals over age 25, finding that residents in historically Dai Viet areas have approximately 0.95 additional years of education (p. 2102). The study also examines a rich set of intermediate outcomes that plausibly mediate the relationship between historical institutions and economic development, including civil society participation, local government functioning, public goods provision (healthcare, education, law enforcement), and various indicators of local collective action capacity (pp. 2106-2111).

For historical periods, the authors use telegraph and road density from colonial-era maps (1878-1926), household income data from the Pacification Attitudes and Analysis Survey (1970-1972), and economic indicators from the Hamlet Evaluation System (1969-1973), including availability of non-rice foodstuffs, manufactured goods, and household access to vehicles (pp. 2103-2105).

本研究的主要因变量是长期经济发展与繁荣程度。作者通过不同时期的多种测量指标来操作化这一概念。对于当代结果，他们主要使用2002年至2012年间越南家庭生活水平调查（VHLSS）中的家庭消费支出数据。具体而言，他们通过从总消费中减去转移收入来构建等效家庭消费指标，并按照Deaton（1997）的方法调整家庭构成：0-4岁儿童计为0.4个成年人，5-14岁儿童计为0.5个成年人（第2097页）。研究发现，大越[daɪ vjɛt]一侧边界的家庭消费比高棉[kmɛr]一侧高出约三分之一（第2084页）。

除消费外，作者还考察了以受教育年限衡量的人力资本，针对25岁以上个体，发现历史上属于大越地区的居民平均多受约0.95年的教育（第2102页）。研究还检验了一系列丰富的中介变量，这些变量可能介导历史制度与经济发展之间的关系，包括公民社会参与、地方政府运作、公共物品供给（医疗、教育、执法）以及各种地方集体行动能力指标（第2106-2111页）。

对于历史时期，作者使用了殖民时代地图（1878-1926年）中的电报线和道路密度、太平化态度与分析调查（1970-1972年）中的家庭收入数据，以及村落评估系统（1969-1973年）中的经济指标，包括非稻米食品供应、制成品供应和家庭车辆拥有情况（第2103-2105页）。

Independent variable (IV)

The main independent variable is the duration of exposure to Dai Viet administrative institutions, operationalized as a binary indicator for whether a village was organized into Vietnamese administrative villages by 1698 versus being incorporated only in the early 19th century. The treatment boundary corresponds to the 1698 establishment of Gia Dinh [ʒaː dɪŋ] province, when the Vietnamese state formally organized the Dong Nai [dɒŋ naɪ] and Sai Gon [saɪ ɡɒn] river basins into administrative villages with specific boundaries, regulated taxes, and population registers (p. 2091).

Areas to the east of the boundary (shown as black-shaded region in Figure 1) were organized as Dai Viet administrative villages in 1698, approximately 150 years prior to French colonization. In contrast, areas to the west were not organized as Vietnamese provinces until the 1820s and 1830s under Emperor Minh Mang [mɪn mæŋ], meaning Dai Viet institutions had little time to take root before colonial rule began (p. 2084). The key feature distinguishing these areas is not simply Vietnamese settlement, which the authors document was a fairly continuous process across both sides of the boundary, but rather the formal organization of communities under Dai Viet’s institutionalized village governance system (p. 2084).

The treatment effectively captures variation in the length of exposure to a specific institutional bundle characterized by: institutionalized village chiefs and councils elected by popular male suffrage since 1461; bureaucratic control of local taxation and military recruitment with village-level quotas; village responsibility for maintaining population and property registers; village supervision of local public works; and substantial village autonomy in policy implementation within parameters set by legal codes (pp. 2086, 2089).

主要自变量是接触大越行政制度的持续时间，操作化为一个二元指标，表示某村庄是否在1698年被组织为越南行政村落，还是直到19世纪初才被纳入。处理边界对应于1698年嘉定[ʒaː dɪŋ]省的建立，当时越南国家将同奈[dɒŋ naɪ]河和西贡[saɪ ɡɒn]河流域正式组织为具有明确边界、规范税收和人口登记的行政村落（第2091页）。

边界以东的地区（图1中黑色阴影区域）于1698年被组织为大越行政村落，比法国殖民早约150年。相比之下，边界以西的地区直到1820年代和1830年代明命帝[mɪn mæŋ]统治时期才被组织为越南省份，这意味着大越制度在殖民统治开始前几乎没有时间扎根（第2084页）。区分这些地区的关键特征不仅仅是越南人的定居，作者记录表明这是一个在边界两侧相当连续的过程，而是社区在大越制度化村庄治理体系下的正式组织（第2084页）。

该处理变量有效捕捉了接触特定制度组合的时间长度差异，这些制度特征包括：自1461年以来通过普遍男性选举产生的制度化村长和村议会；对地方税收和军事征兵的官僚控制及村级配额；村庄负责维护人口和财产登记；村庄监督地方公共工程；以及在法典规定的参数范围内村庄在政策执行方面的实质性自主权（第2086、2089页）。

Stateness

The paper conceptualizes the historical state not as a monolithic entity but as a bundle of characteristics, with particular emphasis on the administrative interface between central authority and local communities. The authors draw on an established literature in Asian studies that distinguishes between two fundamentally different models of state organization: the Sinic (Northeast Asian) model influenced by Chinese statecraft, and the Indic (Southeast Asian) model impacted by Hindu-Buddhist traditions imported from India (p. 2084).

The Dai Viet state, which ruled northern Vietnam and gradually expanded southward, is characterized as a Sinic-style polity that maintained many features of Chinese governance following independence from Chinese overlordship in 939 CE. Key features of this state type include: a centralized state with an impersonal bureaucracy under a dynastic court; uniform territorial administration; well-developed tax systems and legal codes; and critically, institutionalized village governance as the core of daily administration (pp. 2084, 2086). The central state set quotas for tax and military conscript contributions at the village level but lacked the capability to micro-manage local administration. Instead, villages possessed considerable autonomy in policy implementation, and villagers had to work together to provide local public goods, maintain population and property registers, and meet village-level quotas (p. 2084).

In contrast, the Khmer [kmɛr] (Cambodian) state exemplifies the Indic or “mandala” model characterized by: a decentralized patron-client structure; personalized power relations with peasants paying tribute and receiving protection from landowning patrons; semi-independent provincial rule; temple-based public finance systems; and the absence of the village as a central administrative unit (pp. 2084, 2086). The Khmer state’s control over its Mekong [ˈmeɪkɒŋ] periphery was weak, with political appointments and land distribution being personalistic rather than bureaucratic (p. 2089).

The measurement of historical state exposure is therefore based on the timing of incorporation into Vietnamese administrative structures. The authors carefully distinguish between military conquest, economic settlement, and administrative organization. Settlement of the Khmer periphery was a fairly continuous process across what would become the treatment boundary, but formal administrative organization, with the establishment of communes, villages, and hamlets with specific boundaries, regulated taxes, and farming population registers, occurred discretely in 1698 for areas east of the boundary (p. 2091).

Table I in the paper provides a systematic comparison of the two state types across multiple dimensions including: colonial history (Chinese colony versus Indic patron-client state); governmental system (bureaucratic versus personalistic); degree of centralization (centralized versus decentralized); village governance (institutionalized elected councils versus personalistic appointments); and public finance (bureaucratic taxation versus temple-based systems) (p. 2086).

本文将历史国家概念化为一组特征的集合，而非单一实体，特别强调中央权威与地方社区之间的行政接口。作者借鉴亚洲研究中已有的文献传统，区分了两种根本不同的国家组织模式：受中国治国之道影响的中华（东北亚）模式，以及受从印度传入的印度教-佛教传统影响的印度化（东南亚）模式。

大越国家统治着越南北部并逐渐向南扩张，被归类为中华式政体，在公元939年脱离中国统治独立后保留了中国治理的许多特征。这种国家类型的关键特征包括：在王朝宫廷下拥有非人格化官僚体系的中央集权国家；统一的领土行政管理；发达的税收制度和法典；以及关键的，制度化村庄治理作为日常行政的核心。中央政府在村级设定税收和兵役配额，但缺乏微观管理地方行政的能力。相反，村庄在政策执行方面拥有相当大的自主权，村民必须合作提供地方公共物品、维护人口和财产登记、并完成村级配额。

相比之下，高棉（柬埔寨）国家体现了印度化或“曼荼罗”模式，其特征包括：分散的庇护-侍从结构；人格化的权力关系，农民向地主庇护者纳贡并获得保护；半独立的省级统治；基于寺庙的公共财政体系；以及关键的，村庄不作为核心行政单位。高棉国家对其湄公河边缘地区的控制较弱，政治任命和土地分配是人格化而非官僚化的。

因此，历史国家暴露程度的测量基于纳入越南行政结构的时间。作者仔细区分了军事征服、经济定居和行政组织三者。高棉边缘地区的定居在后来成为处理边界的两侧是一个相当连续的过程，但正式的行政组织，建立具有明确边界、规范税收和农业人口登记的社、村、屯，在边界以东地区于1698年离散地发生。

论文中的表I在多个维度上系统比较了两种国家类型，包括：殖民历史（中国殖民地与印度化庇护-侍从国家）；政府体系（官僚化与人格化）；集权程度（集权与分权）；村庄治理（制度化选举产生的议会与人格化任命）；以及公共财政（官僚化税收与基于寺庙的系统）。

Core argument

The core argument of the paper is that historical state institutions, specifically the institutionalized village governance characteristic of Northeast Asian states like Dai Viet, generate persistent positive effects on long-run economic development by “crowding in” local collective action norms that endure long after the original formal institutions have disappeared (p. 2083).

The argument proceeds in several steps. First, the authors contend that the Northeast Asian historical state required villages to coordinate internally to meet obligations imposed by the central government. In Dai Viet, the central state required village councils elected by popular male suffrage to coordinate taxation, public goods provision, conscription, and record keeping. Villages had to work together to provide local public goods, maintain population and property registers, and meet village-level tax and military quotas (p. 2084). This institutional structure created incentives for villagers to develop cooperative norms and practices.

Second, the authors argue that these norms of local cooperation persisted even after the original institutional impetus disappeared. The pre-colonial institutions of both Dai Viet and Khmer were abolished by French colonization, and the entire study region has since been governed by three different central states: France, the Republic of Vietnam (South Vietnam), and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The colonial state did not have the resources to reshape local governance norms in thousands of villages. Informal norms could therefore persist even as formal institutions changed (p. 2084).

Third, the authors propose that persistent norms of local cooperation influence economic development through multiple channels. Local cooperation can address challenges that neither central states nor markets are able to solve, for example, allowing communities to overcome incomplete contracting or informational problems that prevent the central state from providing public goods. When communities can effectively organize to provide public goods like education, healthcare, and law enforcement, residents benefit from better services that enhance human capital and productivity. When civic organizations can coordinate mutual assistance and insurance, households are better protected against shocks. These mechanisms create a virtuous cycle in which areas with stronger local cooperation norms experience better developmental outcomes over time (pp. 2085-2086).

The argument explicitly challenges the hypothesis, advanced by scholars such as Putnam (1994) and Fukuyama (1995), that strong states necessarily crowd out civil society. The authors argue that the relationship between state capacity and local cooperation depends on specific institutional features. When the state operates through institutionalized village governance that requires local participation and coordination, it can actually crowd in cooperative norms rather than crowding them out. The key is whether state institutions complement local organization or substitute for it (p. 2088).

本文的核心论点是，历史国家制度，特别是大越等东北亚国家特有的制度化村庄治理，通过“挤入”地方集体行动规范而对长期经济发展产生持久的积极影响，这些规范在原有正式制度消失很久之后仍然持续存在（第2083页）。

该论证分几个步骤展开。首先，作者认为东北亚历史国家要求村庄在内部进行协调以履行中央政府施加的义务。在大越，中央政府要求由普遍男性选举产生的村议会协调税收、公共物品供给、征兵和档案管理。村民必须合作提供地方公共物品、维护人口和财产登记、并完成村级税收和兵役配额（第2084页）。这种制度结构激励村民发展合作规范和实践。

其次，作者认为这些地方合作规范即使在原有制度推动力消失后也持续存在。大越和高棉的前殖民制度都被法国殖民统治废除，整个研究地区此后先后被三个不同的中央政府统治：法国、越南共和国（南越）和越南社会主义共和国。关键的是，殖民政府没有资源来重塑数千个村庄的地方治理规范，这意味着即使正式制度发生变化，非正式规范也可以持续存在（第2084页）。

第三，作者提出持续的地方合作规范通过多种渠道影响经济发展。地方合作可以解决中央政府和市场都无法解决的挑战，例如，使社区能够克服阻止中央政府提供公共物品的不完全契约或信息问题。当社区能够有效组织起来提供教育、医疗和执法等公共物品时，居民将受益于能够提升人力资本和生产力的更好服务。当公民组织能够协调互助和保险时，家庭能更好地抵御冲击。这些机制创造了一个良性循环，使具有更强地方合作规范的地区随着时间推移获得更好的发展成果（第2085-2086页）。

该论证明确挑战了普特南（1994）和福山（1995）等学者提出的“强国家必然挤出公民社会”的假说。作者认为，国家能力与地方合作之间的关系取决于具体的制度特征。当国家通过需要地方参与和协调的制度化村庄治理来运作时，它实际上可以挤入而非挤出合作规范。关键在于国家制度是补充还是替代地方组织（第2088页）。

Mechanisms

The paper proposes that the primary mechanism of persistence is through informal norms of local cooperation that were historically developed in Dai Viet villages to meet their obligations to the central state. While the treatment, date of organization as a Vietnamese administrative village, is institutional, the authors argue that persistence is not through formal institutions themselves, since pre-colonial institutions were abolished by the French and the entire study region has subsequently been subjected to the same formal institutions (p. 2085).

The first mechanism involves civil society participation and collective action capacity. The historical requirement for Dai Viet villages to coordinate taxation, conscription, public goods provision, and record keeping fostered norms of civic engagement that persisted across generations. The authors document that citizens on the Dai Viet side of the boundary were nearly twice as likely between 1969 and 1973 to participate in local civic organizations. They were also more likely to organize and participate in self-development projects, local self-defense forces, attend local government meetings, and have civic organizations that redistribute to needy households (p. 2086). These patterns persist to the present: households in Dai Viet villages are more likely to donate to charitable organizations in contemporary survey data (p. 2108).

The second mechanism operates through local government functioning. A 1967 constitutional reform in South Vietnam granted villages expansive budgetary powers and public goods provision responsibilities, with citizens electing village heads and councils. The authors argue that this reform worked best in places with a history of participatory village governance (p. 2086). Between 1969 and 1973, villages on the Dai Viet side of the boundary were more likely to collect taxes systematically, have village heads who actually resided in the village, and have all positions filled on the village committee that organized public goods provision. Citizens in Dai Viet villages also reported in public opinion surveys that local government was more responsive to their needs (p. 2086).

The third mechanism involves public goods provision itself. Dai Viet villages provided better access to basic healthcare (government-provided medical services were 20 percentage points more likely to be available), education (6 percentage points more likely to have primary school access, 9 percentage points higher primary completion rate), and law enforcement (22 percentage points more likely to have authorities enforce the law). Villages were also more likely to provide assistance to needy households (pp. 2110-2111).

The authors explicitly consider and rule out several alternative mechanisms. First, they show that the effects are not driven by the Vietnam War, as extensive data suggest security conditions and conflict intensity were similar across the boundary (pp. 2112-2114). Second, the effects do not appear to be driven by land inequality: while 97% of French-owned land was on the Khmer side at the close of the colonial period, there were almost no French estates near the boundary, and contemporary data show no difference in average farm size for agricultural households (pp. 2087, 2114). Third, ethnic heterogeneity does not appear to be a direct cause: nearly everyone in the study region identifies as Vietnamese today and did so 50 years ago, and the historical ethnic composition was similar across the boundary at the time of Dai Viet’s expansion (p. 2087). Fourth, the results are not driven by differential treatment by higher levels of government: there are no effects on public goods provided by provincial government, and citizens on the Dai Viet side actually had more negative views of the national government (pp. 2086-2087).

本文提出持续性的主要机制是通过非正式的地方合作规范，这些规范是大越村庄在历史上为履行对中央政府的义务而发展起来的。虽然处理变量，被组织为越南行政村落的日期，是制度性的，但作者认为持续性并非通过正式制度本身，因为殖民前的制度已被法国废除，整个研究地区此后一直处于相同的正式制度之下（第2085页）。

第一个机制涉及公民社会参与和集体行动能力。大越村庄协调税收、征兵、公共物品供给和档案管理的历史要求培育了代代相传的公民参与规范。作者记录表明，1969年至1973年间，边界大越一侧的公民参与地方公民组织的可能性几乎是另一侧的两倍。他们也更可能组织和参与自我发展项目、地方自卫力量，出席地方政府会议，并拥有向贫困家庭再分配的公民组织（第2086页）。这些模式持续到现在：在当代调查数据中，大越村庄的家庭更可能向慈善组织捐款（第2108页）。

第二个机制通过地方政府运作发挥作用。1967年南越的宪法改革赋予村庄广泛的预算权力和公共物品供给责任，公民选举村长和村议会。作者认为这项改革在具有参与式村庄治理历史的地方效果最好（第2086页）。1969年至1973年间，边界大越一侧的村庄更可能系统性地征税，村长更可能实际居住在村里，组织公共物品供给的村委会更可能所有职位都有人担任。大越村庄的公民在民意调查中也报告说地方政府对其需求更加回应（第2086页）。

第三个机制涉及公共物品供给本身。大越村庄提供了更好的基本医疗服务（政府提供的医疗服务可获得性高20个百分点）、教育（获得小学教育的可能性高6个百分点，小学完成率高9个百分点）和执法（当局执法的可能性高22个百分点）。村庄也更可能向贫困家庭提供援助（第2110-2111页）。

作者明确考虑并排除了几种替代机制。首先，他们表明这些效应不是由越南战争驱动的，因为大量数据表明边界两侧的安全状况和冲突强度相似（第2112-2114页）。其次，这些效应似乎不是由土地不平等驱动的：虽然殖民时期结束时97%的法国人拥有的土地位于高棉一侧，但边界附近几乎没有法国庄园，当代数据显示农业家庭的平均农场规模没有差异（第2087、2114页）。第三，族群异质性似乎不是直接原因：今天研究地区几乎所有人都认同自己是越南人，50年前也是如此，而且在大越扩张时期边界两侧的历史族群构成相似（第2087页）。第四，结果不是由更高层级政府的差别对待驱动的：省级政府提供的公共物品没有差异效应，大越一侧的公民实际上对中央政府持有更负面的看法（第2086-2087页）。

Data & methods

Data

The paper employs an exceptionally rich array of data sources spanning approximately 150 years of Vietnamese history, combining contemporary household surveys, historical administrative records, geographic information, and unique wartime data collections.

Contemporary Economic Data (2002-2012): The primary source for contemporary living standards is the Vietnam Household Living Standards Surveys (VHLSS), collected biennially by the General Statistics Office of Vietnam with technical assistance from the World Bank. The surveys include a core module administered to a large sample of households and an additional expenditure module for a subsample. To create a panel while avoiding repeated observations, the authors drop households surveyed in consecutive rounds. The main outcome is log equivalent household consumption, constructed by subtracting transfers from total consumption and adjusting for household composition (pp. 2097-2098). Years of schooling are measured for individuals over age 25. The unit of analysis is typically the household or individual, with standard errors clustered at the village level.

South Vietnamese Period Data (1969-1973): The Hamlet Evaluation System (HES) provides quarterly data on economic, social, political, and security conditions for nearly all 18,000 South Vietnamese hamlets. This data was compiled jointly by U.S. and Vietnamese advisers in conjunction with local officials and represents an unusually comprehensive administrative dataset. Variables include indicators of economic prosperity, civil society participation, local government functioning, public goods provision, and security conditions. The authors code categorical responses into binary indicators and employ Bayesian Latent Class Analysis to construct summary indices (pp. 2104-2105). The Pacification Attitudes and Analysis Survey (PAAS) provides monthly household-level data from random samples of six hamlets per province, with 15 randomly selected respondents per hamlet stratified by demographics. This survey includes household income (measured in bins with midpoint assignment) and public opinion questions (pp. 2103-2104).

Colonial Period Data (1878-1926): Village-level infrastructure data are extracted from georeferenced maps held by the Bibliothèque Nationale de France [biblijɔtɛk nasjɔnal də fʁɑ̃s]. These maps show telegraph lines, roads, and railroads for 1878, 1901, 1910, and 1926, which the authors intersect with village boundaries to calculate density measures (p. 2103). French landownership data at the close of the colonial period were digitized from records compiled by the Stanford Research Institute (p. 2093).

Geographic and Administrative Data: The study uses gridded geographic data for balance checks, including elevation, slope, temperature, precipitation, agricultural suitability (rice, coconut, sugar), flow accumulation from USGS Hydrosheds, and river density. The unit of analysis for these checks is 10km × 10km grid cells (pp. 2095-2096). Historical boundary data come from Vietnamese historical sources including Dùc and Tao (1972) and Su Quan Trieu Nguyen and Pham (1992) (p. 2085).

Post-Reunification Data (1975-2004): District-level data on land ownership and rice cultivation during 1975-1985 were digitized from provincial yearbooks and declassified Vietnamese Communist Party documents. Commune-level data on secondary school availability (1999-2004) and land certification come from VHLSS commune questionnaires and provincial yearbooks (pp. 2105-2106, 2111).

Census Data: The 2009 population census is used to examine migration patterns by comparing current residence to residence in 2004 (p. 2097). The 2011-2012 Enterprise Census provides information on manufacturing and foreign sector employment (p. 2115).

The geographic coverage focuses on the study region within approximately 25 kilometers of the 1698 boundary (baseline bandwidth), though robustness checks extend to 100 kilometers and all of South Vietnam. The boundary is divided into 25-kilometer segments for fixed effects (p. 2095).

本文采用了跨越约150年越南历史的极为丰富的数据来源，结合了当代家庭调查、历史行政记录、地理信息和独特的战时数据收集。

当代经济数据（2002-2012年）： 当代生活水平的主要来源是越南家庭生活水平调查（VHLSS），由越南统计总局在世界银行的技术援助下每两年收集一次。调查包括针对大样本家庭的核心模块和针对子样本的额外支出模块。为创建面板数据同时避免重复观测，作者剔除了连续轮次中被调查的家庭。主要结果变量是对数等效家庭消费，通过从总消费中减去转移收入并调整家庭构成来构建（第2097-2098页）。受教育年限针对25岁以上个体测量。分析单位通常是家庭或个人，标准误在村级聚类。

南越时期数据（1969-1973年）： 村落评估系统（HES）为近18,000个南越村落提供了关于经济、社会、政治和安全状况的季度数据。这些数据由美国和越南顾问与地方官员共同编制，代表了一个异常全面的行政数据集。变量包括经济繁荣、公民社会参与、地方政府运作、公共物品供给和安全状况的指标。作者将分类响应编码为二元指标，并采用贝叶斯潜在类别分析构建综合指数（第2104-2105页）。太平化态度与分析调查（PAAS）提供了来自每省六个随机抽样村落的月度家庭层面数据，每个村落按人口统计特征分层随机选择15名受访者。该调查包括家庭收入（以分组方式测量并取中点）和民意问题（第2103-2104页）。

殖民时期数据（1878-1926年）： 村级基础设施数据从法国国家图书馆[biblijɔtɛk nasjɔnal də fʁɑ̃s]持有的地理参照地图中提取。这些地图显示了1878年、1901年、1910年和1926年的电报线、道路和铁路，作者将其与村庄边界相交以计算密度指标（第2103页）。殖民时期结束时法国人土地所有权数据从斯坦福研究所编制的记录中数字化（第2093页）。

地理和行政数据： 研究使用网格化地理数据进行平衡检验，包括海拔、坡度、温度、降水、农业适宜性（水稻、椰子、糖）、USGS Hydrosheds的流量累积和河流密度。这些检验的分析单位是10公里×10公里的网格单元（第2095-2096页）。历史边界数据来自越南历史文献，包括Dùc和Tao（1972）以及

Su Quan Trieu Nguyen和Pham（1992）（第2085页）。

统一后数据（1975-2004年）： 1975-1985年间土地所有权和水稻种植的区级数据从省级年鉴和解密的越南共产党文件中数字化。1999-2004年间中学可获得性和土地认证的社级数据来自VHLSS社区问卷和省级年鉴（第2105-2106、2111页）。

人口普查数据： 2009年人口普查用于通过比较当前居住地与2004年居住地来检验迁移模式（第2097页）。2011-2012年企业普查提供了制造业和外资部门就业信息（第2115页）。

地理覆盖范围集中在距1698年边界约25公里范围内的研究区域（基准带宽），尽管稳健性检验扩展到100公里和整个南越。边界被划分为25公里的分段用于固定效应（第2095页）。

Methods

The paper employs a spatial regression discontinuity (RD) design to estimate the causal effect of historical state institutions on long-run development. The fundamental empirical strategy compares nearby villages on opposite sides of the 1698 boundary, the date when areas to the east were formally organized as Vietnamese administrative villages while areas to the west remained under nominal Khmer control until the early 19th century.

The baseline regression specification takes the form:

\[ out_v=\alpha+\gamma DaiViet_v+f(geographic\ location_v) +\sum_i seg^i_v+\beta dist\_hcm_v+\varepsilon_v. \]

where \(out_v\) is the outcome of interest in village \(v\), \(DaiViet_v\) is an indicator equal to 1 if the village was on the Dai Viet side of the 1698 boundary, \(f(geographic\ location_v)\) is the RD polynomial controlling for smooth functions of geographic location, \(seg^i_v\) are boundary segment fixed effects (25km segments), and \(dist\_hcm_v\) controls for distance to Ho Chi Minh City [hoʊ tʃiː mɪn] (p. 2095).

Following Gelman and Imbens (2014), the baseline specification uses a local linear RD polynomial with a 25-kilometer bandwidth. The paper documents extensive robustness to: alternative bandwidths (10-100km); different polynomial specifications (linear versus quadratic in latitude-longitude, distance to boundary, or both); excluding Ho Chi Minh City or all provincial capitals; limiting to river or non-river segments of the boundary; including province fixed effects; and extending the sample to all of South Vietnam (pp. 2098-2101).

Identifying Assumptions: The key identifying assumption is that all relevant factors besides treatment vary smoothly at the boundary, formally, that \(E[c_1\mid x,y]\) and \(E[c_0\mid x,y]\) are continuous at the discontinuity threshold, where \(c_1\) and \(c_0\) are potential outcomes under treatment and control, and \(x\) and \(y\) denote longitude and latitude (p. 2095).

The paper provides extensive evidence supporting this assumption. Table II presents balance checks on geographic characteristics using gridded data, showing no statistically significant differences in elevation, slope, temperature, rice suitability, coconut suitability, sugar suitability, flow accumulation, or river density. Only precipitation shows a marginally significant difference that is small relative to the mean (pp. 2095-2096).

The historical narrative supports the identifying assumption. The authors document that the delay in administrative expansion west of the boundary was due to idiosyncratic political circumstances at the center of the Dai Viet and Khmer empires, specifically, internal civil conflicts in both polities and strategic calculations about the relative benefits of formal annexation versus informal influence, rather than economic or cultural differences at the boundary itself. Vietnamese settlement of the region was a fairly continuous process across what would become the treatment boundary, with similar populations of Vietnamese, Chinese merchants, Khmer communities, and scattered Cham and Malay settlements on both sides (pp. 2090-2092).

Additional Methods: The paper employs Bayesian Latent Class Analysis (LCA) to address concerns about multiple hypothesis testing and categorical response coding in the HES data. LCA uses a finite mixture model to estimate posterior probabilities that each observation belongs to “high” or “low” latent groups based on observed categorical responses, reducing the dimensionality of the comparison (pp. 2104-2105).

Placebo tests include: examining other portions of the same rivers that do not coincide with the historical boundary; comparing across provincial boundaries within purely Dai Viet or Khmer areas; and examining other historical boundaries of Dai Viet expansion where areas on both sides had been under institutionalized village governance for centuries (pp. 2101-2102).

A randomization inference exercise randomly reassigns distance to the boundary 1000 times and compares the distribution of placebo coefficients to the actual estimate (p. 2101).

本文采用空间断点回归（RD）设计来估计历史国家制度对长期发展的因果效应。基本实证策略是比较1698年边界两侧相邻的村庄，该日期东侧地区被正式组织为越南行政村落，而西侧地区直到19世纪初仍处于名义上的高棉控制之下。

基准回归规范形式为：

\[ out_v=\alpha+\gamma DaiViet_v+f(geographic\ location_v) +\sum_i seg^i_v+\beta dist\_hcm_v+\varepsilon_v. \]

其中 \(out_v\) 是村庄 \(v\) 的结果变量，\(DaiViet_v\) 是一个指示变量，如果村庄位于1698年边界的大越一侧则等于1，\(f(geographic\ location_v)\) 是控制地理位置平滑函数的RD多项式，\(seg^i_v\) 是边界分段固定效应（25公里分段），\(dist\_hcm_v\) 控制到胡志明市[hoʊ tʃiː mɪn]的距离（第2095页）。

基准规范使用25公里带宽的局部线性RD多项式。论文记录了广泛的稳健性检验：替代带宽（10-100公里）；不同的多项式规范（经纬度线性与二次、到边界距离、或两者兼有）；排除胡志明市或所有省会城市；限制在边界的河流或非河流分段；包含省份固定效应；以及将样本扩展到整个南越（第2098-2101页）。

识别假设： 关键识别假设是除处理变量外的所有相关因素在边界处平滑变化，形式上，\(E[c_1\mid x,y]\) 和 \(E[c_0\mid x,y]\) 在断点阈值处连续，其中 \(c_1\) 和 \(c_0\) 是处理和对照下的潜在结果，\(x\) 和 \(y\) 表示经度和纬度（第2095页）。

论文提供了支持这一假设的大量证据。表II使用网格数据呈现了地理特征的平衡检验，显示海拔、坡度、温度、水稻适宜性、椰子适宜性、糖适宜性、流量累积或河流密度方面没有统计显著差异。只有降水显示出相对于均值较小的边际显著差异（第2095-2096页）。

历史叙事为识别假设提供了关键支持。作者记录表明，边界以西行政扩张的延迟是由于大越和高棉帝国中心的特殊政治环境，具体而言，两个政体内部的内战以及关于正式吞并与非正式影响相对收益的战略考量，而非边界本身的经济或文化差异。越南人在该地区的定居在后来成为处理边界的两侧是一个相当连续的过程，两侧有相似的越南人、华商、高棉社区以及零星的占族和马来人定居点（第2090-2092页）。

附加方法： 论文采用贝叶斯潜在类别分析（LCA）来解决HES数据中多重假设检验和分类响应编码的问题。LCA使用有限混合模型，基于观察到的分类响应估计每个观测值属于“高”或“低”潜在组的后验概率，降低了比较的维度（第2104-2105页）。

安慰剂检验包括：检验与历史边界不重合的同一河流的其他部分；在纯大越或高棉区域内跨省界进行比较；以及检验大越扩张的其他历史边界，在那里边界两侧都已在制度化村庄治理下运行了几个世纪（第2101-2102页）。

随机推断练习将到边界的距离随机重新分配1000次，并将安慰剂系数的分布与实际估计值进行比较（第2101页）。

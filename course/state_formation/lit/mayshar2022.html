Week 3 · Material–Technological lane · Pristine states (S1)

Joram Mayshar, Omer Moav & Luigi Pascali (2022), “The Origin of the State: Land Productivity or Appropriability?”, JPE.梅沙尔、莫阿夫、帕斯卡利（2022）《国家的起源：土地生产力还是可占用性？》，JPE。

Formal modelPanel FEIV

Dependent variable (DV)

The primary dependent variable is hierarchical complexity or state formation. The authors conceptualize this as the degree of political integration and jurisdictional authority beyond the local community level. This variable is measured in multiple ways across different datasets. First, using Murdock’s Ethnographic Atlas, the authors employ a variable called “jurisdictional hierarchy beyond the local community,” which is an ordered variable with five possible levels: (1) no political authority beyond community, (2) petty chiefdoms, (3) larger chiefdoms, (4) states, and (5) large states (p. 1099). Second, using data from Borcan, Olsson, and Putterman (2018), the authors construct a hierarchy index for 159 modern-day countries, where a score of 1 is assigned if there is a government above the tribal level, 0.75 if there is a chiefdom, and 0 if there is no authority above the tribe (pp. 1102-1103). Third, as proxies for early hierarchy, the authors use the presence and number of ancient cities and archaeological sites (such as pyramids, temples, palaces, and mines), based on the assumption that the existence of cities and monumental architecture indicates social hierarchy (pp. 1103-1104). Fourth, using the Archaeological Atlas of the World, the authors count the number of pre-Neolithic and post-Neolithic archaeological sites to examine how the Neolithic transition affected traces of complex societies (p. 1104).

主要因变量是等级复杂性（hierarchical complexity）或国家形成（state formation）。作者将其概念化为超越地方社区层面的政治整合和管辖权威的程度。该变量在不同数据集中通过多种方式进行测量。首先，使用默多克 (Murdock) 的《民族志地图集》(Ethnographic Atlas)，作者采用了一个名为“超越地方社区的管辖等级”的变量，这是一个包含五个可能层级的有序变量：(1) 社区之外没有政治权威，(2) 小型酋邦，(3) 大型酋邦，(4) 国家，(5) 大型国家（第1099页）。其次，使用博尔坎、奥尔森和普特曼 (Borcan, Olsson, and Putterman, 2018) 的数据，作者为159个现代国家构建了等级指数，其中如果存在部落层级以上的政府则赋值为1，如果是酋邦则赋值为0.75，如果没有部落以上的权威则赋值为0（第1102-1103页）。第三，作为早期等级制度的代理变量，作者使用古代城市和考古遗址（如金字塔、神庙、宫殿和矿山）的存在和数量，其假设是城市和纪念性建筑的存在表明社会等级制度的存在（第1103-1104页）。第四，使用《世界考古地图集》(Archaeological Atlas of the World)，作者统计了新石器时代之前和之后考古遗址的数量，以考察新石器时代转型如何影响复杂社会的痕迹（第1104页）。

Independent variable (IV)

The primary independent variable is the cultivation of cereal grains (as opposed to roots and tubers), which the authors argue represents the key distinction in crop “appropriability.” This variable is operationalized in several ways. First, the authors use a dummy variable that identifies societies whose primary crop is cereals, based on information from the Ethnographic Atlas about the major crop type of societies that practice agriculture (pp. 1099-1102). Second, and more importantly for causal identification, the authors use the productivity advantage of cereals over roots and tubers, measured as the difference between the maximum potential caloric yield of cereals and that of roots or tubers under rain-fed, low-input agriculture. This measure is constructed using data from the FAO’s Global Agro-Ecological Zones (GAEZ) project, which provides estimates of potential crop yields based on agroclimatic conditions that are unaffected by human intervention (pp. 1104-1106). Third, the authors utilize data on the distribution of wild relatives (WRs) of domesticated crops from the Global Crop Diversity Trust. The number of WRs of a domesticated crop in a region proxies for the potential for domestication of that crop in that region. The authors distinguish between areas with only WRs of cereals available, areas with only WRs of roots and tubers, and areas where WRs of both are available (pp. 1107-1108). Fourth, the authors use distance to centers of cereal domestication based on archaeological data identifying approximately 20 centers where independent domestication of plants and animals took place (p. 1109).

主要自变量是谷物的种植（与块根和块茎作物相对），作者认为这代表了作物“可占用性”（appropriability）的关键区别。该变量通过几种方式进行操作化。首先，作者使用一个虚拟变量来识别主要作物为谷物的社会，这基于《民族志地图集》中关于从事农业社会的主要作物类型的信息（第1099-1102页）。其次，对于因果识别更重要的是，作者使用谷物相对于块根和块茎的生产力优势，测量为在雨养、低投入农业条件下谷物的最大潜在热量产量与块根或块茎的最大潜在热量产量之间的差异。这一测量使用联合国粮农组织 (FAO) 全球农业生态区 (GAEZ) 项目的数据构建，该项目提供基于不受人类干预影响的农业气候条件的潜在作物产量估计（第1104-1106页）。第三，作者利用来自全球作物多样性信托基金 (Global Crop Diversity Trust) 的驯化作物野生近缘种 (WRs) 分布数据。某一地区某种驯化作物野生近缘种的数量代表了该地区驯化该作物的潜力。作者区分了仅有谷物野生近缘种的地区、仅有块根和块茎野生近缘种的地区，以及两者兼有的地区（第1107-1108页）。第四，作者使用到谷物驯化中心的距离，基于考古数据识别出大约20个发生植物和动物独立驯化的中心（第1109页）。

Stateness

The authors conceptualize the “state” as a tax-levying elite that emerged following the Neolithic transition from hunting-gathering to sedentary farming. The state is understood as a complex hierarchical society characterized by political integration beyond the local community level, the existence of administrative structures, and the capacity to extract surplus from the farming population through taxation (p. 1092). The emergence of the state is placed within an evolutionary framework of political complexity, ranging from acephalous tribal societies with no political authority beyond the community, through various levels of chiefdoms, to full-fledged states and large states (p. 1099). The authors distinguish between two regimes in their formal model: “anarchy,” where there is no protection against looting and the number of bandits is determined endogenously so that their average revenue from theft equals the alternative productivity in foraging; and “hierarchy,” where there is a net revenue-maximizing elite that commits to a selected rate of taxation and provides farmers with full protection from bandits. A state can exist only if its tax revenue covers the fixed cost of deterring bandits (pp. 1094-1095). This conceptualization draws on Olson’s (1993) notion of the “stationary bandit” who deters roving bandits while extracting surplus, though the authors note their theory does not require assumptions about whether the elite is benign or self-serving. The measurement of stateness employs the ordered hierarchy variable from the Ethnographic Atlas, which captures jurisdictional hierarchy beyond the local community across five levels (p. 1099), as well as the Borcan, Olsson, and Putterman (2018) index that assigns scores based on the presence of government above the tribal level (pp. 1102-1103).

作者将“国家”概念化为新石器时代从狩猎采集向定居农业转型后出现的征税精英。国家被理解为一种复杂的等级社会，其特征是超越地方社区层面的政治整合、行政结构的存在，以及最关键的，通过税收从农业人口中提取剩余的能力（第1092页）。国家的出现被置于政治复杂性的演化框架中，从没有超越社区政治权威的无首领部落社会，经过各级酋邦，到完整的国家和大型国家（第1099页）。作者在其正式模型中区分了两种制度：“无政府状态”，即没有针对掠夺的保护，盗匪的数量是内生决定的，使得他们从盗窃中获得的平均收入等于从事觅食的替代生产力；以及“等级制度”，即存在一个追求净收入最大化的精英群体，承诺选定的税率并为农民提供免受盗匪侵害的全面保护。只有当税收收入能够覆盖威慑盗匪的固定成本时，国家才能存在（第1094-1095页）。这一概念化借鉴了奥尔森 (Olson, 1993) 关于“固定盗匪”的概念，即在提取剩余的同时威慑流动盗匪，尽管作者指出他们的理论不需要假设精英是仁慈的还是自利的。国家性的测量采用《民族志地图集》中的有序等级变量，该变量在五个层级上捕捉超越地方社区的管辖等级（第1099页），以及博尔坎、奥尔森和普特曼 (2018) 的指数，该指数根据部落层级以上政府的存在情况赋值（第1102-1103页）。

Core argument

The core argument of the paper fundamentally challenges the conventional “productivity-and-surplus” theory about the origin of the state, which traces back to Adam Smith and has been the default explanation in both popular and scientific writing. The conventional theory holds that following the adoption of farming, complex social hierarchies emerged as a result of increased food output. Specifically, high output generated a surplus (food in excess of farmers’ subsistence needs), which was a prerequisite for the rise of an elite that taxed farmers and supported bureaucrats, troops, and other specialists who did not engage in food production (p. 1092). The authors challenge this theory and propose instead that hierarchy arose as a result of the shift to dependence on appropriable cereal grains, not simply as a result of increased productivity (p. 1091). The key insight is that cereals are fundamentally different from other crops like roots and tubers in their “appropriability”, the ease with which they can be confiscated, taxed, and transported. Cereals are harvested seasonally and must be stored for year-round consumption, making them vulnerable to appropriation. Their high energy density and durability further enhance their appropriability, thereby facilitating the emergence of tax-levying elites (p. 1092). By contrast, roots and tubers are typically perennial and do not have to be reaped in a particular period, and once harvested they are rather perishable, making them difficult to confiscate and practically impossible to transport for use by a distant elite (p. 1093). The authors contend that surplus is not a prerequisite for the emergence of a tax-levying elite; rather, once the opportunity to appropriate arises, the elite generates the food surplus on which it can flourish (p. 1093). This reverses the causal logic of the conventional theory: it is not that surplus enables taxation, but that the possibility of taxation (enabled by appropriable crops) generates surplus. The authors further argue that increased productivity without increased appropriability would not lead to the emergence of complex hierarchy, as increased output would simply be absorbed by population growth through the Malthusian mechanism (pp. 1093-1094). This explains why complex hierarchies did not emerge among hunter-gatherers despite productivity improvements over time, and why some agricultural societies based on roots and tubers similarly failed to develop states despite productive farming (p. 1094).

本文的核心论点从根本上挑战了关于国家起源的传统“生产力与剩余”理论，这一理论可追溯到亚当·斯密 (Adam Smith)，一直是通俗和科学写作中的默认解释。传统理论认为，在采用农业之后，复杂的社会等级制度是由于食物产出增加而出现的。具体而言，高产出产生了剩余（超出农民生存需要的食物），这是征税精英崛起的先决条件，这些精英向农民征税并供养不从事食物生产的官僚、军队和其他专业人员（第1092页）。作者挑战了这一理论，并提出等级制度是由于转向依赖可占用的谷物而产生的，而不仅仅是由于生产力提高的结果（第1091页）。关键洞见是谷物与块根和块茎等其他作物在“可占用性”，即被没收、征税和运输的容易程度，上存在根本性差异。谷物是季节性收获的，必须储存以供全年消费，这使得它们容易被占用。它们的高能量密度和耐久性进一步增强了其可占用性，从而促进了征税精英的出现（第1092页）。相比之下，块根和块茎通常是多年生的，不必在特定时期收获，一旦收获后相当容易腐烂，使得它们难以被没收，几乎不可能被运输以供远方精英使用（第1093页）。作者主张剩余不是征税精英出现的先决条件；相反，一旦占用的机会出现，精英就会产生其赖以繁荣的食物剩余（第1093页）。这颠倒了传统理论的因果逻辑：不是剩余使得税收成为可能，而是税收的可能性（由可占用作物所促成）产生了剩余。作者进一步论证，没有增加可占用性的生产力提高不会导致复杂等级制度的出现，因为增加的产出会通过马尔萨斯机制被人口增长所吸收（第1093-1094页）。这解释了为什么尽管生产力随时间提高，狩猎采集者中没有出现复杂的等级制度，以及为什么一些基于块根和块茎的农业社会尽管有高产农业，同样未能发展出国家（第1094页）。

Mechanisms

The authors propose several interconnected mechanisms through which the cultivation of appropriable cereal grains influences the emergence of hierarchical complexity and states. First, the storage mechanism: Cereal grains must be harvested within a short period and then stored for year-round consumption due to their seasonal nature. This creates stockpiles of food that are concentrated in time and space, making them vulnerable to appropriation. A tax collector could confiscate part of the stored grain and transport it for consumption by distant elite and other non-food producers, even if there is no food surplus (p. 1093). Second, the equilibrium surplus mechanism: Even if ongoing confiscation would lead to a shrinking population, because of diminishing average product of labor, the smaller population would produce higher output per farmer. This results in an equilibrium with a stable population in which total output exceeds the farming population’s subsistence needs, with the surplus confiscated by the nonfarming elite. This demonstrates that surplus is not a precondition for taxation but rather a consequence of it (p. 1093). Third, the protection mechanism: Stored cereals are appropriable by a would-be elite and by bandits. Therefore, cereal cultivation generated a demand for protection and at the same time facilitated taxation to finance the supply of such protection by the elite (p. 1094). The authors’ formal model shows that a state can exist only if cereals are sufficiently more productive than tubers, as this allows tax revenue to cover the fixed cost of deterring bandits (p. 1095). Fourth, the crop choice mechanism: Farmers choose to cultivate cereals only in locations where their productivity advantage over tubers is sufficiently high to compensate for the loss due to taxation or looting. Where productive roots and tubers are available, farmers would prefer to cultivate these less appropriable crops that protect them from bandits and tax collectors. Complex hierarchies emerged when farmers were constrained to cultivating cereals (p. 1098). Fifth, alternative mechanisms consistent with the evidence: The authors acknowledge that other mechanisms related to cereal properties may also contribute to hierarchy formation, including: (a) increasing returns to scale in the construction of storage facilities leading to communal storage under leaders; (b) communal storage and redistribution providing better food security and requiring leadership to organize; (c) the durability and transportability of cereals promoting trade, which increases the return to public goods such as law and order and requires leadership for their provision; and (d) storage of appropriable cereals increasing the return to attacks and making it easier for the winners of a conflict to extract ongoing surplus from the losers (p. 1098).

作者提出了几个相互关联的机制，通过这些机制，可占用谷物的种植影响等级复杂性和国家的出现。第一，储存机制：谷物必须在短时间内收获，然后由于其季节性特点而储存以供全年消费。这创造了在时间和空间上集中的食物储备，使其容易被占用。税收官员可以没收部分储存的谷物并将其运输以供远方精英和其他非食物生产者消费，即使没有食物剩余（第1093页）。第二，均衡剩余机制：即使持续的没收会导致人口缩减，由于劳动的平均产出递减，较小的人口会产生更高的人均产出。这导致一个具有稳定人口的均衡，其中总产出超过农业人口的生存需要，剩余被非农业精英没收。这表明剩余不是税收的先决条件，而是税收的结果（第1093页）。第三，保护机制：储存的谷物不仅可以被潜在的精英占用，也可以被盗匪占用。因此，谷物种植产生了对保护的需求，同时也促进了税收以资助精英提供这种保护（第1094页）。作者的正式模型表明，只有当谷物的生产力显著高于块茎时，国家才能存在，因为这使得税收收入能够覆盖威慑盗匪的固定成本（第1095页）。第四，作物选择机制：农民只有在谷物相对于块茎的生产力优势足够高以补偿因税收或掠夺造成的损失时，才会选择种植谷物。在有高产块根和块茎可用的地方，农民会倾向于种植这些不太容易被占用的作物，以保护自己免受盗匪和税收官员的侵害。复杂的等级制度是在农民被迫种植谷物时出现的（第1098页）。第五，与证据一致的替代机制：作者承认，与谷物特性相关的其他机制也可能促进等级制度的形成，包括：(a) 储存设施建设中的规模报酬递增导致在领导者下的公共储存；(b) 公共储存和再分配提供更好的食物安全保障并需要领导来组织；(c) 谷物的耐久性和可运输性促进贸易，这增加了对法律和秩序等公共产品的回报，并需要领导来提供；以及 (d) 可占用谷物的储存增加了攻击的回报，并使冲突的胜利者更容易从失败者那里提取持续的剩余（第1098页）。

Data & methods

Data

The authors employ multiple data sets spanning several millennia to test their argument, using diverse geographic coverage and units of analysis. First, the Ethnographic Atlas (Murdock 1967) provides a database of 1,267 societies from around the world, containing information on cultural, institutional, and economic features before significant industrialization. After removing duplicates and observations with missing data, 1,248 societies remain. The sample is global with emphasis on North American and African societies. The focal year of the data is pre-1800 for 3% of the societies, the nineteenth century for 25%, the first half of the twentieth century for 69%, and the second half for 2.5% (pp. 1099-1102). The dependent variable (hierarchy) uses the “jurisdictional hierarchy beyond local community” variable with five levels. The independent variable (cereal cultivation) uses information on the major crop type, with cereals comprising 65.4% and roots/tubers 26.1% of agricultural societies. Additional variables from the Standard Cross-Cultural Sample (SCCS) include farming surplus existence and tax burden measures (p. 1102). Second, the Borcan, Olsson, and Putterman (2018) dataset covers 159 modern-day countries with institutional information every half-century from 50 CE to 2000 CE (pp. 1102-1103). Third, ancient cities data comes from two sources: Reba, Reitsma, and Seto (2016), providing urban settlement locations from 3700 BCE to 2000 CE based on Chandler (1987) and Modelski (2003), and DeGroff’s database of cities founded before 400 CE. Archaeological site data from Ancient Locations.net and the Megalithic Portal complement these sources (pp. 1103-1104). Fourth, the Archaeological Atlas of the World (Whitehouse and Whitehouse 1975) provides 4,215 radiocarbon-dated prehistoric and protohistoric sites globally, of which 825 belong to pretransition years and 3,309 to posttransition years (p. 1104). Fifth, soil suitability data from the FAO’s GAEZ project provides global estimates of potential crop yields for 11 cereal grains and 4 roots/tubers at a 5’ × 5’ cell size (approximately 100 km²), based on agroclimatic conditions under rain-fed, low-input agriculture. Yields in tons are converted to calories using USDA data (pp. 1104-1105). Sixth, wild relatives of domesticated crops data from the Global Crop Diversity Trust provides potential geographical distributions of crop wild relatives, with maps constructed using MaxEnt machine-learning algorithms based on occurrence records and environmental variables (pp. 1107-1108). Seventh, centers of domestication data from Larson et al. (2014) identifies 20 centers of independent domestication and the domesticates from each (p. 1109). Geographic data on population density, precipitation, temperature, elevation, and ruggedness come from GAEZ, GDEM, and HYDE databases. Data are aggregated at the society level (20-mile radius around coordinates), country level, or 1° × 1° decimal degree raster level depending on the analysis (pp. 1107, 1109).

作者采用跨越数千年的多个数据集来检验其论点，使用了多样化的地理覆盖范围和分析单位。第一，《民族志地图集》(Murdock, 1967) 提供了来自世界各地1,267个社会的数据库，包含在显著工业化之前的文化、制度和经济特征信息。在删除重复项和缺失数据的观测后，剩余1,248个社会。样本是全球性的，侧重于北美和非洲社会。数据的焦点年份为1800年前的占3%，十九世纪占25%，二十世纪上半叶占69%，下半叶占2.5%（第1099-1102页）。因变量（等级）使用“超越地方社区的管辖等级”变量，包含五个层级。自变量（谷物种植）使用主要作物类型信息，其中谷物占农业社会的65.4%，块根/块茎占26.1%。来自标准跨文化样本 (SCCS) 的额外变量包括农业剩余存在和税收负担测量（第1102页）。第二，博尔坎、奥尔森和普特曼 (2018) 数据集涵盖159个现代国家，从公元50年到2000年每半个世纪提供制度信息（第1102-1103页）。第三，古代城市数据来自两个来源：雷巴、赖茨马和塞托 (Reba, Reitsma, and Seto, 2016)，基于钱德勒 (Chandler, 1987) 和莫德尔斯基 (Modelski, 2003) 提供从公元前3700年到公元2000年的城市聚落位置；以及德格罗夫 (DeGroff) 的公元400年前建立的城市数据库。来自Ancient Locations.net和巨石门户 (Megalithic Portal) 的考古遗址数据补充了这些来源（第1103-1104页）。第四，《世界考古地图集》(Whitehouse and Whitehouse, 1975) 在全球范围内提供了4,215个经放射性碳定年的史前和原史遗址，其中825个属于转型前年份，3,309个属于转型后年份（第1104页）。第五，来自联合国粮农组织 GAEZ 项目的土壤适宜性数据以5’ × 5’网格大小（约100平方公里）为11种谷物和4种块根/块茎提供全球潜在作物产量估计，基于雨养、低投入农业下的农业气候条件。吨数产量使用美国农业部数据转换为热量（第1104-1105页）。第六，来自全球作物多样性信托基金的驯化作物野生近缘种数据提供作物野生近缘种的潜在地理分布，地图使用MaxEnt机器学习算法基于出现记录和环境变量构建（第1107-1108页）。第七，来自拉森等人 (Larson et al., 2014) 的驯化中心数据识别了20个独立驯化中心及各中心的驯化物种（第1109页）。人口密度、降水、温度、海拔和崎岖度的地理数据来自GAEZ、GDEM和HYDE数据库。数据根据分析在社会层面（坐标周围20英里半径）、国家层面或1° × 1°十进制度栅格层面进行汇总（第1107、1109页）。

Methods

The authors employ a diverse array of empirical methods to test their argument, reflecting the recognition that no single perfect randomized controlled trial could prove or disprove their thesis (p. 1095). First, Two-Stage Least Squares (2SLS) regressions are used with the Ethnographic Atlas data to address reverse causality concerns. The choice of crop might be influenced by social institutions, so the authors instrument for cereal cultivation using the productivity advantage of cereals over roots and tubers based on agroclimatic conditions that are presumably orthogonal to human intervention. The first stage regresses the cereal dummy on the productivity advantage, and the second stage regresses hierarchy on the instrumented cereal variable. The F-statistics on the excluded instrument range from 49.3 to 75.9, indicating a strong instrument (pp. 1110-1113). Standard errors are estimated in three ways: clustered at the country level, or adjusted for spatial correlation using Conley (1999) methodology with distance cutoffs of 5 or 10 decimal degrees. Second, reduced-form regressions examine the direct effect of the productivity advantage of cereals on hierarchy, controlling for land productivity to distinguish the appropriability theory from the conventional productivity theory (pp. 1110-1111). Third, Post-Double-Selection (PDS) 2SLS methodology is employed for specifications with many control variables. Square-root LASSO is used to identify relevant controls in predicting the dependent variable, main regressor, and instrument, with the union of selected variables included in the 2SLS estimates (p. 1113). Fourth, panel regressions exploiting the Columbian Exchange provide variation in crop availability over time. The exchange introduced new crops that changed both land productivity and the productivity advantage of cereals in most countries. The regressions include country and time-period fixed effects to control for time-invariant factors and common trends. The critical identification assumption is that there were no unobserved events in the sixteenth century systematically correlated with the spatial variation in the change in cereal advantage that independently affected hierarchy (pp. 1119-1122). Fifth, pretrend tests assess the identifying assumption by regressing hierarchy before the Columbian Exchange on the change in cereal advantage interacted with a linear trend or year fixed effects. The absence of differential pre-1500 trends supports the validity of the panel design (pp. 1122-1123). Sixth, cross-sectional regressions at the raster level examine the relationship between wild relatives of domesticated crops (or distance to domestication centers) and the presence of ancient cities and archaeological sites (pp. 1124-1128). Seventh, difference-in-differences regressions using radiocarbon-dated sites compare the change in the number of post-Neolithic versus pre-Neolithic sites across areas with different crop availability, including cell and period fixed effects (pp. 1127-1130). Key assumptions for these methods to be valid include: (1) the instrument (productivity advantage of cereals) affects hierarchy only through its effect on cereal cultivation; (2) agroclimatic-based productivity measures are not affected by human intervention and thus not endogenous to hierarchy; (3) no time-varying confounders in the panel analysis that correlate with both cereal advantage changes and hierarchy changes; (4) parallel trends assumption for difference-in-differences.

作者采用多种实证方法来检验其论点，这反映出对于没有单一完美的随机对照试验可以证明或反驳其论点的认识（第1095页）。第一，两阶段最小二乘法 (2SLS) 回归用于《民族志地图集》数据以解决反向因果关系问题。作物选择可能受社会制度影响，因此作者使用基于农业气候条件（被认为与人类干预正交）的谷物相对于块根和块茎的生产力优势作为谷物种植的工具变量。第一阶段将谷物虚拟变量回归到生产力优势上，第二阶段将等级回归到经过工具化的谷物变量上。排除工具的F统计量范围从49.3到75.9，表明工具变量很强（第1110-1113页）。标准误差以三种方式估计：在国家层面聚类，或使用康利 (Conley, 1999) 方法以5或10个十进制度的距离截断调整空间相关性。第二，简约形式回归检验谷物生产力优势对等级的直接效应，同时控制土地生产力以区分可占用性理论和传统生产力理论（第1110-1111页）。第三，双重选择后 (PDS) 2SLS 方法用于具有多个控制变量的设定。使用平方根LASSO识别预测因变量、主要回归变量和工具的相关控制变量，将所选变量的并集纳入2SLS估计（第1113页）。第四，利用哥伦布交换的面板回归提供了作物可用性随时间的变化。该交换引入了新作物，改变了大多数国家的土地生产力和谷物的生产力优势。回归包括国家和时期固定效应以控制时间不变因素和共同趋势。关键识别假设是十六世纪没有与谷物优势变化的空间差异系统相关且独立影响等级的未观测事件（第1119-1122页）。第五，预趋势测试通过将哥伦布交换前的等级回归到谷物优势变化与线性趋势或年份固定效应的交互项上来评估识别假设。1500年前没有差异性趋势支持面板设计的有效性（第1122-1123页）。第六，栅格层面的横截面回归检验驯化作物野生近缘种（或到驯化中心的距离）与古代城市和考古遗址存在之间的关系（第1124-1128页）。第七，使用放射性碳定年遗址的双重差分回归比较具有不同作物可用性地区的新石器时代后与新石器时代前遗址数量的变化，包括单元格和时期固定效应（第1127-1130页）。这些方法有效的关键假设包括：(1) 工具变量（谷物生产力优势）仅通过其对谷物种植的影响来影响等级；(2) 基于农业气候的生产力测量不受人类干预影响，因此不内生于等级；(3) 面板分析中没有与谷物优势变化和等级变化都相关的时变混淆因素；(4) 双重差分的平行趋势假设。

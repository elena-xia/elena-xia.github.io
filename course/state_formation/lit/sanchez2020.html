Dependent variable (DV)

The paper has multiple dependent variables organized along three dimensions. The first dimension is the “extensive margin” of the essential functions of a state, which captures whether armed actors establish state-like institutions at a given location in a given year. The specific extensive-margin DVs include: (a) whether the municipality was attacked by any armed actor; (b) whether an armed actor imposed customs taxation on mining output at the exit of a mine; (c) whether an armed actor imposed entry fees (working permits/visas) to work at the mine; (d) whether an armed actor established a monopoly of violence (i.e., became a “stationary bandit”) at the mine or support village; (e) whether an armed actor provided a security service at the mine or support village; and (f) a normalized mean index combining these outcomes. All of these are coded as binary dummies (0 or 1) for each municipality–year observation. (pp. 48–52, Table 1)

The second dimension is household welfare, measured at support villages. Welfare proxies include: (a) a savings/investment index constructed via principal component analysis of yearly cattle acquisitions; (b) the log of yearly weddings celebrated in the village (a costly, prestigious investment in a bride-price society); (c) the log of yearly immigration into the village (reflecting the relative desirability of living there); and (d) a composite welfare index combining the above via principal component analysis. (pp. 57–58)

The third dimension is the “intensive margin” of the essential functions of a state, which captures the sophistication of taxation and administrative capacity. Specific intensive-margin DVs include: (a) whether a poll tax exists; (b) whether a market (food market) tax exists; (c) whether a toll booth exists at the village; (d) whether a mill tax exists; (e) a tax sophistication index (a weighted average of taxes, where the weight reflects how strongly each tax is associated with fiscal administration); (f) whether a fiscal administration exists at the village; (g) whether a judicial administration exists at the village; and (h) an overall intensive-margin index combining the above.

Independent variable (IV)

The main independent variables are exogenous shocks to the world prices of two minerals extracted in eastern Congo /ˈkɒŋɡoʊ/: coltan (columbite-tantalite) and gold. Specifically, the paper uses the interaction between the logarithm of the US price of coltan (or gold) in year t and a dummy indicating whether municipality j is endowed with coltan (or gold). The key variation is thus a difference-in-differences design: mineral-endowed locations are “treated” by the price shocks, while locations without those minerals serve as comparisons. (pp. 48–49)

The coltan price shock was triggered in early 2000 when Sony /ˈsoʊni/ announced the Playstation II /ˈpleɪsteɪʃən/, which used coltan as a key input. The US price surged from $90 to $590 per kilogram before collapsing during Christmas 2000 when the console underperformed. The gold price shock came later, driven by investors rushing to gold as a safe haven after the global recession, with the price rising sharply especially after 2006, reaching levels up to 6 times pre-shock levels by 2012. (pp. 34, 44, Figure 1)

For the welfare analysis, additional key IVs include the type of stationary bandit present (militia, external armed actor, or FARDC /ɛf eɪ ɑːr diː siː/, the Congolese national army), and an instrumental variable based on the 2003 Sun City /sʌn ˈsɪti/ peace agreement. (pp. 58, 61–66)

Stateness

The paper conceptualizes the state following the Weberian and Tillyan tradition. Following Weber /ˈveɪbər/ (1946), a state is defined by its “monopoly of violence.” Following Tilly /ˈtɪli/ (1985, 1990), the “essential functions of a state” are: (1) war making, eliminating or neutralizing rivals outside the claimed territory; (2) state making, eliminating or neutralizing rivals inside the territory; (3) protection, eliminating or neutralizing enemies of the ruler’s clients (i.e., protecting the property of those taxed); and (4) extraction, acquiring the means to carry out the first three activities, i.e., taxation. (pp. 33, 38–39)

The paper does not treat the state as a binary concept or as a product of some grand social contract. Instead, it views state formation as a continuous, never-complete process that emerges from the activities of armed actors seeking to extract revenue. The paper draws on Olson’s /ˈɒlsən/ (1993) concept of the “stationary bandit”: a roving bandit who, rather than looting and moving on, decides to settle in a territory, establish a monopoly of violence, tax the population in a predictable way, and, because the bandit is now a residual claimant on the local economy, provide protection. Thus, the emergence of the state is not separate from organized crime; rather, states are the most successful organized crime operations that acquired legitimacy over time. Tilly (1985) famously put it: “War makes states.” (pp. 33–34, 38–42)

The paper further distinguishes between the “extensive margin” and the “intensive margin” of state formation. The extensive margin refers to whether state-like functions exist at all (i.e., whether a monopoly of violence, taxation, and protection emerge). The intensive margin refers to how sophisticated these functions become, whether the ruler develops more complex taxes (poll taxes, consumption taxes), creates fiscal and judicial administrations, and generally builds “fiscal capacity” in the sense of Besley and Persson /ˈbɛzli ænd ˈpɛrsən/ (2009). The historical parallel is drawn to the “maturing” of early states described by Ardant /arˈdɑ̃/ (1975) and Claessen and Skalnik /ˈklɑːsən ænd ˈskɑːlnɪk/ (1978). (pp. 40–42)

Measurement is done through a yearly panel dataset covering 650 locations (239 support villages and 411 mining sites) in eastern DRC from 1995 to 2013, reconstructed via recall-based surveys. The “state” is measured by the binary indicators listed above in the DV section: whether a stationary bandit exists, whether customs or other taxes are collected, whether security is provided, and whether fiscal and judicial administrations operate. (pp. 34, 44–48)

本文遵循韦伯和蒂利传统来概念化国家。遵循Weber /ˈveɪbər/（韦伯，1946），国家由其”暴力垄断”来定义。遵循Tilly /ˈtɪli/（蒂利，1985, 1990），”国家的基本功能”包括：(1) 战争制造，消除或中和领土外的竞争对手；(2) 国家制造，消除或中和领土内的竞争对手；(3) 保护，消除或中和统治者客户的敌人（即保护被征税者的财产）；(4) 攫取，获得执行前三项活动的手段，即征税。（第33、38–39页）

关键的是，本文不将国家视为二元概念或某种伟大社会契约的产物。相反，它将国家形成视为一个持续的、永远不会完成的过程，源于武装行为者寻求攫取收入的活动。论文借鉴了Olson /ˈɒlsən/（奥尔森，1993）的”固定匪帮”概念：一个流动匪帮决定不再掠夺后转移，而是定居在一个领地内，建立暴力垄断，以可预测的方式向人口征税，并且，因为匪帮现在是当地经济的剩余索取者，提供保护。因此，国家的出现并非独立于有组织犯罪；相反，国家就是那些最成功的、随时间获得了合法性的有组织犯罪行动。蒂利（1985）的名言是：”战争制造国家。”（第33–34、38–42页）

论文进一步区分了国家形成的”广延边际”和”集约边际”。广延边际指的是类国家功能是否存在（即暴力垄断、税收和保护是否出现）。集约边际指的是这些功能变得多么复杂，统治者是否发展出更复杂的税收（人头税、消费税），是否创建财政和司法管理机构，以及是否总体上建立了

Besley and Persson /ˈbɛzli ænd ˈpɛrsən/（贝斯利和佩尔松，2009）意义上的”财政能力”。历史类比来自Ardant /arˈdɑ̃/（阿尔当，1975）和Claessen and Skalnik /ˈklɑːsən ænd ˈskɑːlnɪk/（克莱森和斯卡尔尼克，1978）所描述的早期国家的”成熟化”。（第40–42页）

衡量方式是通过一个年度面板数据集，覆盖刚果（金）东部650个地点（239个支持村庄和411个矿场），时间跨度为1995年至2013年，基于回忆式调查重建。”国家”通过上述因变量部分列出的二元指标来衡量：是否存在固定匪帮，是否征收海关税或其他税，是否提供安全服务，以及是否运作财政和司法管理机构。（第34、44–48页）

Core argument

The core argument is that the essential functions of a state, monopoly of violence, taxation, and protection, can emerge endogenously from the revenue-maximizing behavior of armed actors, rather than from any social contract or deliberate state-building project. When the value of extractable output rises and that output can be observed, armed actors have stronger incentives to settle as stationary bandits, establish customs taxation, and provide protection at production sites. When the output is hard to observe (concealable), armed actors still have incentives to capture the surplus, but they must do so indirectly, by settling in the consumption economies (support villages) where miners spend their income, introducing more sophisticated taxes (working permits, poll taxes, market taxes), and building fiscal and judicial administrations to support collection. (pp. 34–35, 42–43)

The paper further argues that the welfare effect of having a stationary bandit depends on the armed actor’s objectives. When the stationary bandit’s mission encompasses the population’s well-being, as with popular militias or the FARDC (despite its corruption), the net effect on household welfare is positive. This is because the protection these actors provide, and the order they establish, outweighs the costs of their taxation. In contrast, when the stationary bandit is an external armed actor with no ties to the local population, the welfare effect is negligible or negative. (pp. 35, 57–66)

Finally, the paper identifies a novel “administration resource curse”: when output can be directly taxed (as with coltan), a resource boom leads stationary bandits to focus on easy-to-collect customs taxes and to dismantle their more sophisticated fiscal and judicial administrations in support villages. The opportunity cost of maintaining an administration rises when customs revenue is abundant. In contrast, when output is concealable (as with gold), a resource boom forces stationary bandits to invest in more sophisticated taxation and fiscal capacity. (pp. 35–36, 64–68)

核心论点是，国家的基本功能，暴力垄断、税收和保护，可以从武装行为者追求收入最大化的行为中内生地产生，而非来自任何社会契约或刻意的国家建设项目。当可提取产出的价值上升且该产出可以被观察到时，武装行为者有更强的激励定居为固定匪帮，建立海关税收，并在生产地点提供保护。当产出难以观察（可隐匿）时，武装行为者仍有激励攫取剩余，但必须间接地，通过定居在矿工花费收入的消费经济体（支持村庄），引入更复杂的税收（工作许可、人头税、市场税），并建设财政和司法管理机构来支持征收。（第34–35、42–43页）

论文进一步论证，固定匪帮对福利的影响取决于武装行为者的目标。当固定匪帮的使命涵盖人口福祉，如民众民兵或FARDC（尽管存在腐败），对家庭福利的净效应是正面的。这是因为这些行为者提供的保护和建立的秩序超过了其税收的成本。相反，当固定匪帮是与当地人口无联系的外部武装行为者时，福利效应可以忽略或为负。（第35、57–66页）

最后，论文识别了一种新型的”行政资源诅咒”：当产出可以直接征税时（如钶钽铁矿），资源繁荣导致固定匪帮专注于易征收的海关税，并拆除其在支持村庄的更复杂的财政和司法管理机构。当海关收入充裕时，维持行政机构的机会成本上升。相反，当产出可隐匿时（如黄金），资源繁荣迫使固定匪帮投资于更复杂的税收和财政能力。（第35–36、64–68页）

Mechanisms

8.1 Observable vs. Concealable Output

The central mechanism hinges on the observability of mining output. Coltan is bulky, a miner can produce up to 50 kg per day, and it must be transported in 75 kg bags by foot from the mine to the support village. This makes coltan output highly observable and easy to tax through customs (roadblocks at the mine exit). Gold, by contrast, has an extremely high value-to-weight ratio, daily production per worker is between 0.1 and 10 grams. Miners routinely conceal gold by swallowing it. Gold is thus called the “immaterial mineral.” A customs tax on gold would face systematic evasion and is thus rarely observed. (pp. 44, 50)

When the coltan price rises, the returns to establishing a roadblock and customs taxation at coltan mines increase. Armed actors thus create monopolies of violence and provide protection at coltan mines, the “essential functions of a state” emerge at the production site. When the gold price rises, armed actors cannot tax gold output directly. Instead, they settle in the support villages where miners’ families live and where income from gold is spent. There, they introduce working permits (a tax on mining labor rather than output), poll taxes, and market taxes. Because these taxes are harder to collect, armed actors also invest in fiscal and judicial administrations. (pp. 34–35, 50–52)

8.2 Trade Infrastructure

The coltan effect is concentrated near airstrips, the infrastructure through which coltan is exported, given the DRC’s poor road network. This confirms that the mechanism operates through the ability to tax and ship observable output. The gold effect, by contrast, is equally strong whether or not the village is near an airstrip, consistent with gold’s concealability making physical trade infrastructure irrelevant for the armed actors’ taxation strategy. (p. 54)

8.3 Type of Armed Actor and Welfare

The paper identifies four types of armed actors: (1) village self-defense militias, formed by villagers themselves; (2) regional militias, which originated as village militias but expanded; (3) external armed actors, representing foreign ethnic groups; and (4) the FARDC, the Congolese national army, which is infiltrated by organized crime. Village militias and the FARDC are seen as more legitimate (29% and 70% of episodes, respectively) and are more coethnic with the local population. External armed actors are seen as least legitimate (17%) and commit the most violent attacks (72% of their attacks include pillage, 59% sexual violence, 61% death). (pp. 54–57, Table 2)

The welfare mechanism works as follows: when the stationary bandit’s objective function places weight on the population’s welfare (as with militias and the FARDC), the protection they provide and the order they establish create a net positive effect. The value added through protection of property rights exceeds the value destroyed through tax distortions. When the stationary bandit cares only about extraction (external actors), the surplus is expropriated away and household welfare does not improve. (pp. 35, 42, 57–66)

8.4 Administration Resource Curse

When coltan prices rise, stationary bandits at coltan support villages dismantle their fiscal and judicial administrations. The logic is one of opportunity costs: customs taxation of coltan output is easy and profitable, so armed actors reallocate their limited organizational resources toward customs at mines and away from more sophisticated taxation in villages. Conversely, when gold prices rise, stationary bandits cannot rely on customs taxation and must invest in fiscal capacity to capture the surplus, introducing poll taxes, market taxes, and building administrative capacity. This mechanism is confirmed even after controlling for changes in the composition of stationary bandits (organization fixed effects). (pp. 35–36, 64–68, Table 5)

可观察产出 vs. 可隐匿产出

核心机制取决于矿产产出的可观察性。钶钽铁矿体积大，一个矿工每天可生产多达50公斤，必须以75公斤的袋装从矿区步行运到支持村庄。这使得钶钽铁矿产出高度可观察，容易通过海关（在矿区出口设路障）征税。相比之下，黄金的价值重量比极高，每个工人的日产量在0.1到10克之间。矿工常通过吞咽来隐藏黄金。因此黄金被称为”非物质矿物”。对黄金的海关税将面临系统性逃税，因此很少被观察到。（第44、50页）

当钶钽铁矿价格上升时，在钶钽铁矿矿区建立路障和海关税收的回报增加。武装行为者因此在钶钽铁矿矿区建立暴力垄断并提供保护，国家的”基本功能”在生产地点出现。当黄金价格上升时，武装行为者无法直接对黄金产出征税。相反，他们定居在矿工家庭居住和矿工花费收入的支持村庄。在那里，他们引入工作许可（对采矿劳动而非产出征税）、人头税和市场税。由于这些税更难征收，武装行为者还投资于财政和司法管理机构。（第34–35、50–52页）

贸易基础设施

钶钽铁矿效应集中在靠近简易机场的地区，鉴于刚果（金）糟糕的公路网络，这是钶钽铁矿出口的基础设施。这证实了该机制通过对可观察产出征税和运输的能力来运作。相比之下，黄金效应在靠近和远离机场的村庄同样强烈，这与黄金的可隐匿性使实体贸易基础设施对武装行为者的征税策略无关紧要的判断一致。（第54页）

武装行为者类型与福利

论文识别了四类武装行为者：(1) 村庄自卫民兵，由村民自己组建；(2) 地区民兵，源于村庄民兵但规模扩大；(3) 外部武装行为者，代表外国族群利益；(4) FARDC，即刚果国民军，被有组织犯罪渗透。村庄民兵和FARDC被认为更合法（分别在29%和70%的事件中），且与当地人口更多同族。外部武装行为者被认为最不合法（17%），并犯下最暴力的攻击（72%的攻击包括抢劫，59%包括性暴力，61%包括死亡）。（第54–57页，表2）

福利机制如下运作：当固定匪帮的目标函数赋予人口福祉以权重时（如民兵和FARDC），他们提供的保护和建立的秩序产生净正效应。通过保护产权创造的附加值超过了税收扭曲破坏的价值。当固定匪帮只关心攫取时（外部行为者），剩余被没收，家庭福利不会改善。（第35、42、57–66页）

行政资源诅咒

当钶钽铁矿价格上升时，钶钽铁矿支持村庄的固定匪帮拆除其财政和司法管理机构。其逻辑在于机会成本：钶钽铁矿产出的海关税收既容易又有利可图，因此武装行为者将其有限的组织资源从村庄的复杂税收重新分配到矿区的海关。相反，当黄金价格上升时，固定匪帮不能依赖海关税收，必须投资于财政能力以攫取剩余，引入人头税、市场税，并建设行政能力。即使在控制了固定匪帮组成变化（组织固定效应）之后，这一机制仍得到证实。（第35–36、64–68页，表5）

Data & methods

Data

9.1 Geographic Coverage and Unit of Analysis

The study covers the eastern Democratic Republic of the Congo /ˌdɛməˈkrætɪk rɪˈpʌblɪk əv ðə ˈkɒŋɡoʊ/ (DRC), specifically the provinces of Sud Kivu /suːd ˈkiːvuː/ and Nord Kivu /nɔːr ˈkiːvuː/. The sample consists of 239 support villages and their corresponding 411 mining sites, which together form 239 municipalities. The unit of analysis is the municipality–year (for mine-level and village-level analyses) and the household–year (for welfare analyses). (pp. 44–47, Figure 2)

9.2 Time Period

The panel covers 1995 to 2013. Data collection took place from July 2012 through June 2013 in Sud Kivu and from February through October 2015 in Nord Kivu. (p. 46)

9.3 Data Sources and Collection

The dataset was reconstructed through recall-based surveys. In each support village, a team of 10 surveyors trained local “history specialists”, individuals with long involvement in mining or village elders, to consolidate a municipality history over one week. Simultaneously, surveyors administered private interviews in 6–8 randomly selected households per village. At the end of the week, surveyors held a day-long meeting with the history specialists to cross-check data. The data from this meeting constitute the main source. The author acknowledges three challenges of recall data: measurement error from imperfect recall of magnitudes, measurement error from imperfect recall of dates, and reporting bias for sensitive information. Three months of piloting were used to develop protocols tailored to the cultural context. (pp. 44–46)

9.4 Measurement of Key Variables

Mineral prices: US prices of coltan and gold are taken from the US Geological Survey (2016). Local DRC prices are from the recall survey but are acknowledged to be “extremely unreliable” (which motivates the use of US prices in the reduced-form specification). Mineral endowment dummies are constructed from existing datasets (International Peace Information Service 2009; Référentiel Géographique Commun 2010) supplemented by the author’s own prospections. The armed actor classification (village militia, regional militia, external, FARDC) is based on fieldwork conversations with active officers and combatants of numerous armed groups between 2010 and 2018. (pp. 44–49, Table 2)

For the welfare analysis, only households settled in the support village before 2000 are included to avoid compositional effects from migration. Household-year data are aggregated to support village–year means. An additional implicit association test (IAT) was administered to approximately 10 households in 106 Nord Kivu support villages to measure biases toward different armed groups. (pp. 57–58, 67)

Methods

10.1 Main Specification: Reduced-Form Difference-in-Differences

The primary empirical strategy is a reduced-form linear probability model estimated via OLS:

\[ Y_{jt}=\beta_t+\alpha_j+\gamma_c C_j p^{US}_{c,t} +\gamma_g G_j p^{US}_{g,t}+\varepsilon_{jt}. \]

Here \(Y_{jt}\) is a binary outcome in municipality \(j\) in year \(t\), \(\alpha_j\) and \(\beta_t\) are municipality and year fixed effects, \(C_j\) (\(G_j\)) is a dummy for coltan (gold) endowment, and \(p^{US}_{c,t}\) (\(p^{US}_{g,t}\)) is the log of the US price of coltan (gold). The coefficients \(\gamma_c\) and \(\gamma_g\) indicate how a 1 percentage point increase in the US mineral price differentially affects outcomes in mineral-endowed locations relative to non-endowed locations. Standard errors are clustered at the municipality level. (pp. 48–49)

10.2 Key Identifying Assumptions

For this strategy to identify causal effects, several assumptions must hold. First, parallel trends: absent the price shocks, outcomes in mineral-endowed and non-endowed locations would have evolved similarly. The author provides evidence that pre-shock trends are parallel and that including leads does not affect the contemporaneous coefficient. Second, exogeneity of US prices: the US price of coltan and gold must be unaffected by the behavior of armed actors in the DRC. For gold, the DRC’s share of global output is negligible (0.08%), making this plausible. For coltan, the DRC’s share is larger (up to 20% by 2010), but the year-2000 price spike was driven by speculative demand for Playstation II and the failure of Australian supply, not by DRC conditions. The author also shows that restricting the coltan analysis to 1999–2001 (the only credibly exogenous interval) leaves results unchanged. Third, the price shocks must not be correlated with other unobserved shocks differentially affecting mineral vs. non-mineral locations. (pp. 48–49, 54)

10.3 Instrumental Variables for Welfare Analysis

To estimate the causal effect of different types of stationary bandits on household welfare, the author uses the 2003 Sun City peace agreement as an instrument. The agreement led the RCD /ɑːr siː diː/ (the main external armed actor) and some regional militias to voluntarily withdraw from their territories. The IV strategy is a 2SLS using \(POST_t\times SB^{2002}_j\) as an instrument for whether a stationary bandit is present, where \(POST_t\) indicates years after 2003 and \(SB^{2002}_j\) indicates whether a location had a stationary bandit in 2002. The exclusion restriction requires that the peace agreement affected household welfare only through the withdrawal (or retention) of stationary bandits, not through other channels. The first-stage F-statistic is above 20. (pp. 61–66, Table 4)

10.4 Additional Methods

For the intensive-margin analysis, the author includes stationary-bandit organization fixed effects to separate changes in armed actors’ behavior from changes in the composition of stationary bandits. The author also reports 13 robustness checks including 2SLS using local prices, Arellano-Bond estimation, alternative year intervals, leads and lags, controls for time-varying village characteristics, and exclusion/inclusion of the FARDC. (pp. 49, 64–68)

其中 \(Y_{jt}\) 是市镇 \(j\) 在 \(t\) 年的二元结果变量，\(\alpha_j\) 和 \(\beta_t\) 分别是市镇和年份固定效应，\(C_j\)（\(G_j\)）是钶钽铁矿（黄金）禀赋虚拟变量，\(p^{US}_{c,t}\)（\(p^{US}_{g,t}\)）是钶钽铁矿（黄金）美国价格的对数。系数 \(\gamma_c\) 和 \(\gamma_g\) 表示美国矿物价格每增加1个百分点，对矿产蕴藏地相对于非蕴藏地的差异影响。标准误在市镇层面聚类。（第48–49页）

关键识别假设

要使该策略识别因果效应，必须满足几个假设。第一，平行趋势：在没有价格冲击的情况下，矿产蕴藏地和非蕴藏地的结果变量将以类似方式演变。作者提供了冲击前趋势平行的证据，并表明包含前导项不影响当期系数。第二，美国价格的外生性：钶钽铁矿和黄金的美国价格不受刚果（金）武装行为者行为的影响。对于黄金，刚果（金）的全球产出份额微不足道（0.08%），使这一假设合理。对于钶钽铁矿，刚果（金）的份额较大（到2010年高达20%），但2000年的价格飙升由PlayStation II的投机需求和澳大利亚供应失败驱动，而非刚果（金）条件。作者还表明，将钶钽铁矿分析限制在1999–2001年（唯一可信外生的区间）不改变结果。第三，价格冲击不得与差异性影响矿产与非矿产地区的其他不可观察冲击相关。（第48–49、54页）

Shared week context: the blackboard principal-agent model & cross-reading synthesis

Lecture framing: the Feb. 26 class used a principal-agent toy model to organize the week around legibility, monitoring, and centralization. The ruler chooses an institutional form, indirect rule/tax farming (\(I\)) or direct rule/state administration (\(D\)); the agent chooses effort \(e\in\{0,1\}\); and effort affects the probability of high tax revenue \(R\in\{L,H\}\).

\[ \Pr(R=H\mid e=1)=p\in(0,1),\qquad \Pr(R=H\mid e=0)=0. \]

The key monitoring parameter is:

\[ q=\Pr(e=0\mid R=L),\qquad q\in\left[\frac{1}{2},1\right]. \]

Indirect rule/tax farming works through a share contract. The agent's incentive compatibility constraint and the ruler's optimal share are:

\[ pSH\ge k,\qquad S_I^*=\frac{k}{pH}. \]

The ruler's profit under indirect rule is independent of monitoring quality:

\[ \pi_I=p(1-S_I^*)H=pH-k. \]

Direct rule/state administration adds the threat of dismissal. If the agent is retained, the position has continuation value \(V>0\). The incentive constraint, optimal share, and profit are:

\[ pSH+qV\ge k,\qquad S_D^*=\max\left\{0,\frac{k-qV}{pH}\right\}. \]
\[ \pi_D=\begin{cases} pH, & qV>k,\\ pH-k+qV, & qV\le k. \end{cases} \]

The institutional switch from \(I\) to \(D\) requires paying a transition cost \(C\). Direct rule is chosen when:

\[ \pi_D-C\ge\pi_I\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad qV\ge C\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad q^*=\frac{C}{V}. \]

The core takeaway is that tax farming is the fallback institution when monitoring is weak, while direct administration becomes attractive once monitoring and dismissal can discipline agents enough to justify the transition cost.

从 \(I\) 转向 \(D\) 需要支付制度转换成本 \(C\)。直接统治被选择当且仅当：

\[ \pi_D-C\ge\pi_I\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad qV\ge C\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad q^*=\frac{C}{V}. \]

核心结论：监控弱时，包税制是兜底制度；当监控与解雇威胁足以约束代理人并覆盖转换成本时，科层行政才变得有吸引力。

文献在模型中的角色核心被解释变量\(q\) 的状态
Kiser (1994)合约选择：包税制 vs. 国家行政征收。税收征集的制度形式。基本外生；税种、国家规模、统治者自主性、控制技术影响监控。
Sng (2014)规模扩张稀释监控能力。清朝财政能力衰退。在关键模型中对统治者近似外生；距离和规模降低有效监控。
Stasavage (2020)官僚技术提高后，治理从早期民主转向等级制。早期民主 vs. 等级统治。开始内生化 \(q\)：文字、测绘、官僚制提高可读性。
Sánchez de la Sierra (2020)以矿物可见度作为可征税性的经验代理。武装团体的征税形式。对武装行为者外生，由商品物理特征决定。

本周的渐进逻辑是：从 Kiser 的狭窄合约选择问题出发，依次加入叛乱约束（Sng）、可读性的内生化（Stasavage），以及具体机制的因果识别（Sánchez de la Sierra）。

Leticia Arroyo Abad & Noel Maurer (2021), “History Never Really Says Goodbye: A Critical Review of the Persistence Literature,” JHPE 1.阿罗约·阿巴德、毛勒（2021）《历史从未真正说再见：持续性文献的批判性综述》，JHPE。

Review / critique▼ traces to the present

Dependent variable (DV)

This paper is a methodological review article rather than an empirical study with its own dependent variable. However, it examines how persistence studies generally employ contemporary economic and political outcomes as dependent variables. The authors note that persistence studies typically regress “a current day outcome on a historical variable” (p. 32). Common dependent variables in the reviewed literature include: GDP per capita, income levels, educational attainment, political attitudes, voting behavior, trust levels, provision of public goods, and measures of inequality. The authors distinguish between “apples to oranges” studies that use economic variables on the left-hand side and “apples to apples” studies that use non-economic outcomes such as political or cultural variables (p. 34).

本文是一篇方法论综述文章，而非具有独立因变量的实证研究。然而，它考察了持续性研究通常如何将当代经济和政治结果作为因变量。作者指出，持续性研究通常“将当前的结果变量对历史变量进行回归”（第32页）。被综述文献中常见的因变量包括：人均国内生产总值、收入水平、教育程度、政治态度、投票行为、信任水平、公共产品供给以及不平等程度的衡量指标。作者区分了“苹果对橘子”研究（以经济变量作为因变量）和“苹果对苹果”研究（以非经济结果如政治或文化变量作为因变量）（第34页）。

Independent variable (IV)

As a review article, this paper does not propose its own independent variable. Instead, it surveys the historical “treatment” variables used in persistence studies. The authors identify that persistence studies search for “deep historical roots of current phenomena” (p. 32). Key independent variables discussed in the reviewed literature include: colonial institutions and origins (Acemoglu et al. 2001; Engerman and Sokoloff 1997), the slave trade (Nunn 2008), precolonial ethnic distributions (Murdock 1959), pre-Columbian population density, genetic diversity (Ashraf and Galor 2013), technology adoption in ancient times (Comin et al. 2010), and various colonial-era policies such as the mita forced labor system in Peru (Dell 2010) or landlord systems in British India (Banerjee and Iyer 2005). The authors note that some studies trace treatments back millennia, asking questions like “Was the Wealth of Nations Determined in 1000 B.C.?” (p. 44).

作为一篇综述文章，本文并未提出自己的自变量。相反，它调查了持续性研究中使用的历史“处理”变量。作者指出，持续性研究寻求“当前现象的深层历史根源”（第32页）。被综述文献中讨论的关键自变量包括：殖民制度与起源（阿西莫格鲁等人2001年；恩格曼与索科洛夫1997年）、奴隶贸易（努恩2008年）、前殖民时期族群分布（默多克1959年）、前哥伦布时期人口密度、遗传多样性（阿什拉夫与加洛尔2013年）、古代技术采用（科明等人2010年），以及各种殖民时期政策，如秘鲁的米塔（mita）强制劳动制度（戴尔2010年）或英属印度的地主制度（班纳吉与艾耶尔2005年）。作者指出，一些研究将处理效应追溯到数千年前，提出诸如“国富是否在公元前1000年就已注定？”这样的问题（第44页）。

Stateness

While this review article does not focus specifically on state formation theory, it engages with several dimensions of how states and state-building are treated in persistence studies. The authors discuss how institutional persistence relates to state capacity and governance. They note that social science fundamentally “assumes the persistence of human institutions” because “rules that can change suddenly and randomly are no rules at all” (p. 33). A constitution, for instance, “cannot shape political behavior if politicians can change the procedures that govern lawmaking at their whim” (p. 33). The paper references Douglass North’s framework explaining how institutions might change slowly over time (p. 33).

Several state-related concepts appear throughout the review. First, colonial state formation is central to many persistence studies, particularly the distinction between “extractive” and “inclusive” institutions created by European colonizers (Acemoglu et al. 2001, discussed on p. 34). The authors examine how the factor endowments of different colonies shaped institutions governing “property rights and market transactions” (p. 33). Second, the paper discusses precolonial state structures, noting debates about whether the slave trade destabilized precolonial African states (p. 54) and Igor Kopytoff’s /ˈkɒpɪtɒf/ criticism of Murdock’s /ˈmɜːrdɒk/ ethnic mapping (pp. 39-40). Third, post-colonial state capacity is addressed through studies examining how different colonial legacies affected later state development, such as Miguel’s (2004) comparison of nation-building policies in Kenya /ˈkenjə/ versus Tanzania /ˌtænzəˈniːə/ (p. 37) and Backhaus’s (2019) study of educational convergence in Poland /ˈpoʊlənd/ after the partition era (p. 37).

The authors also discuss mechanisms through which state capacity persists or changes. Suryanarayan and White’s study shows how white elites in the American South “deliberately weakened the institutions that enabled efficient tax collection” to prevent redistribution (p. 49). Guardado’s (2018) study examines whether corrupt colonial officials, measured by the prices the Spanish imperial government offered for governorships, had long-term consequences for governance (pp. 56-57). The paper thus treats states not as static entities but as evolving institutional configurations shaped by historical contingencies and critical junctures.

虽然这篇综述文章并未专门聚焦于国家形成理论，但它涉及持续性研究中如何处理国家和国家建构的若干维度。作者讨论了制度持续性如何与国家能力和治理相关联。他们指出，社会科学从根本上“假定人类制度的持续性”，因为“能够突然和随机改变的规则根本不是规则”（第33页）。例如，宪法“如果政客能够随心所欲地改变立法程序，就无法塑造政治行为”（第33页）。文章引用了道格拉斯·诺斯解释制度如何随时间缓慢变化的框架（第33页）。

整篇综述中出现了若干与国家相关的概念。首先，殖民国家形成是许多持续性研究的核心，特别是欧洲殖民者创建的“榨取性”与“包容性”制度之间的区分（阿西莫格鲁等人2001年，在第34页讨论）。作者考察了不同殖民地的要素禀赋如何塑造了管理“产权和市场交易”的制度（第33页）。其次，文章讨论了前殖民时期的国家结构，指出关于奴隶贸易是否破坏了前殖民时期非洲国家稳定性的争论（第54页），以及伊戈尔·科皮托夫（Igor Kopytoff /ˈkɒpɪtɒf/）对默多克（Murdock /ˈmɜːrdɒk/）族群地图的批评（第39-40页）。第三，后殖民国家能力通过研究不同殖民遗产如何影响后来国家发展的文献得到讨论，例如米格尔（2004年）对肯尼亚（Kenya /ˈkenjə/）与坦桑尼亚（Tanzania /ˌtænzəˈniːə/）国家建设政策的比较（第37页），以及巴克豪斯（2019年）关于波兰（Poland /ˈpoʊlənd/）在分治时期后教育趋同的研究（第37页）。

作者还讨论了国家能力持续或变化的机制。苏里亚纳拉亚南与怀特的研究表明，美国南方的白人精英“故意削弱了使有效税收征管成为可能的制度”以防止再分配（第49页）。瓜尔达多（2018年）的研究考察了腐败的殖民官员，以西班牙帝国政府为各总督职位开出的价格来衡量，是否对治理产生了长期后果（第56-57页）。因此，本文将国家视为受历史偶然性和关键节点塑造的演变中的制度配置，而非静态实体。

Core argument

The core argument of this review is that while persistence studies have produced valuable insights and brought history into mainstream social science, the literature has “overcorrected for past oversights” (p. 32). The authors contend that claims about deep historical roots of modern phenomena “should be met with healthy skepticism” (p. 32). They quote Karl Marx’s observation that “all that is solid melts into air” to emphasize how rapidly social attitudes, political institutions, economic outcomes, and human geography can change within a single lifetime (p. 32).

The authors advance several interconnected arguments. First, they argue that many persistence papers are paradoxically also “anti-persistence” papers, demonstrating that interventions can successfully change institutions contradicts the notion of immutable historical determinism (pp. 35-38). For example, Alesina and Fuchs-Schündeln’s (2007) study showing that Communist rule changed German preferences demonstrates that “interventions can change cultural attitudes as little as four decades” (p. 36).

Second, the authors argue that the persistence literature suffers from publication bias. The “lack of clear historically-grounded mechanisms, combined with publication bias, opens the possibility that the literature is leading us to believe that there is more persistence than actually exists” (p. 35). Without clearly-specified mechanisms, “the scales will be weighted even further towards misidentifying spurious correlations and overstating the actual degree of persistence” (p. 35).

Third, they argue that persistence studies often rely on problematic historical data. Using the examples of Murdock’s African ethnic boundaries (described at the time as “a special menace,” “dogmatic,” and “factually wrong” by contemporary scholars, p. 40) and pre-Columbian population estimates for Latin America (pp. 40-43), they show that “historical datasets will be found to have errors and will be superseded” (p. 38).

Fourth, they argue that many persistence studies suffer from “the compression of history”, regressing historical treatments on contemporary outcomes without checking intermediate periods (pp. 50-56). They demonstrate this by replicating Nunn’s (2008) slave trade analysis across multiple years and finding that “the relationship between slave exports and GDP per capita grew stronger after independence” rather than being constant over time (p. 54).

Fifth, they argue that spatial autocorrelation threatens many persistence findings. Geographic proximity creates similarities that can be mistaken for historical causation, leading to “inflated coefficients and understated standard errors” (p. 60).

The overarching argument is captured in the conclusion: “The past matters but it is not a prison. It should come as no surprise that institutions and historical shocks persist over time. But institutions persist, change, and adapt. Societies can recover from institutional trauma and ‘bad’ institutions can be reformed” (p. 60).

本综述的核心论点是，虽然持续性研究产生了有价值的见解并将历史带入了主流社会科学，但这一文献“对过去的疏忽进行了过度纠正”（第32页）。作者认为，关于现代现象深层历史根源的主张“应当以健康的怀疑态度来对待”（第32页）。他们引用卡尔·马克思“一切坚固的东西都烟消云散了”的观察，强调社会态度、政治制度、经济结果和人文地理可以在一代人的时间内迅速改变（第32页）。

作者提出了若干相互关联的论点。首先，他们认为许多持续性论文悖论性地也是“反持续性”论文，证明干预可以成功改变制度，这与不可改变的历史决定论观念相矛盾（第35-38页）。例如，阿莱西纳与富克斯-舒恩德尔恩（2007年）的研究表明共产主义统治改变了德国人的偏好，证明“干预可以在短短四十年内改变文化态度”（第36页）。

其次，作者认为持续性文献受到发表偏倚的影响。“缺乏清晰的、基于历史的机制，加上发表偏倚，使得文献可能让我们相信存在比实际更多的持续性”（第35页）。如果没有明确规定的机制，“天平将进一步偏向于错误识别虚假相关性和高估实际的持续程度”（第35页）。

第三，他们认为持续性研究经常依赖有问题的历史数据。以默多克的非洲族群边界（被当时的学者描述为“特殊的威胁”、“教条主义的”和“事实错误的”，第40页）和拉丁美洲前哥伦布时期人口估计（第40-43页）为例，他们表明“历史数据集将被发现存在错误并将被取代”（第38页）。

第四，他们认为许多持续性研究存在“历史压缩”问题，将历史处理变量对当代结果进行回归，却不检验中间时期（第50-56页）。他们通过在多个年份复制努恩（2008年）的奴隶贸易分析来证明这一点，发现“奴隶出口与人均GDP之间的关系在独立后变得更强”，而非随时间保持恒定（第54页）。

第五，他们认为空间自相关威胁着许多持续性研究的发现。地理邻近性产生的相似性可能被误认为历史因果关系，导致“膨胀的系数和低估的标准误”（第60页）。

总体论点在结论中得到概括：“过去很重要，但它不是监狱。制度和历史冲击随时间持续存在并不令人惊讶。但制度不仅持续存在，它们也会改变和适应。社会可以从制度创伤中恢复，‘坏’制度可以被改革”（第60页）。

Mechanisms

The authors emphasize that “persistence studies need a convincing mechanism to explain what stops the effects of past events from dissipating. After all, people move, institutions change, and borders shift. Without mechanisms what you have is quantum persistence: spooky effects at a (temporal) distance” (p. 43). They review and critique various mechanisms proposed in the persistence literature.

Institutional inertia is one commonly proposed mechanism. The authors discuss how Douglass North’s framework proposes that institutions “might change, slowly!, over time” (p. 33). Historical institutionalists have long concerned themselves with “critical junctures,” during which radical institutional change is possible (p. 38). The implication is that between critical junctures, institutions persist through path dependence and self-reinforcing processes.

Cultural transmission is extensively discussed as a mechanism. Voigtländer and Voth (2012) showed that medieval pogroms in Germany /ˈdʒɜːrməni/ correlated geographically with anti-Semitic hostility after World War I. The association “weakens with higher levels of migration and in the Hanseatic trading cities, whose economic base required a modicum of tolerance to function” (p. 49), providing evidence for the cultural transmission hypothesis. Lupu and Peisakhin (2017) found that descendants of Crimean /kraɪˈmiːən/ Tatars /ˈtɑːtərz/ who experienced more brutality during Stalin’s /ˈstɑːlɪnz/ deportation are more likely to identify as Tatar and oppose Russian annexation, with “attitudes passed on through family ties” (p. 36). Chen et al. (2020) argue that China’s /ˈtʃaɪnəz/ imperial exam system created “pro-education traditions among elite families, which in turn passed those attributes on to their children and grandchildren” (p. 49).

Agglomeration economies and complementarities are discussed as economic mechanisms. Comin et al. (2010) propose “path dependence deriving from complementarities between old and new technologies, adoption costs, and feedback effects that make further technological advance easier once past technologies have been mastered” (p. 44). However, the authors criticize this as purely theoretical without historical evidence.

Elite capture and political economy mechanisms are also reviewed. Dell (2010) proposes that haciendas protected peasants from colonial exploitation, saw more productivity growth due to more secure property rights, and were better able to mobilize for public investment (pp. 46-47). Suryanarayan and White show that white elites in the American South “deliberately weakened the institutions that enabled efficient tax collection” to prevent future redistribution (p. 49). Banerjee and Iyer (2005) propose that the Indian landlord system created “a polarized and conflictual political environment created by peasant ‘memories of an oppressive and often absentee landlord class’” (p. 48).

Intergroup complementarities are discussed through Jha’s (2013) study showing that medieval Indian trading cities were less likely to experience intercommunal violence because “patterns of occupational specialization by ethnicity” required “intergroup cooperation to function” (p. 49).

The authors propose a novel mechanism through Giuliano and Nunn (2020): environmental stability. Societies in more climatically stable environments show more persistence because “established practices would be less likely to be challenged in areas where the environment was more stable” (p. 50).

Critically, the authors identify three problematic categories of mechanism specification: (1) studies relying on theoretical models “without historical evidence that they indeed operated” (p. 43); (2) studies confusing “alternative outcome measures with evidence of historical mechanisms” (p. 43); and (3) studies presenting “plausible mechanisms but tortur[ing] history in the process” (p. 43). They argue that “identifying convincing mechanisms implies paying attention to the history ‘in the middle,’ painfully constructing historical datasets, and sweating the details” (p. 50).

作者强调，“持续性研究需要一个令人信服的机制来解释是什么阻止了过去事件的效应消散。毕竟，人们会迁移，制度会改变，边界会转移。没有机制，你所拥有的就是量子持续性：（时间）距离上的诡异效应”（第43页）。他们回顾并批评了持续性文献中提出的各种机制。

制度惯性是一种常见的提议机制。作者讨论了道格拉斯·诺斯的框架如何提出制度“可能随时间，缓慢地！，改变”（第33页）。历史制度主义者长期以来关注“关键节点”，在此期间激进的制度变革是可能的（第38页）。其含义是，在关键节点之间，制度通过路径依赖和自我强化过程而持续存在。

文化传递作为一种机制被广泛讨论。福伊格特兰德与福斯（2012年）表明，德国（Germany /ˈdʒɜːrməni/）中世纪的大屠杀在地理上与第一次世界大战后的反犹太敌意相关。这种关联“随着更高的移民水平和在汉萨贸易城市中而减弱，这些城市的经济基础需要一定程度的宽容才能运作”（第49页），为文化传递假说提供了证据。卢普与佩萨欣（2017年）发现，在斯大林（Stalin /ˈstɑːlɪnz/）驱逐期间经历了更多暴行的克里米亚（Crimean /kraɪˈmiːən/）鞑靼人（Tatars /ˈtɑːtərz/）后代更可能认同为鞑靼人并反对俄罗斯吞并，“态度通过家庭纽带传递”（第36页）。陈等人（2020年）认为，中国（China /ˈtʃaɪnəz/）的科举考试制度在精英家庭中创造了“崇尚教育的传统，这些家庭反过来将这些属性传递给他们的子孙”（第49页）。

聚集经济和互补性被讨论为经济机制。科明等人（2010年）提出“源自新旧技术之间互补性的路径依赖、采用成本以及使进一步技术进步在掌握过去技术后变得更容易的反馈效应”（第44页）。然而，作者批评这纯粹是理论性的，没有历史证据。

精英俘获和政治经济学机制也得到了回顾。戴尔（2010年）提出，庄园保护农民免受殖民剥削，由于更安全的产权而获得了更多的生产率增长，并更有能力动员公共投资（第46-47页）。苏里亚纳拉亚南与怀特表明，美国南方的白人精英“故意削弱了使有效税收征管成为可能的制度”以防止未来的再分配（第49页）。班纳吉与艾耶尔（2005年）提出，印度地主制度创造了“由农民对’压迫性的、经常缺席的地主阶级’的记忆所创造的两极化和冲突性的政治环境”（第48页）。

群体间互补性通过贾（2013年）的研究得到讨论，该研究表明中世纪印度贸易城市不太可能经历社区间暴力，因为“按族群划分的职业专业化模式”需要“群体间合作才能运作”（第49页）。

作者通过朱利亚诺与努恩（2020年）提出了一种新颖的机制：环境稳定性。气候更稳定的环境中的社会表现出更多的持续性，因为“在环境更稳定的地区，既定做法不太可能受到挑战”（第50页）。

批判性地，作者识别了三类有问题的机制规定：（1）依赖理论模型“而没有历史证据表明它们确实运作”的研究（第43页）；（2）将“替代结果测量与历史机制的证据混淆”的研究（第43页）；（3）提出“貌似合理的机制但在此过程中折磨历史”的研究（第43页）。他们认为，“识别令人信服的机制意味着关注’中间的’历史，痛苦地构建历史数据集，并注重细节”（第50页）。

Data & methods

Data

As a review article, this paper primarily synthesizes and reanalyzes existing studies rather than collecting original data. However, the authors conduct several replication exercises to support their methodological critiques.

Geographic coverage: The reviewed studies span the globe, with particular attention to Africa, Latin America, Europe, South Asia, and the United States. Specific regions discussed include: Germany (East and West), Austria /ˈɒstriə/, Crimea /kraɪˈmiːə/, Ukraine /juːˈkreɪn/, Poland, British and French Africa, Togoland /ˈtoʊɡoʊlænd/, Kenya, Tanzania, Peru /pəˈruː/, Bolivia /bəˈlɪviə/, Argentina /ˌɑːrdʒənˈtiːnə/, and various other American states and Latin American countries.

Time period: The studies reviewed cover treatments ranging from 1000 B.C. (Comin et al. 2010) to the 20th century, with outcomes measured from the colonial period through the 2010s.

Unit of analysis: The authors note that persistence studies operate at multiple levels, countries, provinces, municipalities, districts, and sometimes individuals. They discuss the shift around 2010 when “scholars realized that they could collect subnational data and test for long-term persistence within countries” (p. 34).

Replication data and exercises:

The authors replicate Nunn’s (2008) slave trade analysis using “new GDP per capita data from the latest release of the Maddison Project (2020) for every year between 1950 and 2018” (p. 54). They ran “the regressions from Table III of Nunn (2008)” with the sample reduced to 50 observations after dropping Somalia /səˈmɑːliə/ and the D.R. Congo /ˈkɒŋɡoʊ/ (p. 54). This exercise demonstrates how the slave trade coefficient and its significance varied over time (Figure 6, p. 55).

For geographic robustness tests, the authors replicate three country-level studies (Acemoglu et al. 2002; Comin et al. 2010; Ashraf and Galor 2013) adding World Bank regional classifications as controls (Table 1, p. 58). For subnational analysis, they replicate Dell (2010) and Bruhn and Gallego (2012), adding latitude and longitude squared as controls (Table 2, p. 59).

Data quality critiques:

The authors extensively critique Murdock’s (1959) African ethnic boundaries data, noting it was criticized by contemporaries as “a special menace,” “dogmatic,” and “factually wrong” (pp. 39-40). Linguists found that “a large proportion of the linguistic statements are incorrect, uncritical, or even meaningless” (p. 40). Historians noted it “can never become a standard handbook to the peoples of Africa” due to unreliable sources (p. 40).

For Latin American pre-Columbian population estimates, the authors use Pyle (1992) and González (1992) to illustrate the uncertainty involved. They note the wide variation between “High Counters” (assuming >90% depopulation) and “minimalists” (assuming ~20% depopulation) for New Spain /speɪn/ (p. 41). They provide a detailed example of estimating San Luis /sæn luˈis/ province in Argentina, showing how estimates depend on cumulative judgment calls and assumptions about depopulation rates from San Juan /sæn wɑːn/ (pp. 41-42).

作为一篇综述文章，本文主要综合和重新分析现有研究，而非收集原始数据。然而，作者进行了若干复制练习以支持其方法论批评。

地理覆盖范围：被综述的研究遍及全球，特别关注非洲、拉丁美洲、欧洲、南亚和美国。讨论的具体地区包括：德国（东德和西德）、奥地利（Austria /ˈɒstriə/）、克里米亚（Crimea /kraɪˈmiːə/）、乌克兰（Ukraine /juːˈkreɪn/）、波兰、英属和法属非洲、多哥兰（Togoland /ˈtoʊɡoʊlænd/）、肯尼亚、坦桑尼亚、秘鲁（Peru /pəˈruː/）、玻利维亚（Bolivia /bəˈlɪviə/）、阿根廷（Argentina /ˌɑːrdʒənˈtiːnə/）以及其他各种美国州和拉丁美洲国家。

时间段：被综述的研究涵盖从公元前1000年（科明等人2010年）到20世纪的处理变量，结果测量从殖民时期到2010年代。

分析单位：作者指出，持续性研究在多个层面运作，国家、省份、市镇、地区，有时是个人。他们讨论了大约2010年的转变，当时“学者们意识到他们可以收集次国家级数据并在国家内部检验长期持续性”（第34页）。

复制数据和练习：

作者使用“麦迪逊项目（2020年）最新发布的1950年至2018年每年的新人均GDP数据”复制了努恩（2008年）的奴隶贸易分析（第54页）。他们运行了“努恩（2008年）表III的回归”，在剔除索马里（Somalia /səˈmɑːliə/）和刚果民主共和国（D.R. Congo /ˈkɒŋɡoʊ/）后，样本减少到50个观测值（第54页）。这一练习展示了奴隶贸易系数及其显著性如何随时间变化（图6，第55页）。

对于地理稳健性检验，作者复制了三项国家层面的研究（阿西莫格鲁等人2002年；科明等人2010年；阿什拉夫与加洛尔2013年），添加了世界银行区域分类作为控制变量（表1，第58页）。对于次国家级分析，他们复制了戴尔（2010年）和布伦与加列戈（2012年），添加了纬度和经度的平方作为控制变量（表2，第59页）。

数据质量批评：

作者广泛批评了默多克（1959年）的非洲族群边界数据，指出它被同时代人批评为“特殊的威胁”、“教条主义的”和“事实错误的”（第39-40页）。语言学家发现“很大比例的语言陈述是不正确的、不批判的或甚至是无意义的”（第40页）。历史学家指出，由于不可靠的来源，它“永远不可能成为非洲各民族的标准手册”（第40页）。

对于拉丁美洲前哥伦布时期的人口估计，作者使用派尔（1992年）和冈萨雷斯（1992年）来说明其中的不确定性。他们注意到对于新西班牙（New Spain /speɪn/），“高估者”（假设>90%的人口减少）与“低估者”（假设约20%的人口减少）之间的巨大差异（第41页）。他们提供了估计阿根廷圣路易斯（San Luis /sæn luˈis/）省的详细例子，表明估计如何依赖于累积的判断和关于圣胡安（San Juan /sæn wɑːn/）人口减少率的假设（第41-42页）。

Methods

The authors employ multiple methodological approaches to assess their critical arguments about the persistence literature.

Narrative literature review: The primary method is a critical synthesis of the persistence studies literature, organized thematically around common methodological pitfalls. The authors systematically review canonical studies to illustrate problems with data quality, mechanism specification, temporal compression, and spatial autocorrelation.

Bibliometric analysis: The authors present Figure 1 (p. 35), drawing on Cioni et al. (2021), showing “persistence studies as a share of all published articles on economic history” from 2001-2019. This quantifies the growth of the persistence literature and documents that “persistence studies have made the most headway in general interest economics journals” rather than specialized economic history journals.

Replication and robustness analysis: The authors conduct original replication exercises. For Nunn (2008), they re-estimate the main specification for each year from 1950-2018 using updated Maddison Project data, producing time-series of coefficients and p-values (Figure 6, p. 55). This decompresses the history to test whether the relationship holds across intermediate periods.

Geographic robustness tests: Following Kelly (2020), the authors add World Bank regional dummies to cross-country regressions (Table 1, p. 58) and coordinates as controls to subnational regressions (Table 2, p. 59). These tests assess whether persistence findings survive controls for spatial trends.

Assumptions for validity: The authors articulate several assumptions that must hold for persistence study methods to be valid:

Historical data accuracy: The historical treatment variable must be measured with reasonable accuracy. The authors show this assumption often fails, citing Murdock’s ethnic boundaries and pre-Columbian population estimates.

Exclusion restriction for instruments: When instrumental variables are used, the instrument must affect outcomes only through the proposed channel. The authors question whether settler mortality (Acemoglu et al. 2001) or pre-Columbian population density satisfy this assumption.

Stable unit treatment value assumption (SUTVA): Units must be independent, but spatial autocorrelation violates this assumption. The authors note that “everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related than distant things” (Tobler’s First Law of Geography, p. 57).

Temporal stability of relationships: The relationship between historical treatment and outcomes should be stable across time if the proposed mechanism operates consistently. The authors show this often fails by demonstrating how coefficients change across periods.

No omitted variable bias: Geographic factors may confound relationships between historical treatments and outcomes. The authors demonstrate that adding regional controls reduces coefficient magnitudes.

Categorization of studies: The authors develop a useful typology distinguishing “apples to oranges” studies (economic outcomes, non-economic treatments) from “apples to apples” studies (non-economic outcomes and treatments) (p. 34), and identify three categories of problematic mechanism specification (p. 43).

作者采用多种方法论途径来评估他们对持续性文献的批判性论点。

叙述性文献综述：主要方法是对持续性研究文献的批判性综合，按常见方法论陷阱的主题组织。作者系统地回顾经典研究，以说明数据质量、机制规定、时间压缩和空间自相关方面的问题。

文献计量分析：作者呈现了图1（第35页），引用西奥尼等人（2021年），展示了2001-2019年“持续性研究占所有发表的经济史文章的份额”。这量化了持续性文献的增长，并记录了“持续性研究在通用经济学期刊中取得了最大进展”，而非专业经济史期刊。

复制和稳健性分析：作者进行原创性复制练习。对于努恩（2008年），他们使用更新的麦迪逊项目数据对1950-2018年的每一年重新估计主要规范，产生系数和p值的时间序列（图6，第55页）。这解压了历史以检验关系是否在中间时期成立。

地理稳健性检验：遵循凯利（2020年），作者在跨国回归中添加世界银行区域虚拟变量（表1，第58页），并在次国家级回归中添加坐标作为控制变量（表2，第59页）。这些检验评估持续性发现是否在控制空间趋势后仍然成立。

有效性假设：作者阐明了持续性研究方法有效所必须满足的若干假设：

历史数据准确性：历史处理变量必须以合理的准确性测量。作者表明这一假设经常失败，引用了默多克的族群边界和前哥伦布时期人口估计。

工具变量的排他性限制：当使用工具变量时，工具必须只通过提议的渠道影响结果。作者质疑定居者死亡率（阿西莫格鲁等人2001年）或前哥伦布时期人口密度是否满足这一假设。

稳定单位处理值假设（SUTVA）：单位必须独立，但空间自相关违反了这一假设。作者指出“一切事物都与其他一切事物相关，但近处的事物比远处的事物关系更密切”（托布勒地理学第一定律，第57页）。

关系的时间稳定性：如果提议的机制一致运作，历史处理与结果之间的关系应该随时间保持稳定。作者通过展示系数如何跨时期变化来表明这经常失败。

无遗漏变量偏误：地理因素可能混淆历史处理与结果之间的关系。作者证明添加区域控制变量会减少系数大小。

研究分类：作者发展了一个有用的类型学，区分“苹果对橘子”研究（经济结果，非经济处理）与“苹果对苹果”研究（非经济结果和处理）（第34页），并识别了三类有问题的机制规定（第43页）。

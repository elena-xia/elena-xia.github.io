Week 2 · Coercion–Extraction lane · Pristine states (S1)

Robert L. Carneiro (1970), “A Theory of the Origin of the State,” Science.卡内罗（1970）《国家起源的一种理论》，Science。

Deductive theoryComparative historyTilly: precursor

Dependent variable (DV)

The origin and formation of the state. Carneiro /karˈnejru/ seeks to explain why pristine states emerged in certain geographic locations instead of others, and what conditions were necessary for autonomous political units to transform into centralized states with coercive authority. The dependent variable is essentially the transition from autonomous villages to integrated political units with centralized government capable of taxation, conscription, and law enforcement (p. 2).

主要因变量：国家的起源和形成。具体而言，Carneiro 试图解释为何pristine states出现在特定的地理位置而非其他地方，以及通过什么条件使自治的政治单位转变为具有强制性权力的中央集权国家。因变量本质上是从自治村落向拥有征税、征兵和执法能力的中央集权政府的一体化政治单位的过渡。

Independent variable (IV)

The primary independent variable is environmental circumscription, which is defined as the geographic constraint of arable /‘ærəbəl/ agricultural land by natural barriers such as mountains, deserts, or seas. Secondary independent variables: (1) population pressure that creates competition for limited resources; (2) resource concentration, namely “social circumscription,” namely the clustering of valuable resources that creates similar constraining effects; (3) warfare as the proximate mechanism through which political integration occurs (pp. 3-4).

主要自变量是Environmental circumscription，

定义为由山脉、沙漠或海洋等自然屏障对可耕种农业用地的地理限制。次要自变量包括：(1) 人口压力/增长，导致对有限资源的竞争；(2) 资源集中，Carneiro 称之为social circumscription，指宝贵资源的聚集产生类似的限制效应；(3) 战争，这是政治整合发生的直接机制。

Stateness

Carneiro/karˈnejru/ conceptualizes the state as “an autonomous political unit, encompassing many communities within its territory and having a centralized government with the power to collect taxes, draft men for work or war, and decree and enforce laws” (p. 2). This definition emphasizes three core elements: territorial scope (multiple communities), centralized authority, and coercive capacity (taxation, conscription, legal enforcement). The state is measured qualitatively through archaeological and historical evidence of political integration, tribute systems, and administrative hierarchies. Carneiro/karˈnejru/ explicitly distinguishes the state from chiefdoms and villages, and the former had formal coercive institutions rather than only consensual authority (pp. 2, 5).

将国家概念化为“一个自治的政治单位，在其领土内包含许多社区，并拥有一个有权征税、征召人员劳动或作战、以及颁布和执行法律的中央政府”。 该定义强调了三个核心要素：领土范围（多个社区）、中央集权和强制能力（税收、征兵、执法）。 国家通过考古和历史证据（政治整合、贡赋系统和行政等级制度）进行定性测量。明确区分了国家与酋邦和村落，区别在于是否存在正式的强制性制度，而不仅仅是协商性权威。

Core argument

Carneiro’s /karˈnejru/ core argument is that states arise through warfare in environmentally circumscribed areas. When agricultural land is bounded by geographic barriers (mountains, deserts, seas), population growth creates pressure on limited resources. Unlike in uncircumscribed environments, defeated groups can flee to new territories; in circumscribed areas, defeated populations cannot escape and have to surrender. This initially takes the form of tribute payment (proto-taxation), and through successive rounds of warfare and conquest, small villages aggregate into chiefdoms, chiefdoms aggregate into kingdoms, and kingdoms aggregate into empires. Carneiro /karˈnejru/ rejects voluntaristic explanations (social contract, automatic agricultural surplus) since they’re historically unfounded. Instead, coercion through warfare is the universal mechanism of state formation (pp. 2-5).

国家是在环境受限的地区通过战争产生的。 当农业用地受到地理屏障（山脉、沙漠、海洋）的限制时，人口增长会对有限的资源造成压力。 在非受限环境中，战败群体可以逃往新领土；而在受限地区，战败人口无法逃脱，必须服从政治从属。 这种从属最初表现为缴纳贡赋（原始税收），通过连续的战争和征服，小村落聚合成酋邦，酋邦聚合成王国，王国聚合成帝国。 该理论明确拒绝自愿论解释（社会契约、农业剩余自动论），认为它们缺乏历史依据，并主张通过战争的强制是国家形成的普遍机制。

Mechanisms

(1) Environmental circumscription creates fixed boundaries for agricultural land, preventing territorial expansion; (2) Population growth increases human density and resource competition; (3) Warfare is made for scarce agricultural land, intensifying in frequency and severity; (4) Defeated populations, unable to flee due to geographic barriers, must surrender;(5) Surrender manifests as tribute/taxation rather than displacement; (6) Winners absorb defeated villages, and become larger political units; (7) Repeated cycles of warfare and incorporation progressively make villages aggregate into chiefdoms, chiefdoms into kingdoms, and kingdoms into multi-valley empires; (8) Administrative complexity increases as territorial units expand, requiring bureaucratic institutions. A supplementary mechanism involves “resource concentration” or “social circumscription”: even without strict environmental barriers, the concentration of valuable resources (like várzea/vaːrzɛa/ land along the Amazon) creates similar situation by making certain areas disproportionately attractive, thus intensifying competition and warfare in those zones (pp. 3-6).

环境限制为农业用地划定了固定边界，阻止了领土扩张；人口增长增加了人口密度和资源竞争；针对稀缺农地的战争爆发，且频率和烈度加剧；战败人口因地理屏障无法逃离，必须屈服于胜利者；政治从属表现为贡赋/税收而非驱逐；成功的征服将战败村落纳入更大的政治单位；反复的战争和合并循环逐步将村落聚合成酋邦，酋邦聚合成流域王国，王国聚合成跨流域帝国；随着领土单位扩张，行政复杂性增加，需要官僚机构。

一个补充机制涉及“资源集中”或“社会限制”：即使没有严格的环境屏障，宝贵资源的集中（如亚马逊沿岸的肥沃冲积土）也会产生类似的动力，使某些区域格外具有吸引力，从而加剧这些区域的竞争和战争。

Data & methods

Data

Carneiro /karˈnejru/ compared archaeological and historical evidence from multiple world regions. Primary cases: (1) Coastal Peru /pəˈruː/: 78 short, narrow valleys circumscribed by mountains, desert, and sea, providing the most detailed reconstruction of state formation through warfare and political incorporation (pp. 4-5);(2) Amazonia /əˌmæˈzoʊniə/: a contrasting case of uncircumscribed environment where villages remained autonomous, showing that warfare alone is insufficient without circumscription (pp. 4); (3) The Nile /naɪ/ Valley, Tigris/ˈtaɪɡrɪs/-Euphrates/ˈjuːfreɪtiːz/ (Mesopotamia/ˌmes.ə.pəˈteɪ.mi.ə/), Indus Valley, Yellow River valley, and Mesoamerica/“mesəʊəˈmerɪkə”/: parallel cases where circumscription and warfare produced pristine states (pp. 3-4). Ethnographic data from the Yanomamö of Venezuela /ˌven.əˈzweɪ.lə/ illustrates the social circumscription mechanism through resource concentration effects (pp. 6-7). Evidence includes archaeological remains (earthworks, fortifications, settlement patterns), Spanish colonial accounts, and early anthropological observations. Variables are measured qualitatively through presence/absence of geographic barriers, evidence of warfare (weapons, defensive structures, skeletal trauma), and indicators of political hierarchy (differential burial practices, monumental architecture, administrative records) (pp. 3-7).

采用了来自多个世界区域的比较考古学和历史证据。主要案例包括：（1）秘鲁沿海：78个短而窄的山谷，受山脉、沙漠和海洋限制，提供了通过战争和政治合并形成国家的最详细重建；(2)亚马逊地区：作为非受限环境的对比案例，尽管有战争，村落仍保持自治，证明仅有战争而无限制是不够的；(3)尼罗河流域、底格里斯-幼发拉底河（美索不达米亚）、印度河流域、黄河流域和中美洲：被引用为限制和战争产生原生国家的平行案例。来自委内瑞拉Yanomamö人的民族志数据说明了通过资源集中效应产生的社会限制机制。证据包括考古遗存（土方工程、防御工事、聚落形态）、西班牙殖民记录和早期人类学观察。 变量通过地理屏障的存在/缺失、战争证据（武器、防御结构、骨骼创伤）以及政治等级指标（差异化墓葬、纪念性建筑、行政记录）进行定性测量。

Methods

Carneiro /karˈnejru/ employs comparative historical analysis. He compares circumscribed environments (coastal Peru, Nile Valley, Mesopotamia) where states emerged, with uncircumscribed environments (Amazonia, eastern North American woodlands) where states did not emerge, despite similar agricultural practices and warfare. This quasi-experimental design isolates environmental circumscription as the key differentiating factor. The theory is also tested against “deviant cases”: areas that have some characteristics of civilization but do not follow the circumscription rule (Classic Maya, Hwang Valley of China, Petén/peˈten/ region), which he explains through the supplementary mechanism of social circumscription.

Key assumptions include: (1) warfare is universal among agricultural societies; (2) population naturally tends to grow; (3) humans prefer to remain in productive areas rather than migrate to marginal lands; (4) defeated groups prefer political subordination rather than extinction. This article is primarily deductive, deriving predictions from ecological constraints and testing them against the historical record (pp. 3-7).

采用比较历史分析，使用差异法。分析策略比较了产生国家的受限环境（秘鲁沿海、尼罗河流域、美索不达米亚）与未产生国家的非受限环境（亚马逊、北美东部林地），尽管它们有类似的农业实践和战争。 这种准实验设计将环境限制分离为关键的区分因素。 该理论也针对“偏差案例”进行了检验：即具有文明特征但明显不符合限制规则的地区（古典玛雅、中国黄河流域、佩滕地区），Carneiro 通过社会限制这一补充机制来解释。

关键假设: (1) 战争在农业社会中是普遍的；(2) 人口有自然增长的趋势；(3) 人类倾向于留在生产力高的地区，而非迁移到边缘土地；(4) 对于战败群体而言，政治从属优于灭绝。该方法主要是演绎的，从生态约束中推导出预测，并根据历史记录进行检验。

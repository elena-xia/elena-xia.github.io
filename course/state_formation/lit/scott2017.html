Week 2 · Material–Technological lane · Pristine states (S1)

James C. Scott (2017), Against the Grain: A Deep History of the Earliest States, chs. 4–5.斯科特（2017）《作茧自缚：早期国家的深层历史》第4–5章。

Comparative historyDeductive theory

Dependent variable (DV)

The main dependent variable in these chapters is the formation, maintenance, and expansion of early states, particularly in Mesopotamia/ˌmes.ə.pəˈteɪ.mi.ə/ (explaining how pristine states emerged and what conditions were necessary for their persistence). The dependent variable encompasses several interrelated phenomena: the emergence of territorial political units with tax-collecting apparatuses, the concentration of population and grain production under state control, and the development of coercive institutions that enabled surplus extraction.

主要因变量是早期国家的形成、维持和扩张，特别是美索不达米亚地区。 Scott根本上感兴趣的是解释原生国家是如何出现的，以及它们持久存在需要什么条件。更具体地说，因变量包含几个相互关联的结果：拥有税收机构的领土政治单位的出现、人口和谷物生产在国家控制下的集中、以及使得剩余提取成为可能的强制性制度的发展。 Scott试图解释为什么国家在特定的时间和地点出现，以及为什么它们采取了依赖谷物农业和强迫劳动的“谷物国家”的特定形式。

Independent variable (IV)

Scott proposes multiple independent variables operating at different levels, but the core factors can be grouped into three main categories: (1) The availability of a grain-and-manpower module, a pre-existing concentration of sedentary cultivators who growed domesticated cereal grains on fertile alluvial or loess soils. Scott argues this module was the “only possible scaffolding available for the design of a state” (p. 1). The ecological conditions of rich, well-watered alluvium were necessary prerequisites. (2) Climate change and environmental aridity during the period from 3500 to 2500 BCE, forcing populations to concentrate around remaining watercourses and reduced alternative subsistence options like foraging and hunting (p. 5-6). (3) The availability and deployment of coerced labor (including slavery, debt bondage, corvée /kɔːˈveɪ/ labor, and forced resettlement). Scott argues that it was essential for extracting surplus from populations that otherwise had no incentive to produce beyond subsistence (p. 36-38).

Scott 提出了在不同层面上运作的多个自变量，但核心因果因素可归为三大类：(1) “谷物与人力模块（grain-and-manpower module）”的可用性，即在肥沃的冲积土或黄土上种植驯化谷物的定居耕作者的预先集中。Scott 认为该模块是“国家设计唯一可能的脚手架”。肥沃、水源充足的冲积土的生态条件是必要的先决条件。(2) 公元前 3500 年至 2500 年期间的气候变化和环境干旱，迫使人口集中在剩余的水道周围，并减少了觅食和狩猎等替代生存选择。(3) 强迫劳动（包括奴隶制、债务奴役、徭役和强迫重新安置）的可用性和部署，Scott 认为这对于从原本没有动力生产超过维持生计所需的剩余的人口中提取剩余至关重要。

Stateness

Scott conceptualizes “stateness” as a continuous rather than binary phenomenon, describing it as “a more-or-less proposition rather than strictly either/or” (p. 2-3). He identifies multiple phenomena that, when present together, indicate the existence of a state. These include: a territorial political unit that monopolizes coercive force; strata of officials specialized in tax assessment and collection; executive power over a stratified, hierarchical society with appreciable division of labor (weavers, artisans, priests, metalworkers, clerks, soldiers, cultivators); and potentially an army, defensive walls, a monumental ritual center or palace, and a king or queen (p. 3).

Among these, Scott treats three as the clearest signs of stateness: territoriality (marked by walls), a specialized tax apparatus (officials engaged in collection), and the capacity for record-keeping (p. 3). He uses the state of Uruk/‘uːrʊk/ (firmly established by 3200 BCE) as a paradigmatic case, noting its walls built between 3300 and 3000 BCE, its specialist scribal class, soldiers with armor, standardized weights and measures, and evidence of work gangs mobilized by temples (p. 3-5).

Scott measures stateness empirically through archaeological evidence: the presence of city walls, cuneiform tablets recording taxes and rations, beveled-rim bowls used for distributing grain rations, and bas reliefs depicting prisoners of war in neck shackles (p. 4-5, p. 17).

Scott 将“国家”概念化为一种连续而非二元的现象，将其描述为“一个程度命题，而非严格的非此即彼”。 他确定了多种属性，当这些属性同时存在时，表明国家的存在。 这些包括：垄断强制力量的领土政治单位；专门从事税收评估和征收的官员阶层； 对分层、等级社会拥有行政权力，并具有明显的劳动分工（织工、工匠、祭司、金属工人、办事员、士兵、耕作者）； 以及可能拥有的军队、防御墙、纪念性仪式中心或宫殿，以及国王或王后。 在这些特征中，Scott 认为三个最能体现国家性：领土性（以城墙为标志）、专门的税收机构（从事征收的官员）和记录保存能力。 他以公元前 3200 年已稳固建立的乌鲁克（Uruk）为范例，指出其建于公元前 3300 年至 3000 年之间的城墙、其专业的书吏阶层、配备盔甲的士兵、标准化的度量衡以及寺庙动员工作队的证据。 Scott 通过考古证据实证测量国家性：城墙的存在、记录税收和口粮的楔形文字泥板、用于分发谷物口粮的斜边碗，以及描绘戴颈镣战俘的浮雕。

Core argument

(1) Only cereal grains (wheat, barley, millet, rice, maize) could serve as the basis for state formation because they alone possess the combination of characteristics required for effective taxation: they are “visible, divisible, assessable, storable, transportable, and rationable” (p. 14). Unlike tubers that can be left underground, legumes/‘leɡjuːm/ that ripen continuously, or mobile pastoralist wealth, cereal grains ripen simultaneously above ground at a predictable time, making them uniquely susceptible to state appropriation (p. 14-18). This explains why there have been no “lentil states,” “potato states,” or “cassava/kəˈsɑːvə/ states” in history despite the nutritional adequacy of these crops (p. 14).

(2) The spatial concentration of population on alluvial /əˈluːviəl/ soils was essential because “archaic states, in the crudest material terms, were all agrarian and required an appropriable surplus of agro-pastoral products to feed nonproducers” (p. 9). Given ancient transport logistics, this meant concentrating cultivators and arable land within the smallest possible radius. “No alluvium, no state” captures this geographic determinism (p. 9).

(3) Coerced labor in various forms was indispensable to early state formation. Without compulsion, peasants with direct access to means of subsistence would not voluntarily produce surplus beyond their needs. This was the logic of what Sahlins /sɑːlɪnz/ called the “domestic mode of production” (p. 37). Only through “corvée labor, forced delivery of grain or other products, debt bondage, serfdom, communal bondage and tribute, and various forms of slavery” could surplus be extracted (p. 37-38). “One is tempted to assert: ‘No slavery, no state’” (p. 41).

Scott 认为只有谷物（小麦、大麦、小米、稻米、玉米）能够作为国家形成的基础，因为只有它们具备有效征税所需的特征组合：它们是“可见的、可分割的、可评估的、可储存的、可运输的和可定量的”。 与可以留在地下的块茎、连续成熟的豆类或流动的游牧财富不同，谷物在可预测的时间在地上同时成熟，使其独特地易于被国家占有。 这解释了为什么尽管扁豆、土豆或木薯在营养上足够，历史上却没有出现“扁豆国家”、“土豆国家”或“木薯国家”。

Scott 认为人口在冲积土壤上的空间集中是必不可少的，因为“粗略地从物质角度看，古代国家都是农业国家，需要农牧产品的可占用剩余来养活非生产者”。 考虑到古代的运输物流，这意味着将耕作者和可耕地集中在尽可能小的半径内。 “无冲积土，无国家”捕捉了这种地理决定论。

Scott 主张各种形式的强迫劳动对于早期国家的形成是不可或缺的。 没有强制，能够直接获得生存手段的农民不会自愿生产超过其需求的剩余，这是萨林斯（Sahlins）所说的“家庭生产模式”的逻辑。 只有通过“徭役、强迫通过谷物或其他产品交付、债务奴役、农奴制、集体奴役和贡赋，以及各种形式的奴隶制”，剩余才能被提取。 Scott 甚至提出“人们不禁要断言：‘无奴隶制，无国家’”。

Mechanisms

(1) Climate-induced concentration: The period from 3500 to 2500 BCE was marked by steep declines in sea level and reduced water volume in the Euphrates /juːˈfreɪtiːz/. This increasing aridity caused rivers to shrink to main channels, forced populations to huddle around remaining watercourses, and caused soil salinization that sharply reduced arable land. The result was that “90 percent of the population lived in settlements of thirty hectares or more”, a concentration that “intensified the grain-and-manpower modules that were ideal for state formation” (p. 6). Climate change thus “delivered, as it were, an assembled population and concentrated cereal grains in an embryonic state space that could not, at that epoch, have been assembled by any other means” (p. 6-7).

(2) Legibility: Cereal grains create a “legible” population amenable to taxation. Because grains ripen simultaneously above ground, tax collectors can arrive at harvest time and “cut, thresh, and confiscate the entire harvest in one operation” (p. 15). The small grain format allows for precise measurement by weight and volume, enabling standardized accounting. This legibility extends to population through census-taking, which provided “the basis for assigning the head tax and corvée labor, and for conscription” (p. 27-28). Writing emerged primarily as “bookkeeping” for state administration for more than half a millennium before it was used for literature, mythology, or religious texts (p. 26).

(3) City walls served the function of protection and confinement. While protecting against external raiders, walls were “built quite as much to keep Chinese taxpaying cultivators inside as to keep the barbarians (nomads) outside” (p. 23). The so-called anti-Amorite /ˈæməraɪt/ walls between the Tigris /ˈtaɪɡrɪs/ and Euphrates /juːˈfreɪtiːz/ may have been “designed more to keep cultivators in the state ‘zone’ than to keep out the Amorites” (p. 23-24).

(4) States required labor for tasks that free subjects would resist, like quarrying, mining, canal digging, brick making, galley rowing. War captives provided “a disposable proletariat for the most onerous work,” sparing subjects from degrading drudgery that might “provoke flight or rebellion” while satisfying “military and monumental ambitions” (p. 42, 55). Women captives were valued “at least as much for their reproductive services as for their labor,” helping to alleviate the “otherwise unhealthy effects of concentration and the domus” (p. 53-54).

(5) The population-replenishment mechanism: Given high mortality rates in early state centers, states faced constant demographic challenges. Wars of capture, forced resettlement, and pro-natalist policies were employed to maintain and expand population. The Uruk population “tripled over two hundred years, an increase unlikely to have come from natural population growth, given the high mortality rates” (p. 4). Bas reliefs depicting prisoners in neck shackles “suggest another means by which the population was augmented” (p. 4-5).

（1）气候诱导的集中：公元前3500年至2500年期间，海平面急剧下降，幼发拉底河水量减少。日益干旱导致河流缩减为主河道，迫使人口聚集在剩余水道周围，土壤盐碱化急剧减少了可耕地。结果是“90%的人口生活在30公顷或更大的定居点中”，这种集中“强化了适合国家形成的谷物与人力模块”（第6页）。气候变化因此“可以说，交付了一个聚集的人口和集中的谷物到一个胚胎状态的空间，这是那个时代任何其他手段都无法聚集的”（第6-7页）。

（2）易读性：谷物创造了易于征税的可辨识（legible）人口。由于谷物在地上同时成熟，税收官可以在收获时到达，“一次性切割、脱粒并没收整个收成”（第15页）。小颗粒形式允许通过重量和体积进行精确测量，从而实现标准化核算。这种易读性延伸到人口普查，提供了“分配人头税和徭役以及征兵的基础”（第27-28页）。文字的出现主要是作为国家行政的“簿记”，在用于文学、神话或宗教文本之前，这种情况持续了半个多世纪（第26页）。

（3）城墙具有保护和禁闭的双重功能。虽然表面上是为了防御外部袭击者，但城墙的建立“既是为了将中国的纳税耕作者留在里面，也是为了将野蛮人（游牧民）挡在外面”（第23页）。底格里斯河和幼发拉底河之间所谓的反阿摩利人墙可能“更多是为了将耕作者留在国家‘区域’内，而不是为了将阿摩利人挡在外面”（第23-24页）。

（4）国家需要劳动力来完成自由臣民会反抗的任务：采石、采矿、挖运河、制砖、划船。战俘为“最繁重的工作提供了一次性无产阶级”，使臣民免于可能“激起逃跑或反叛”的侮辱性苦役，同时满足“军事和纪念性野心”（第42、55页）。女俘虏“至少因其生殖服务而与其劳动一样受到重视”，有助于缓解“集中和家庭生活的其他不健康影响”（第53-54页）。

（5）人口补充机制：鉴于早期国家中心的高死亡率，国家面临持续的人口挑战。捕获战争、强迫重新安置和鼓励生育政策被用来维持和扩充人口。乌鲁克人口“在两百年内增加了三倍，鉴于高死亡率，这种增长不太可能来自自然人口增长”（第4页）。描绘戴颈镣战俘的浮雕“暗示了人口增加的另一种手段”（第4-5页）。

Data & methods

Data

(1) Archaeological evidence includes: physical remains of city walls at Uruk (250 hectares, twice the size of classical Athens) (p. 5); cuneiform /ˈkjuːnɪˌfɔːm/ tablets from Uruk Level IV (circa 3,300–3,100 BCE) documenting “barley (as rations and taxes), war captives, male and female slaves” (p. 27); beveled-rim bowls holding approximately two liters of barley, interpreted as standardized ration containers (p. 17); and bas reliefs depicting prisoners in neck fetters (p. 4-5, p. 46-47).

(2) Textual sources include: the Epic of Gilgamesh /ˌɡɪlɡəˈmɛʃ/ (dating from Ur’s Third Dynasty, circa 2100 BCE) (p. 26); Sumerian/ˈsuːmərɪən/ texts such as “Debate between Sheep and Grain” (p. 1); administrative tablets from Umma (circa 2,255 BCE) detailing census data, tax assessments, and labor assignments (p. 27-28); records of 469 prisoners of war from a “house of prisoners” during the reign of Rim-Anum (circa 1805 BCE) (p. 45-46); utopian hymns that reveal ordinary conditions through their negation (p. 47-49); and the Code of Hammurabi (p. 47).

Overall, geographic and temporal coverage spans primarily Mesopotamia from approximately 3500 to 2000 BCE, with the city-state of Uruk receiving most attention as “the most documented archaeologically” (p. 4). Comparative cases include: ancient Egypt (Old Kingdom through New Kingdom, 2686-1069 BCE) (p. 49-51); early China (Qin Dynasty 221-206 BCE and Han Dynasty) (p. 9-10, p. 29-32, p. 51-52); classical Greece (particularly Athens and Sparta) (p. 32-33, p. 41-42); and the Roman Empire (p. 41-42, p. 56-58).

The unit of analysis varies between the city-state (Uruk, Ur, Umma, Lagash), the empire (neo-Assyrian, Qin, Han, Roman), and occasionally individual institutions (the Uruk textile workshops, the “house of prisoners”).

考古证据构成了分析的实证骨干。包括：乌鲁克城墙的物理遗存（250 公顷，是古典雅典的两倍大）；来自乌鲁克 IV 层（约公元前 3300–3100 年）的楔形文字泥板，记录了“大麦（作为口粮和税收）、战俘、男奴和女奴”；容纳约两升大麦的斜边碗，被解释为标准化口粮容器；以及描绘戴颈镣战俘的浮雕。

文本来源包括：吉尔伽美什史诗（源自乌尔第三王朝，约公元前 2100 年）；苏美尔文本如“羊与粮之争”；来自 Umma（约公元前 2255 年）的行政泥板，详细记录了人口普查数据、税收评估和劳动力分配；Rim-Anum 统治时期（约公元前 1805 年）“战俘之家”中 469 名战俘的记录；通过否定揭示普通状况的乌托邦赞美诗；以及汉谟拉比法典。

地理和时间覆盖范围主要跨越公元前 3500 年至 2000 年的美索不达米亚，乌鲁克城邦作为“考古记录最详尽的”受到最多关注。比较案例包括：古埃及（古王国到新王国，公元前 2686-1069 年）；早期中国（秦朝公元前 221-206 年和汉朝）；古典希腊（特别是雅典和斯巴达）；以及罗马帝国。

分析单位在城邦（乌鲁克、乌尔、Umma、Lagash）、帝国（新亚述、秦、汉、罗马）和偶尔的个别机构（乌鲁克纺织作坊、“战俘之家”）之间变化。

Methods

(1) Comparing Mesopotamia, Egypt, China, Greece, and Rome to identify broad patterns in state formation.

(2) Reading utopian or ritual texts by negation, or against the grain. What is suspended during ritual exceptions reveals ordinary conditions. A hymn describing a ritual moment when “the slave woman was an equal of her mistress” and “the creditor did not enter one’s house” is treated as indirect evidence of ordinary oppression (p. 48-49).

(3) Inferring the purpose of institutions from their effects. The coincidence of pristine states and pristine writing “tempts one to the crude functionalist conclusion that would-be state makers invented the forms of notation that were essential to statecraft” (p. 25).

(4) Examining places where states did not form, such as wetlands with diverse subsistence options, pastoral zones, and mountainous regions, to identify conditions for state emergence (pp. 11-13, 19-21).

Assumptions required for validity include:

(1) Archaeological remains and cuneiform tablets provide reasonably accurate representations of past conditions, while acknowledging that “documents are forged and fiddled for private advantage or to please superiors” and that “there is no necessary relation between the records on the one hand and the facts on the ground on the other” (p. 26-27).

(2) The comparative method assumes sufficient similarity across cases to permit generalization. Scott acknowledges that evidence from later periods (neo-Assyrian Empire, classical Greece and Rome) may not be reliably projected backward to Uruk-period Mesopotamia (p. 46-47).

(3) The functionalist logic assumes that institutions persist because they serve identifiable purposes for those in power, which risks overlooking unintended consequences, path dependence, or cultural factors independent of material interests.

确定了美索不达米亚、埃及、中国、希腊和罗马的共同模式，以建立国家形成的一般原则。基于谷物的税收、强迫劳动和人口控制在这些独立案例中的重现加强了论点的普遍性。

解读来源：Scott 通过否定来解释乌托邦或仪式文本。仪式例外期间暂停的事物揭示了普通状况。一首描述“女奴与女主人平等”和“债权人不进家门”的仪式时刻的赞美诗提供了日常压迫的间接证据。

功能主义推断：Scott 经常从机构的效果推断其目的。原生国家和原生文字的巧合“诱使人们得出粗糙的功能主义结论，即想要建国的人发明了对治国术至关重要的符号形式”。

负面案例分析：Scott 通过检查未形成国家的条件（如具有多样化生存选择的湿地、牧区、山区）来加强其论点，以确定国家出现的必要条件。

Scott 假设考古遗存和楔形文字泥板提供了过去状况的合理准确表征，同时承认“文件是为了私人利益或取悦上级而被伪造和篡改的”，并且“记录与实地事实之间没有必然联系”。

比较方法假设案例之间有足够的相似性以允许概括。Scott 承认来自较晚时期（新亚述帝国、古典希腊和罗马）的证据可能无法可靠地向后投射到乌鲁克时期的美索不达米亚。

功能主义逻辑假设制度之所以持续存在是因为它们服务于当权者的可识别目的，这可能会忽视意外后果、路径依赖或独立于物质利益的文化因素。

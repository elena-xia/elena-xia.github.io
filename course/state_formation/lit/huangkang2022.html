Dependent variable (DV)

The main outcome the paper seeks to explain is state formation in Korea and Japan between roughly the fifth and ninth centuries CE, that is, the emergence of centralized, bureaucratically administered, territorially defined states with the capacity to tax populations, organize military force, and provide public goods. More specifically, the authors are not working with a single, tightly operationalized dependent variable in the quantitative sense. Rather, they treat state formation as a historical institutional outcome visible through a cluster of state-like features, above all the development of centralized bureaucracy, which they explicitly describe as the most central component of the state. They then illustrate this outcome through concrete institutional markers such as administrative hierarchies, national taxation, census-taking, legal codes, military organization, and public goods provision.

A more accurate and fair reading is therefore that the DV is not a single uniform indicator composed of six equally weighted parts, but a broader historical process of state-building whose most important institutional expression is bureaucratization. The paper does point to major milestones, such as Japan’s population census, centralized tax system, legal code, large bureaucracy, and provincial–district–village hierarchy, as well as Korea’s Chinese-style administrative codes, national taxation, military reorganization, and later civil service examination, but these are used as evidence of state formation, not as a formally standardized measurement scheme.

It is also worth being slightly more cautious than your original draft about treating the civil service examination as a fully symmetric element across both cases: Table 1 lists a Korean civil service examination in 788, but for Japan it refers more cautiously to a national Confucian academy in 671 and an attempt to create civil service exams, rather than presenting the Japanese case in exactly the same way.

这篇文章试图解释的主要结果，是公元5至9世纪间韩国与日本的国家形成，也就是中央集权、官僚化、领土化国家的出现与巩固，这样的国家具备征税、组织军事力量和提供公共物品的能力。更准确地说，作者并不是在使用一个像量化研究那样被严格统一操作化的单一因变量；相反，他们把国家形成视为一个历史性的制度结果，通过一组“更像国家”的制度特征来呈现，其中最核心的抓手是中央集权官僚制。作者明确把 bureaucracy 视为 state 最中心的组成部分，再辅以行政层级、全国税收、人口普查、法典、军事组织与公共物品供给等具体制度征象来展示这一结果。

因此，更准确也更 fair 的写法是：这个 DV 不是一个由六个等权部分组成的统一指标，而是一个更广义的历史国家建设过程，其中最重要的制度体现是官僚化。文章确实列举了许多关键制度节点，例如日本的人口普查、中央税制、法典、大型官僚系统以及省，郡，村层级结构；韩国的中国式行政法典、全国税收、军事重组和后来的科举，但这些更像是国家形成的证据，而不是一个严格标准化的测量方案。另一个需要比你原稿更谨慎的地方是：不要把 civil service examination 处理得仿佛韩日两案完全对称。表1对韩国写的是 788 年设立科举，但对日本写得更谨慎，是 671 年设立国学并“尝试建立科举”，并非完全相同层级、完全成熟的制度。

Independent variable (IV)

The paper’s main explanatory factor is best understood not as a single independent variable in the narrow sense, but as a broader causal framework of non-bellicist diffusion under a hegemonic regional order. At its center is the claim that Korea and Japan formed states primarily through emulation and learning from China, rather than through bellicist interstate competition. The authors adopt the diffusion framework associated with Simmons, Dobbin, and Garrett and distinguish among coercion, competition, learning, and emulation. Their substantive argument is that, in historical East Asia during the period under study, coercion from China was absent, classic bellicist competition was weak or absent, and emulation, supported by learning, was the dominant mechanism of institutional borrowing.

Emulation is defined as actors adopting policies or ideas deemed appropriate from a social-cultural perspective, an aspirational process driven by the logic of appropriateness, where peripheral elites copy the institutions and norms of a prestigious civilization they respect and admire (p. 6). Learning is defined as a more rational, cost–benefit-oriented process where actors assess the consequences of alternative policies and adopt those deemed more effective, a Bayesian updating process (p. 6). The domestic-level driver of emulation and learning was the competition between the court and the nobility, where courts strategically imported Sinic norms to consolidate power at the expense of the aristocratic class (pp. 24–26).

这篇文章的主要解释因素，最好理解为一种更广义的因果解释框架，而不是狭义上的单一自变量。其核心主张是：韩国和日本的国家形成主要是通过对中国的模仿与学习实现的，而不是通过战争性国家间竞争。作者借用了 Simmons、Dobbin 和 Garrett 的扩散框架，把扩散区分为 coercion、competition、learning 和 emulation 四种机制；他们的实质性论证是，在本文所讨论的东亚历史时期，来自中国的强制并不存在，经典意义上的战争竞争也很弱或基本缺席，而emulation 在 learning 的支持下构成了最主要的制度扩散机制。

模仿被定义为行为体从社会文化视角出发，采纳被视为“适当的”政策或观念，这是一种由适当性逻辑驱动的、具有向往性质的过程，边缘精英复制他们所尊重和仰慕的高声望文明的制度和规范 (p. 6)。学习被定义为更理性的、基于成本-收益分析的过程，行为体评估不同政策的后果并采纳被认为更有效的那些，一种贝叶斯更新过程 (p. 6)。推动模仿与学习的国内层面驱动力是宫廷与贵族之间的竞争，宫廷战略性地引入中华规范以巩固权力、削弱贵族阶层 (pp. 24–26)。

Stateness

The authors conceptualize the state primarily through its administrative-bureaucratic dimension, while also incorporating coercion, taxation, territorial rule, and legality. They explicitly cite Giddens, Hui, Vu, and Centeno, and their synthesis is clear: the state is a political apparatus ruling over territory, backed by law and coercive capacity, but bureaucratization is treated as the central institutional core. This is one reason the article repeatedly foregrounds “centralized bureaucratic control,” “administrative capacity,” and the creation of bureaucratic hierarchies when describing state formation in Korea and Japan.

In terms of measurement, the article relies on historically sequenced qualitative indicators, not formal indices. The authors trace the gradual adoption of Chinese-style institutions and practices that they interpret as evidence of state-building: administrative codes; Chinese-style official titles; provinces and other territorial subdivisions; national taxation and census-taking; conscript militaries; Confucian academies for training officials; legal codes such as the Taika reforms and Taiho code; and, in some cases, civil service examinations or efforts to create them. They also treat the imitation of Chang’an in capitals such as Kyongju and Fujiwara-kyō as part of a broader pattern of systematic state-building mimicry. At the same time, the paper insists that these state-building elements were embedded in a wider process of Sinicization: state formation was “inseparable from” broader adoption of Chinese civilization, including writing, Buddhism, Confucianism, and other cultural forms. To be fair to the authors, however, this does not mean that every cultural import is itself measured as “state capacity.” Rather, the argument is that political institutions in Korea and Japan cannot be understood apart from the larger civilizational environment that made those institutions legitimate, prestigious, and thinkable.

In terms of measurement, the authors use a historically descriptive approach rather than quantitative indices. They trace the presence or absence and the timing of adoption of specific institutional markers: administrative hierarchies (provinces, districts, villages), national census and taxation systems, Chinese-style legal and administrative codes (e.g., Japan’s Taika /ˈtaɪkə/ Reforms of 645, Taiho /ˈtaɪhoʊ/ Code of 702; Korea’s /kəˈriːə/ Chinese-style law codes of 373 and 520), civil service examination systems (Korea 788, Japan modeled on Tang /tɑːŋ/), national Confucian academies (Korea 682, Japan ca. 671), and national conscript militaries (Korea 570, Japan 685) (pp. 15, 20–21). The authors also use the physical construction of capital cities modeled on Chinese Chang’an /tʃɑːŋˈɑːn/ (Kyongju /ˈkjʌŋdʒuː/ in Korea, Nara /ˈnɑːrə/ and Kyoto /ˈkjoʊtoʊ/ in Japan) as evidence of institutional mimicry (p. 22).

A notable feature is that the authors treat state formation as inseparable from broader Sinicization. By Sinicization, they mean the broad adoption of Chinese civilization, including language, writing, religion (Buddhism), philosophy (Confucianism), social structure, art, architecture, and even clothing. This broader civilizational context is treated as part of state formation rather than background alone (pp. 13–14).

作者对“国家”的概念化，首先是从其行政，官僚制维度出发，同时也纳入了强制、税收、领土统治和法律秩序。文章明确援引了 Giddens、Hui、Vu 和 Centeno，其综合后的核心很清楚：国家是一个统治特定领土的政治机器，以法律和强制能力为后盾，但官僚化是国家最中心的制度核心。这也是为什么文章在描述韩日国家形成时，

一再突出“centralized bureaucratic control”“administrative capacity”以及各种官僚层级结构的建立。

在“测量”上，文章依赖的是按时间顺序展开的定性历史征象，而不是量化指数。作者追踪了一系列中国式制度与实践的逐步引入，并把它们解释为国家建设的证据：行政法典、中国式官职与官号、领土分区与行政层级、全国税收和人口普查、征兵制军队、培养官僚的儒学机构、如大化改新与大宝律令之类的法典，以及在某些情况下的科举或建立科举的尝试。作者还把庆州、藤原京等都城对长安的模仿，视为 broader state-building mimicry 的一部分。与此同时，文章又反复强调，这些国家建设要素嵌入在更广泛的Sinicization 进程之中：国家形成与文字、佛教、儒学等中国文明要素的引入“不可分离”。但为了准确和 fair，也要补一句：这并不等于作者把一切文化借入都直接当作“国家能力”的测量指标；更准确地说，作者认为韩国与日本的政治制度无法脱离那个让这些制度显得正当、有声望、并且可想象的更大文明环境来理解。

在测量方面，作者采用历史描述性方法而非量化指标。他们追溯特定制度标志的有无及其采纳时间：行政层级（省、郡、村）、全国性人口普查与税收体系、中国式法律和行政法典（如日本645年的大化改新 /ˈtaɪkə/、702年的大宝律令 /ˈtaɪhoʊ/；韩国373年和520年的中国式法典）、科举考试制度（韩国788年，日本仿照唐制 /tɑːŋ/）、国立儒学院（韩国682年，日本约671年）以及国民征兵制军队（韩国570年，日本685年）(pp. 15, 20–21)。作者还以仿照中国长安 /tʃɑːŋˈɑːn/ 修建的都城，韩国庆州 /ˈkjʌŋdʒuː/、日本奈良 /ˈnɑːrə/ 和京都 /ˈkjoʊtoʊ/，作为制度模仿的证据 (p. 22)。

一个显著特点是，作者不将国家形成视为孤立的政治现象，而是将其视为更广泛的中国化（华化）进程不可分割的一部分，包括对中国文明的全面采纳，涵盖语言、文字、宗教（佛教）、哲学（儒学）、社会结构、艺术、建筑乃至服饰。这一更广泛的文明脉络本身被视为国家形成的构成性要素，而非仅仅是其背景 (pp. 13–14)。

Core argument

The core argument is that state formation in Korea and Japan between the fifth and ninth centuries CE was driven primarily by emulation and learning from Chinese civilization, not by bellicist interstate competition. This directly challenges the dominant “bellicist paradigm” in the social sciences, epitomized by Charles Tilly ’s dictum that “war made the state, and the state made war” (p. 2). The authors contend that the bellicist thesis is not a universal deductive theory but rather an inductively derived, Europe-specific narrative that has been erroneously generalized (pp. 2–3).

The argument rests on three pillars. First, the authors demonstrate the near-total absence of interstate war in the East Asian region during the period of state formation. Between the fifth and eighth centuries, there was only one interstate war involving Korea, Japan, or China: the Korean War of Unification of 660–668 (p. 9). For centuries before and after, these polities did not compete for territory, prestige, or authority. Most critically, during the third to fifth centuries, when initial state formation began in both Korea and Japan, China itself was politically divided and posed no military threat whatsoever (p. 9). This directly undermines the causal chain posited by the bellicist thesis.

Second, the authors present extensive empirical evidence of comprehensive emulation and learning from China. The overall scale of borrowing in both Korea and Japan was essentially total, encompassing political institutions as well as religion, philosophy, language, writing, art, architecture, education, clothing, and social structure (pp. 13–18). The authors argue that such wholesale adoption implies emulation (aspirational imitation driven by the logic of appropriateness) rather than selective learning (rational cost–benefit assessment of individual elements) (pp. 7, 16).

Third, the authors identify a domestic political mechanism: the competition between the court and the nobility. Korean and Japanese courts strategically imported Chinese institutions, particularly Buddhism, Confucianism, and meritocratic civil service systems, to consolidate central authority at the expense of entrenched aristocratic classes. Chinese investiture and tribute relationships provided domestic legitimacy, and the institutional apparatus of the Chinese-style state enabled courts to channel noble ambitions through state institutions rather than against them (pp. 24–26).

The paper positions itself as offering a positive theoretical alternative to the bellicist thesis, rather than only a critique of it: that hegemonic systems characterized by civilizational influence and a logic of appropriateness can drive state formation through emulation and learning in the absence of war (pp. 2–3, 27).

核心论点是：公元五至九世纪韩国和日本的国家形成主要是由对中国文明的模仿和学习驱动的，而非由战争性国家间竞争驱动。这直接挑战了社会科学中占主导地位的“战争主义范式”，其典型表述是Charles Tilly 的名言：“战争造就了国家，国家制造了战争” (p. 2)。作者认为，战争主义论题并非普适性的演绎理论，而是一个归纳推导的、欧洲特定的叙事，被错误地推广为普遍规律 (pp. 2–3)。

论证建立在三大支柱之上。第一，作者证明了在国家形成时期，东亚地区几乎完全不存在国家间战争。五至八世纪之间，涉及韩国、日本或中国的国家间战争仅有一场：660–668年的朝鲜统一战争 (p. 9)。在此前后的数个世纪中，这些政治体并未相互争夺领土、声望或权威。最为关键的是，在三至五世纪韩国和日本初始国家形成阶段，中国本身处于政治分裂状态，对外毫无军事威胁 (p. 9)。这直接瓦解了战争主义论题所假定的因果链条。

第二，作者提供了大量实证证据，证明了对中国全面的模仿与学习。韩国和日本的借鉴规模本质上是全方位的，不仅涵盖政治制度，还包括宗教、哲学、语言、文字、艺术、建筑、教育、服饰和社会结构 (pp. 13–18)。作者认为，如此大规模的全盘采纳暗示的是模仿（由适当性逻辑驱动的向往性模仿），而非选择性学习（对单个要素的理性成本-收益评估）(pp. 7, 16)。

第三，作者识别出一个国内政治机制：宫廷与贵族之间的竞争。韩国和日本的宫廷战略性地引入中国制度，特别是佛教、儒学和以才能为标准的科举体系，以巩固中央权威、削弱根深蒂固的贵族阶层。中国的册封和朝贡关系提供了国内合法性，中国式国家的制度机器使宫廷得以将贵族的野心引导至国家制度之内而非对抗国家 (pp. 24–26)。

论文将自身定位为不仅是对战争主义论题的否定，更是提供了一个积极的理论替代方案：以文明影响力和适当性逻辑为特征的霸权体系，能够在没有战争的情况下通过模仿和学习驱动国家形成 (pp. 2–3, 27)。

Mechanisms

The paper proposes a layered causal story rather than a single mechanism. At the broadest level, the authors argue that East Asia was a hegemonic regional order centered on China, not a European-style multipolar system of recurrent existential threats. In this environment, diffusion moved from core to periphery under conditions in which war was relatively rare and China’s civilizational prestige remained powerful even when China itself was politically divided or militarily weak. This systemic background matters because it makes non-coercive borrowing intelligible: Korean and Japanese elites looked to China not mainly out of fear, but because Chinese civilization had become the authoritative regional model of political and cultural order.

Within that broader order, the key diffusion mechanisms are emulation and learning, with the authors insisting that these are analytically distinct but empirically intertwined. Emulation refers to aspirational copying driven by a logic of appropriateness: actors adopt institutions, norms, and practices because they are seen as authoritative, legitimate, prestigious, or simply the proper way to organize rule. Learning refers more to a logic of consequences, in which actors assess what works better and borrow accordingly. The authors explicitly say that emulation, learning, and competition can coexist, and that it is probably impossible to definitively distinguish emulation from learning; nevertheless, they argue that the overall pattern in Korea and Japan points more strongly toward emulation. Their evidence rests on three probative criteria: the borrowing was highly comprehensive rather than selective; the justifications found in historical texts appealed more often to legitimacy and appropriateness than to explicit cost–benefit reasoning; and many borrowed practices involved direct mimicry rather than substantial localization. Examples include Chinese writing systems, Confucian political discourse, legal-administrative codes, taxation, capitals modeled on Chang’an, and repeated textual or institutional reproduction of Chinese forms.

At the domestic level, the paper adds a more proximate political mechanism: competition between the court and the nobility. Korean and Japanese courts imported Sinic norms and institutions partly because these enhanced royal prestige and domestic legitimacy, and partly because they helped the court centralize power and channel noble ambition into the institutions of the state. The authors describe this as a process in which nobles increasingly competed within the state, as officials, exam candidates, or scholar-elites, rather than against it. This domestic mechanism is important, but it should be stated somewhat cautiously: the paper clearly advances it, and ties it to legitimacy, prestige, Buddhism, Confucianism, and bureaucratic centralization, but it presents it more as a historically grounded extension of the argument than as a fully demonstrated micro-level mechanism in the stricter sense. Finally, the authors also point to a regionwide epistemic community, especially Buddhist monks and Confucian scholars, along with tribute missions and Japanese embassies to China, as one important transmission channel through which ideas, texts, techniques, and institutional models circulated.

这篇文章提出的不是单一机制，而是一套分层展开的因果叙事。在最宏观的层面，作者认为东亚是一个以中国为中心的霸权性区域秩序，而不是像欧洲那样由持续性生存威胁塑造的多极体系。在这种环境中，扩散是从 core 向 periphery 发生的；战争相对稀少，而中国的文明声望即便在中国本身政治分裂或军事虚弱时也依旧强大。这个体系背景之所以重要，是因为它让非强制性的制度借入变得可理解：韩日精英之所以面向中国，不主要是出于恐惧，而是因为中国文明已经成为该区域政治与文化秩序的权威范本。

在这个更大的区域秩序之内，关键扩散机制是 emulation 与 learning。作者坚持认为二者在分析上可以区分，但在经验上高度交织。Emulation 指的是由适当性逻辑驱动的向往性模仿：行为者之所以采纳制度、规范与实践，是因为它们被视为有权威、正当、有声望，或者干脆就是“应该如此”的统治方式。Learning 则更接近后果逻辑：行为者评估什么更有效，因此加以借鉴。作者明确说 emulation、learning 和 competition 可以共存，而且几乎不可能 definitively 区分前两者；但他们仍然主张，就整体模式而言，韩国和日本更符合 emulation。其证据依赖三条“试探性”标准：借入规模高度全面而非选择性；历史文本中的理由更多诉诸 legitimacy / appropriateness，而不是明确的成本收益推理；以及许多借入体现为直接 mimicry，而不是大幅度本地化。相关例子包括中国文字系统、儒家政治话语、法律与行政法典、税制、模仿长安的都城，以及大量文本和制度形式对中国样式的再生产。

在国内层面，文章又加上了一个更近端的政治机制：宫廷与贵族之间的竞争。韩国与日本的王室/宫廷之所以引入中国式规范与制度，一部分是因为这些东西能提升王权的 prestige 和 domestic legitimacy，另一部分则是因为它们有助于中央集权，把贵族的野心和竞争导入国家制度内部。作者把这一过程描述为：贵族越来越不是在国家之外与国家对抗，而是在国家之内作为官员、考试参与者或 scholar-elites 去竞争。这个国内机制很重要，但表述上宜略微保守：文章确实清楚提出了它，并把它与 legitimacy、prestige、佛教、儒学和官僚集中化联系起来；但它更像是作者用历史材料支持的一条延伸性解释，而不是在严格意义上被完全证明的微观机制。最后，作者还强调一个区域性的知识共同体，特别是佛教僧侣、儒者，以及朝贡使团和日本遣使，作为制度、文本、技术和思想传播的重要渠道。

Data & methods

Data

The paper relies entirely on qualitative historical evidence drawn from secondary historiographic sources. There is no original primary-source archival research, no quantitative dataset, and no formal statistical analysis. The data can be categorized as follows:

Geographic coverage and time period: The study covers the Korean peninsula (focusing on Silla /ˈsɪlə/, Paekche /ˈpɛktʃeɪ/, and Koguryŏ /koʊˈɡʊrjoʊ/) and Japan (focusing on the Yamato /ˈjɑːmɑːtoʊ/ state and its successors through the Nara /ˈnɑːrə/ and early Heian /ˈheɪɑːn/ periods), roughly from 400 to 800 CE, with contextual references extending earlier and later (pp. 1, 12–15).

Unit of analysis: The primary unit of analysis is the polity (kingdom or state), though the argument also addresses sub-state elite actors (the court and the nobility) and the regional system as a whole.

Measuring the DV (state formation): The authors trace institutional development through secondary historical accounts, documenting the timing of adoption of specific state institutions as listed in their summary timeline (Table 1, p. 15). Sources include standard East Asian history textbooks and monographs: Seth /sɛθ/ (2016) on Korean history, Holcombe /ˈhoʊlkoʊm/ (2011) on East Asian history, Ebrey /ˈɛbri/ and Walthall /ˈwɔːlθɔːl/ (2014) on pre-modern East Asia, de Bary /dəˈbɛri/ et al. (2001) on Japanese historical sources, Lee /liː/ and de Bary (1997) on Korean historical sources, Batten /ˈbætən/ (1986, 2003) on Japanese frontiers, and Sugimoto /suːɡɪˈmoʊtoʊ/ and Swain /sweɪn/ (1989) on Japanese science and culture.

Measuring the IV (emulation and learning vs. bellicist competition): The authors assess the mechanism of diffusion using three criteria: (1) overall scale of borrowing (total vs. selective); (2) justification given at the time (logic of appropriateness vs. consequences); and (3) whether there was mimicry or modification for each individual institution (pp. 7–8). Evidence for the absence of bellicist pressures is drawn from the historical record of interstate wars (or lack thereof), relying on secondary accounts of diplomatic and military history across the region.

Measuring the absence of war: The authors document that between the fifth and eighth centuries there was only one interstate war involving any two of Korea, Japan, and China, the Korean War of Unification of 660–668 (p. 9), and they present detailed evidence that even this war does not fit the bellicist causal logic (pp. 9–11).

Primary historical sources cited indirectly: The paper quotes from or references the Samguk sagi /sɑːmɡʊk sɑːɡi/ (1145), Samguk yusa /sɑːmɡʊk juːsɑː/ (13th century), Kojiki /koʊˈdʒiːki/ (712), Nihon Shoki /niˈhɒn ˈʃoʊki/ (720), Prince Shotoku /ʃoʊˈtoʊkuː/’s Seventeen Injunctions (604), and the Taiho /ˈtaɪhoʊ/ and Yoro /ˈjoʊroʊ/ Codes, but always through secondary translations and interpretations (pp. 10, 17–23).

论文完全依赖从二手历史文献中提取的定性历史证据。没有原始一手档案研究，没有量化数据集，也没有正式的统计分析。数据可按以下类别归纳：

地理覆盖范围与时间段： 研究覆盖朝鲜半岛（聚焦新罗 /ˈsɪlə/、百济 /ˈpɛktʃeɪ/ 和高句丽 /koʊˈɡʊrjoʊ/）和日本（聚焦大和 /ˈjɑːmɑːtoʊ/ 国及其后继政权，直至奈良 /ˈnɑːrə/ 和平安 /ˈheɪɑːn/ 时代初期），大约从公元400年至800年，上下文参考在时间上有所延伸 (pp. 1, 12–15)。

分析单位： 主要分析单位是政治体（王国或国家），但论证也涉及次国家层级的精英行为者（宫廷和贵族）以及整个区域体系。

因变量（国家形成）的测量： 作者通过二手历史叙述追踪制度发展，记录特定国家制度被采纳的时间，如其总结时间线所示（表1，p. 15）。资料来源包括标准的东亚史教科书和专著：Seth /sɛθ/ (2016) 关于韩国史，Holcombe /ˈhoʊlkoʊm/ (2011) 关于东亚史，Ebrey /ˈɛbri/ 和Walthall /ˈwɔːlθɔːl/ (2014) 关于前现代东亚，de Bary /dəˈbɛri/ 等 (2001) 关于日本历史文献，Lee /liː/ 和de Bary (1997) 关于韩国历史文献，Batten /ˈbætən/ (1986, 2003) 关于日本边疆，以及Sugimoto /suːɡɪˈmoʊtoʊ/ 和Swain /sweɪn/ (1989) 关于日本科学与文化。

自变量（模仿和学习 vs. 战争竞争）的测量： 作者使用三个标准评估扩散机制：(1) 借鉴的总体规模（全面 vs. 选择性）；(2) 当时给出的理由（适当性逻辑 vs. 后果逻辑）；(3) 对每个具体制度而言，是模仿还是修改适应 (pp. 7–8)。关于战争压力不存在的证据来自国家间战争（或其缺失）的历史记录，依据的是该区域外交和军事史的二手叙述。

战争缺失的测量： 作者记录了五至八世纪之间涉及韩国、日本或中国之中任何两者的国家间战争仅有一场，660–668年的朝鲜统一战争 (p. 9)，并提供了详细证据表明即使这场战争也不符合战争主义的因果逻辑 (pp. 9–11)。

间接引用的一手历史文献： 论文引用或提及了《三国史记》/sɑːmɡʊk sɑːɡi/ (1145)、《三国遗事》/sɑːmɡʊk juːsɑː/ (13世纪)、《古事记》/koʊˈdʒiːki/ (712)、《日本书纪》/niˈhɒn ˈʃoʊki/ (720)、圣德太子 /ʃoʊˈtoʊkuː/ 十七条宪法 (604) 以及大宝律令 /ˈtaɪhoʊ/ 和养老律令 /ˈjoʊroʊ/，但均通过二手翻译和解读引用 (pp. 10, 17–23)。

Methods

The paper employs qualitative comparative-historical analysis based on secondary historical scholarship, interpretive reading of texts, and historically ordered comparison. It does not use formal modeling, statistical analysis, original archival coding, or a quantitative cross-polity design. The authors assemble evidence from historiography on Korea, Japan, and East Asia more broadly, and use that material to build a structured argument against the universality of the bellicist thesis while advancing diffusion-based alternatives. The paper compares East Asia with Europe at the level of system type and historical pathway, and within East Asia it juxtaposes Korea and Japan as parallel cases of state formation under Chinese hegemonic influence. It also relies heavily on a chronological reconstruction of institutional adoption, summarized especially in Table 1, to show that state-building unfolded gradually, unevenly, and largely outside a context of interstate war.

It is reasonable to say that the article contains process-tracing-like elements, because it reconstructs sequences of borrowing, institutional change, and justification over time, and uses those sequences to argue that emulation and learning fit the historical record better than bellicist competition. However, for the sake of accuracy and fairness, it is better not to label the paper simply as rigorous process tracing. The paper does not systematically stage explicit hoop tests, smoking-gun tests, or finely discriminating within-case tests between rival mechanisms in the stricter methodological sense. Rather, it is better described as an interpretive comparative-historical argument that combines chronological narrative, conceptual distinction, and anti-bellicist negative-case reasoning. A central part of the method is precisely to show what did not happen: the paper argues that the core bellicist expectations fail in East Asia because interstate war was nearly absent, the only major war did not line up temporally or causally with state-building, and the most state-like or most Sinicized polity did not straightforwardly behave as bellicist logic would predict.

Assumptions for validity: Several assumptions must hold for this method to be persuasive. First, the historiographic consensus on which the authors rely must be accurate, since they conduct no original primary-source research, they are dependent on the interpretations of area-studies historians. Second, the three-part framework for distinguishing emulation from learning (scale, justification, mimicry vs. modification) must be accepted as a valid operationalization of analytically distinct concepts, an issue the authors themselves acknowledge is difficult (pp. 6–7). Third, the absence of evidence for bellicist pressures must be accepted as evidence of absence, a logical step that requires confidence in the completeness of the historical record. Fourth, the comparison with Europe must be accepted as analytically productive despite the enormous differences in geography, chronology, technology, and social structure.

Negative case analysis: A significant portion of the argument is devoted to demonstrating what did NOT cause state formation, namely, interstate war. The authors systematically examine three bellicist hypotheses and show that none finds support in the historical record: (1) that the most state-like polity should win wars (Silla, the least Sinicized, won); (2) that war should produce state formation (centuries of state-building occurred without war); and (3) that more state formation should lead to more war (the Tang refrained from annexation, Silla did not expand beyond the peninsula, and Japan’s borders remained fixed) (pp. 10–11).

这篇文章使用的是定性的比较历史分析，其基础是二手历史研究、对文本的解释性阅读，以及按时间顺序展开的历史比较。它没有使用形式模型、统计分析、原创档案编码，也没有构造量化的跨政治体比较设计。作者从韩国史、日本史以及更广义东亚史的史学研究中汇集材料，用这些材料一方面反驳 bellicist thesis 的普遍性，另一方面提出基于扩散的替代解释。文章在体系类型和历史路径层面把东亚与欧洲进行对照；在东亚内部，又把韩国和日本并置为在中国霸权性影响下形成国家的两个平行案例。它还非常依赖制度采纳的时间顺序重建，尤其体现在表1，以说明国家建设是渐进的、不均衡的，而且大体上发生在国家间战争缺失的环境中。

如果要说得细一点，可以说这篇文章带有一些 process-tracing-like 的成分，因为它确实重建了制度借入、制度变化和正当化话语的时间序列，并据此主张 emulation 和 learning 比 bellicist competition 更符合历史记录。但为了准确和 fair，最好不要直接把这篇文章称为严格意义上的 process tracing。因为它并没有像严格方法论意义上的过程追踪那样，系统设置 hoop tests、smoking-gun tests，或对竞争性机制进行细密的 case-internal discrimination。更准确的说法是：它是一篇解释性的比较历史论证，把时间序列叙事、概念区分和反 bellicist 的“否定性论证”结合在一起。其方法中的一个核心部分，恰恰是展示什么没有发生：文章试图证明，bellicist 的几个关键预期在东亚都不成立，国家间战争几乎缺席，唯一那场重要战争在时间和因果上都对不上国家建设，而且最“国家化”或最中国化的 polity 也没有按 bellicist 逻辑那样直接表现。

方法有效性的前提假设： 若干假设必须成立，该方法才具有说服力。第一，作者所依赖的历史学界共识必须准确，由于他们未进行原始一手资料研究，完全依赖于区域研究历史学家的解读。第二，区分模仿与学习的三部分框架（规模、理由、模仿 vs. 修改适应）必须被接受为对分析上可区分的概念的有效操作化，这是作者自己也承认困难的问题 (pp. 6–7)。第三，战争压力证据的缺失必须被接受为缺失的证据，这一逻辑步骤要求对历史记录的完整性有信心。第四，与欧洲的比较必须被接受为具有分析生产力的，尽管在地理、时间、技术和社会结构方面存在巨大差异。

否定性案例分析：论证的重要部分致力于证明什么没有导致国家形成，即国家间战争。作者系统检验了三个战争主义假说，并表明历史记录均不支持它们：(1) 最具国家形态的政治体应赢得战争（实际上中国化程度最低的新罗获胜）；(2) 战争应产生国家形成（数世纪的国家建设在没有战争的情况下进行）；(3) 更多国家形成应导致更多战争（唐朝放弃吞并，新罗未向半岛以外扩张，日本边界保持不变）(pp. 10–11)。

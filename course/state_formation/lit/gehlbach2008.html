Scott Gehlbach (2008), Representation through Taxation, chs. 1, 3.盖尔巴赫（2008）《通过税收的代表》第1、3章。

Formal modelPanel FECounter-thesis

Dependent variable (DV)

The main dependent variable is the provision of sector-specific collective goods (such as justice, police protection, regulatory frameworks, and other state services) to different economic sectors in postcommunist states. A secondary dependent variable is economic development, specifically the growth (or stunting) of new private enterprise which is exemplified by small firms in competitive industries in postcommunist economies. In Chapter 1, Gehlbach frames the overarching puzzle as: who did postcommunist politicians favor in the provision of collective goods, and what were the consequences for economic development? (pp. 17–18). In Chapter 3, the formal model’s dependent variable is explicitly the equilibrium allocation of collective goods across sectors (p. 64).

主要因变量是向后共产主义国家中不同经济部门提供的部门特定的集体物品（如司法、警察保护、监管框架及其他国家服务）。次要因变量是经济发展，具体而言是新兴私营企业的增长（或萎缩），其典型代表是后共产主义经济中竞争性行业里的小企业。在第一章中，Gehlbach 将总体难题表述为：在提供集体物品时，后共产主义政治家偏袒了谁，其对经济发展有何后果？（pp. 17–18）。在第三章中，形式模型的因变量明确是集体物品在各部门间的均衡配置（p. 64）。

Independent variable (IV)

The main independent variable is the “taxability” of economic sectors, defined as the degree to which firms in a sector find it costly to hide revenues from tax authorities (p. 65). This concept captures the structural ease with which the state can extract revenue from different types of economic activity. Taxability is shaped by the design of postcommunist tax systems, which in turn was determined by initial conditions at the start of the transition (inherited industrial structure, proximity to Western Europe, and levels of economic development) (pp. 17–18). A secondary independent variable is the organization of economic sectors (i.e., their capacity for collective action in the Olsonian sense), which interacts with taxability to determine collective-goods provision. Gehlbach argues that taxability, as well as organization, determines political representation.

主要自变量是各经济部门的“可征税性”（taxability），其定义为某一部门中的企业向税务机关隐瞒收入的代价有多高（p. 65）。这一概念捕捉的是国家从不同类型经济活动中汲取收入的结构性难易程度。可征税性由后共产主义税制的设计所塑造，而后者又由转型之初的初始条件所决定（继承下来的产业结构、与西欧的邻近程度，以及经济发展水平）（pp. 17–18）。次要自变量是经济部门的组织化程度（即其在 Olson 意义上的集体行动能力），它与可征税性相互作用，共同决定集体物品的提供。Gehlbach 论证，决定政治代表的是可征税性，而不仅仅是组织化程度。

Stateness

Gehlbach conceptualizes state primarily through its fiscal capacity, the ability to design tax systems, enforce tax compliance, and extract revenue from the economy. State building in this account is about the creation of tax systems following the collapse of communism. Under communism, taxation was merely an accounting exercise because the state owned productive assets; the transition to private ownership required building bureaucracies capable of locating and taxing private actors (p. 20). The key dimension of state capacity here is not coercive or administrative capacity in general, but specifically the state’s differential capacity to tax different sectors. The state is “strong” facing monopolistic enterprises inherited from the Soviet era (because it knows how to monitor and tax them) but “weak”facing small, private firms (because its tax apparatus was never designed to reach them). This uneven fiscal capacity is the central feature of the postcommunist state in Gehlbach’s account.

Gehlbach 主要通过国家的财政能力来构想国家，即设计税制、强制税收遵从以及从经济中汲取收入的能力。在这一论述中，国家建构关乎共产主义崩溃之后税制的创建。在共产主义制度下，征税不过是一项会计工作，因为国家拥有生产性资产；向私有制的转型则要求建立能够定位并向私人行为者征税的官僚机构（p. 20）。这里国家能力的关键维度不是一般意义上的强制或行政能力，而是国家向不同部门征税的差别化能力。面对从苏联时代继承下来的垄断性企业，国家是“强”的（因为它知道如何监督并向其征税），而面对小型私营企业则是“弱”的（因为其税收机构从未被设计成能够触及它们）。在 Gehlbach 的论述中，这种不均衡的财政能力是后共产主义国家的核心特征。

Core argument

The core argument is that in the postcommunist world, political representation, namely which sectors of the economy receive favorable treatment from the state, is determined by both the organization of interests (according to Olson) and, sometimes more importantly, the expected tax compliance of those sectors. Gehlbach calls it “representation through taxation”.

First, initial conditions at the start of the postcommunist transition led to divergent tax systems: the former Soviet Union (FSU) continued relying on enterprise taxes and taxes on goods and services (familiar communist-era revenue sources), while Eastern Europe transitioned to personal income taxation to align with EU norms (pp. 5–6).

Second, these tax system designs produced very different patterns of tax compliance. In the FSU, tax authorities concentrated on large, monopolistic enterprises, leaving small firms and competitive industries relatively free to evade taxes. In Eastern Europe, the revenue net was cast more widely, producing smaller differences in compliance across sectors (p. 6).

Third, these patterns of differential taxability shaped politicians’ incentives. In the FSU, where differences in taxability were large and tax revenue was politically urgent (to pay state wages and maintain power), politicians disproportionately provided collective goods to sectors that were easy to tax, even if those sectors were not politically organized. This came at the expense of sectors that were harder to tax, notably the small-business sector, which matters for long-term development (pp. 3, 11–14).

Gehlbach argues that governments do more than structure tax systems around existing economic activity. They also structure the tax base itself, promoting activity they can tax at the expense of activity they cannot tax (p. 6). Against Olson’s Logic of Collective Action, he argues that organized interests do not always win. Unorganized but highly taxable sectors may be favored when tax revenue is politically important (pp. 10–11). Against Robert Bates’s /ˈrɒbərt ˈbeɪtsɪz/ Markets and States in Tropical Africa, he argues that the organization of interests is neither necessary nor sufficient to explain development outcomes; the state’s capacity to extract revenue from different sectors is equally or more important (p. 16).

核心论点是：在后共产主义世界，政治代表（即经济中哪些部门获得国家的优待）不仅由利益的组织化（依 Olson 之见）所决定，而且有时主要由这些部门预期的税收遵从所决定。Gehlbach 称之为“通过征税实现代表”（representation through taxation）。

第一，后共产主义转型之初的初始条件导致了分化的税制：前苏联（FSU）继续依赖企业税以及对商品和服务的征税（共产主义时代熟悉的收入来源），而东欧则转向个人所得税，以与欧盟规范接轨（pp. 5–6）。

第二，这些税制设计产生了极为不同的税收遵从模式。在前苏联，税务机关集中关注大型垄断企业，而让小企业和竞争性行业相对自由地逃税。在东欧，收入之网撒得更广，从而使各部门间的遵从差异较小（p. 6）。

第三，这些差别化可征税性的模式塑造了政治家的激励。在前苏联，可征税性差异巨大且税收在政治上迫在眉睫（用以支付国家工资并维持权力），政治家便不成比例地向易于征税的部门提供集体物品，即便这些部门在政治上并未组织起来。这是以牺牲较难征税的部门为代价的，尤其是对长期发展至关重要的小企业部门（pp. 3, 11–14）。

Gehlbach 论证，政府不仅仅围绕既有经济活动来构建税制；它们还构建税基本身，扶持它们能够征税的经济活动，而牺牲它们无法征税的经济活动（p. 6）。针对 Olson 的《集体行动的逻辑》（Logic of Collective Action），他论证有组织的利益并不总是获胜。当税收在政治上重要时，无组织但高度可征税的部门可能受到偏袒（pp. 10–11）。针对 Robert Bates 的《热带非洲的市场与国家》（Markets and States in Tropical Africa），他论证利益的组织化对于解释发展结果既非必要也非充分；国家从不同部门汲取收入的能力同等重要甚或更为重要（p. 16）。

Mechanisms

Same as the left, plus the following:

The central mechanism is that tax compliance is “noncontractible.” Organized sectors cannot credibly promise that their members will pay more taxes in exchange for collective goods, because individual firms have incentives to free-ride by hiding what they can get away with (pp. 61, 64–65). This means that the anticipated (not promised) tax compliance of sectors, determined by their structural “taxability,” shapes the division of gains between the state and private actors from any policy the politician might adopt (p. 62).

The politician’s objective function is maximizing a weighted average of tax revenue (from all sectors) and contributions (from organized sectors only). When tax revenue is politically important (high γ in the formal model), the politician favors sectors that provide more tax revenue, which are those whose firms find it more costly to hide, i.e. more taxable sectors. When tax revenue is less important, the standard Olsonian logic prevails and organized sectors are favored regardless of taxability.

Once a sector becomes a major revenue source through deliberate government promotion, politicians become dependent on it for revenue, creating a “revenue trap.” Even when a new governor (Kuznetsov /kuzˈnɛtsɒf/) tried to dismantle preferential treatment for vodka, he was forced to retreat because the regional budget depended on vodka tax revenue (pp. 15–16). This creates path dependence: the initial choice of tax system design locks in patterns of economic development, as labor and capital remain in sectors that receive collective goods, and politicians remain dependent on those sectors for revenue (p. 17).

In the FSU, the disproportionate provision of collective goods to “old” (easily taxable) economic activity crowded out “new” (hard to tax) private enterprise. Small businesses, which could easily evade taxes, received less government support, worse regulatory treatment, and less police and judicial protection, not because they lacked lobbying power (though they did), but because they contributed little to state revenue (pp. 11–14).

同左，另加以下内容：

核心机制在于税收遵从是“不可缔约的”（noncontractible）。有组织的部门无法可信地承诺其成员将缴纳更多税款以换取集体物品，因为个别企业有搭便车的激励，会隐瞒它们侥幸能瞒过的部分（pp. 61, 64–65）。这意味着各部门预期的（而非承诺的）税收遵从，由其结构性的“可征税性”所决定，塑造了在政治家可能采取的任何政策中国家与私人行为者之间收益的分配（p. 62）。

政治家的目标函数是最大化税收收入（来自所有部门）与政治献金（仅来自有组织部门）的加权平均。当税收在政治上重要时（形式模型中的 γ 较高），政治家偏袒能提供更多税收收入的部门，即那些企业隐瞒代价更高的部门，也就是更可征税的部门。当税收不那么重要时，标准的 Olson 式逻辑占上风，无论可征税性如何，有组织的部门都受到偏袒。

一旦某一部门通过政府的刻意扶持成为主要收入来源，政治家便在收入上依赖它，从而形成一个“收入陷阱”（revenue trap）。即便一位新州长（Kuznetsov）试图拆除对伏特加的优惠待遇，他也因地区预算依赖伏特加税收而被迫退让（pp. 15–16）。这产生了路径依赖：税制设计的初始选择锁定了经济发展的模式，因为劳动力和资本停留在获得集体物品的部门中，而政治家在收入上仍依赖这些部门（p. 17）。

在前苏联，向“旧的”（易于征税的）经济活动不成比例地提供集体物品，挤出了“新的”（难以征税的）私营企业。能够轻易逃税的小企业，获得的政府支持更少、监管待遇更差、警察和司法保护更少，这并非因为它们缺乏游说能力（尽管它们确实缺乏），而是因为它们对国家收入贡献甚微（pp. 11–14）。

Data & methods

Data

Gehlbach uses multiple types of data across the book. Chapter 1 relies primarily on evidence from Pskov oblast /pskɒf ˈɒblæst/, a region in northwestern Russia:

1. Interviews with regional officials (e.g., Vadim Petrukhin /vɑːˈdiːm pɪˈtruːxɪn/, head of the oblast Committee for Economic Development, interviewed July 13, 2005) and civil society actors in Pskov (p. 2, fn. 4).

2. Local and national newspaper reports from Pskovskaia Pravda, Rossiiskaia Gazeta, Pskovskaia Guberniia, Pskovskaia Lenta Novostei, and others (throughout Ch. 1).

3. Online forum posts by former governor Mikhailov under the pseudonym “Specialist,” confirmed by individuals in Pskov (p. 9, fn. 22).

4. Audit Chamber reports on subsidies to vodka production (p. 8, fn. 20).

Gehlbach 在全书中使用多种类型的数据。第一章主要依赖来自 Pskov 州（Pskov oblast，俄罗斯西北部的一个地区）的证据：

1. 对地区官员的访谈（例如州经济发展委员会主任 Vadim Petrukhin，于 2005 年 7 月 13 日受访）以及 Pskov 的公民社会行为者（p. 2, 脚注 4）。

2. 来自 Pskovskaia Pravda、Rossiiskaia Gazeta、Pskovskaia Guberniia、Pskovskaia Lenta Novostei 等的地方和全国性报纸报道（贯穿第一章）。

3. 前州长 Mikhailov 以化名“Specialist”发布的在线论坛帖子，经 Pskov 的相关人士确认（p. 9, 脚注 22）。

4. 审计署关于伏特加生产补贴的报告（p. 8, 脚注 20）。

Methods

Qualitative case study (Chapter 1). A detailed process-tracing study of vodka politics in Pskov oblast, Russia, drawing on interviews, newspaper archives, government reports, and online forum posts. This is a plausibility probe and illustration of the causal mechanisms (entire Ch. 1).

The formal model’s predictions depend on the truthfulness of contribution schedules and the noncontractibility of tax compliance. The case study’s generalizability depends on Pskov being representative of broader dynamics in the FSU, which Gehlbach argues it is.

定性案例研究（第一章）。对俄罗斯 Pskov 州伏特加政治的详细过程追踪研究，借助访谈、报纸档案、政府报告和在线论坛帖子。这既是一次可信性探查，也是对因果机制的例证（整个第一章）。

形式模型的预测取决于政治献金安排的真实性和税收遵从的不可缔约性。案例研究的可推广性取决于 Pskov 是否能代表前苏联更广泛的动态，Gehlbach 论证它确实如此。

Class Notes

Week 9 lecture: discussion of this reading

Gehlbach flips the fiscal-contract logic. The government supports sectors not because taxpayers voluntarily comply, but because some sectors are easier to tax. A Russian governor's vodka monopoly is the emblematic example: vodka is legible and taxable.

In the class discussion, Gehlbach was placed against Levi. Levi's quasi-voluntary compliance depends on fairness, coordination, and commitment; Gehlbach instead shows a coercive path in which the government feeds taxable sectors so that the tax base survives.

Coercive complianceGovernment threatens; citizens comply when threat is credible.Closest to Gehlbach.
Quasi-voluntary complianceFairness + coordination + commitment.Levi's innovation.

The instructor then represented Levi's quasi-voluntary compliance as a two-citizen coordination game. Each citizen chooses comply or evade; the government commitment problem is assumed solved.

Citizen 1 / Citizen 2ComplyEvade
Comply\((V-T+\sigma,\,V-T+\sigma)\)\((-T-S,\,0)\)
Evade\((0,\,-T-S)\)\((0,\,0)\)

Here \(V-T\) is the net benefit of public goods after taxes, \(\sigma\) is the civic-duty glow/fairness payoff, and \(S>0\) is the sucker's cost paid by the lone taxpayer when others evade.

With \(V-T+\sigma>0\) and \(S>0\), both \((\text{Comply},\text{Comply})\) and \((\text{Evade},\text{Evade})\) are Nash equilibria. Quasi-voluntary compliance therefore requires solving two problems at once: the government must provide \(V\), and citizens must believe others will also pay.

The open research space sits between Levi and Gehlbach: fiscal-contract-like outcomes may arise from voluntary coordination, coercive taxability, or mixtures of both.

Gehlbach 翻转了财政契约逻辑。政府扶持某些部门，不是因为纳税人自愿合规，而是因为某些部门更容易被征税。俄罗斯州长建立伏特加垄断就是标志性例子：伏特加可读、可见、可税。

课堂讨论将 Gehlbach 与 Levi 对照。Levi 的准自愿合规依赖公平感、协调和承诺；Gehlbach 则展示一条强制路径：政府“喂养”可税部门，使税基能够存活。

其中 \(V-T\) 是纳税后获得公共品的净收益，\(\sigma\) 是公民义务光环/公平感收益，\(S>0\) 是“冤大头成本”：当别人逃税而你独自纳税时承受的额外成本。

当 \(V-T+\sigma>0\) 且 \(S>0\) 时，\((\text{纳税},\text{纳税})\) 和 \((\text{逃税},\text{逃税})\) 都是纳什均衡。因此，准自愿合规必须同时解决两个问题：政府确实提供 \(V\)，公民也相信别人会一起纳税。

Levi 与 Gehlbach 之间留下了开放研究空间：看似财政契约的结果，可能来自自愿协调，也可能来自强制性可税性，或者来自二者的混合。

Shared week context: course framing & four-reading synthesis

This week compares four classic or synthetic accounts of limited government, fiscal contracts, and tax compliance. The shared premise is that a ruler wants revenue, but sustainable revenue requires solving constraints that pure coercion cannot cheaply solve.

\[ \max R=(\text{tax revenue})-(\text{collection costs}) \quad\text{s.t.}\quad \{\text{bargaining power},\text{ transaction costs},\text{ discount factor}\}. \]

Levi (1988)

Starts with the ruler as revenue maximizer, then adds quasi-voluntary compliance. Compliance is more than coercion; it combines perceived fairness, coordination among taxpayers, and the ruler's credibility.

North & Weingast (1989)

Turns the problem into a commitment game. Institutions impose a penalty \(P\) on exploitation, making provision credible when \(P>G\). Levi's bargaining power can be read as one source of \(P\).

Stasavage (2011)

Endogenizes the penalty parameter: \(P=f(A,M)\), where \(A\) is alignment between creditors and representatives and \(M\) is monitoring capacity. The effect is strongest in small, creditor-controlled polities.

Gehlbach (2008)

Flips the fiscal-contract logic. The government may support taxable sectors without voluntary compliance: it feeds sectors that are easy to tax. Taxability works like a transaction-cost/legibility condition.

The theoretical map is therefore not a simple sequence from coercion to legitimacy. It is a set of alternative ways to make revenue extraction sustainable: quasi-voluntary compliance, institutional penalties, endogenous representative constraints, or coercive support for legible taxable sectors.

\[ \frac{T-G}{1-\beta}>T\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad \beta>\frac{G}{T}. \]

The repeated-game result shows where Levi and Olson connect to North and Weingast: patient rulers may sustain cooperation even without formal institutions, but when time horizons shorten, institutional penalties become more important.

本周四篇文献共同讨论有限政府、财政契约与税收合规。共同前提是：统治者想要收入，但可持续收入不能只靠昂贵的强制，还必须解决若干约束。

\[ \max R=(\text{税收收入})-(\text{征税成本}) \quad\text{s.t.}\quad \{\text{议价能力},\text{ 交易成本},\text{ 贴现因子}\}. \]

Levi (1988)

以“统治者最大化收入”为起点，随后加入准自愿合规。合规不只是强制，而是由公平感、纳税人之间的协调、以及统治者可信承诺共同支撑。

North & Weingast (1989)

将问题转写为承诺博弈。制度对掠夺施加惩罚 \(P\)，当 \(P>G\) 时，提供公共品才可信。Levi 的议价能力可以理解为 \(P\) 的来源之一。

Stasavage (2011)

将惩罚参数内生化：\(P=f(A,M)\)，其中 \(A\) 是债权人与代表者的利益一致性，\(M\) 是监控能力。小规模、债权人控制议会的政体中效果最强。

Gehlbach (2008)

翻转财政契约逻辑。政府可以在没有自愿合规的情况下扶持可税部门：它“喂养”容易征税的部门。可税性相当于一种交易成本/可读性条件。

因此，这张理论图谱不是从强制走向合法性的单线进步史，而是展示几种让汲取可持续的替代路径：准自愿合规、制度性惩罚、内生代表约束，或对可读可税部门的强制性扶持。

\[ \frac{T-G}{1-\beta}>T\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad \beta>\frac{G}{T}. \]

重复博弈结果说明 Levi/Olson 与 North-Weingast 的连接点：有耐心的统治者即使没有正式制度，也可能维持合作；但当时间视野变短，制度性惩罚就变得更关键。
